The film begins in June 2011 when the VM2 virus infects a French woman. Within the next 28 days, 84% of the European population was infected. The virus becomes a global pandemic as it eventually reaches Japan and the United States. Six months later, after the zombie plague has wiped out ninety percent of the US population, a small group of survivors fight their way across the United States to a rumored refuge on the island of Catalina.
