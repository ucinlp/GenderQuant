Maria  is a housekeeper, who is married to Filiberto 'Berto' Morucchi  who is currently without a job so expecting to inherit some money from his uncle Matteo. Maria's best friend is Ermelinda . Meanwhile, Maria does her job. Her first employer is a woman who cheats on her husband. Then, Maria works for a marriage  which is about collapsing. Finally, she is hired by Raffaele , a math teacher who is engaged to marry her. Nonetheless, eventually uncle Matteo passes away.
