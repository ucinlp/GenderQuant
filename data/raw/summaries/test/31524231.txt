A tropical island cursed by voodoo is purchased by a land developer  who plans to open a luxury hotel. He hires a sea Captain  to take him and a socialite  to the nearby island. Arriving at their location, the group is greeted by a shaman and his granddaughter Luna . They are warned to leave as the island is reported home to zombies of the dead natives. But Luna, who just might be a ghost, takes a liking to the greedy land owner.
