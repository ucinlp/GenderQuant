In order to earn a little extra income, a former cop Ray  takes a job as the head of security at The House of the Rising Sun, an underworld strip club that also houses illegal gambling. When a gang of thieves strike fast and hard, the police finger Ray as the mastermind of the violent robbery. Now, in order to regain his honor and avoid ending up back in prison, Ray must catch the real culprits, before the police catch up with him.
