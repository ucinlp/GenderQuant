The Stooges are warehouse workers who are assigned to delivering some Arabian antiques  for a Mr. Bradley . While unpacking the goods at Mr. Bradley's house, Shemp stumbles upon a magic lamp that he, at first, dubs a "syrup pitcher." After giving the lamp a cleaning, a genie appears , startling Shemp. Calling the genie "genius," the Stooges are pursued two Arabian thugs  who are after the magic lamp.
