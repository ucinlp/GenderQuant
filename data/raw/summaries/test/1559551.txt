Boy , a young widower, lives alone on a nuclear testing site in the desert. He is convinced the world is about to end and carves Kachina dolls, believing they contain magical powers. Boy comes to the aid of Hollywood couple Buffy and Harry  when their car breaks down as they are traveling through the desert on a trip they're taking as a "second honeymoon". Boy becomes infatuated with Buffy and refuses to let the couple leave because of his ambition to create a better world with her.{{cite web}}
