Dutch colonial army in West Java holds a martial arts tournament to select the best warrior to confront local warrior-freedom fighter Parmin . The winner is an obscure blind swordsman and the Dutch commander De Mandes  commissions him. However, the swordsman is sympathetic with Jaka Sembung's cause and things get more complicated when magician seductress Dewi Magi as well as her guru  intervene on behalf of the Dutch.
