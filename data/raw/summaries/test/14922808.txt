The film tells of Al Goddard , a detective who works for the United States Postal Inspection Service. He's assigned to arrest two criminals who've allegedly murdered a U.S. postal detective. Goddard must locate the witness to the murder, an attractive young nun named Sister Augustine . He poses as a crook, and gains the confidence of the killers' honcho Boettiger ([[Paul Stewart , who has worked out a plan to steal one million bucks from the post office. Once they discover the deception, the villains take Goddard and witness Sister Augustine prisoner. This leads to a fight in an industrial district.
