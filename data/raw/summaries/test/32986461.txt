Frannie Vaughn  comes back home after being away a long time, only to find that her mother has died. She also finds out that her sister, Natalie  and her husband, Jake , are planning to sell the acres once owned by Frannie and Natalie's family. To buy the land back from her sister, Frannie gets a job and falls in love with a fellow worker, Rubin . In the end, Frannie's dreams come true and Natalie comes to her senses and moves in to the old family house with Frannie and Rubin.
