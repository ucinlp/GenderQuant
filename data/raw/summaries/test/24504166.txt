Alfonso  challenges his friends Ferrando and Guglielmo to the fidelity of their respective boyfriends, Dorino and Fiorino. Ferrando and Guglielmo insist their lovers are true, Alfonso sets out to test both. He takes his first stab at Dorino and summons Despino to help conquer Fiorino. Having lost their bets, Ferrando and Guglielmo surrender to Alfonso, who claims his prize.
