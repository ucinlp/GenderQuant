Los Jinetes del Alba is a film adaptation of a novel by Jesús Fernández Santos, made as a five parts TV miniseries. It follows the life of Marian, a young woman who greatest ambitions is to be the owner of the resort where she works. The action is set in Las Caldas, a small town in Asturias, where the lives of its inhabitants are forever changed with the arrival of the Asturian revolution of 1934 and the Spanish Civil War.
