Harry Graham  is a lonely and beaten down man who has recently been released from prison, serving time for murdering his wife many years ago. His son Jimmy  is after him seeking justice for his mother's murder. Jimmy tracks Harry down to a run-down seashore community where he is now eking out a living in a trailer park with his girlfriend Jenny . When Jimmy at last confronts his father face to face, they both know they have to deal with many unresolved emotional barriers in their relationship.
