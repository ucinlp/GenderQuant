Barnacle Bill  is a sailor on a ship that has just come into port. As soon as he can get off the ship, he heads for Nancy Lee's  house. When he gets there he begins knocking on her door. Bimbo and Betty begin singing the lyrics to a tame version of "Barnacle Bill the Sailor." The actions of the film follow along the song's storyline, with Barnacle Bimbo romancing Betty and then leaving her to go back to sea.
