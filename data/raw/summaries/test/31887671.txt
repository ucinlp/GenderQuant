Mother Fish follows the story of a middle-aged Vietnamese woman  working in a suburban sweatshop. In the evening when the workers have left, she is transported back to the night she and her sister  fled her homeland, led by an uncle promising to reunite them with their father. Through the setting of the sweatshop, the woman remembers the journey. The boat is unprepared for the ocean crossing, as are they. Food and water supplies are low, their engine breaks, and the threat of rape and death at the hands of South-sea pirates is real. Through the woman's memory the audience relives the experience of crossing the ocean in search of a better life.Mother Fish  - IMDb
