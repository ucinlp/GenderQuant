The movie begins in Madurai where a young boy Sakthi  leads a happy life with his parents. A shocking incident changes his life. His parents are murdered by an anti-social and Johnny is forced to go to prison, where he is tortured. He comes across four criminals in the prison - Pandu aka Pandurangan , Dabba aka Prem Kumar , Mari  and Michael . They sympathise with Sakthi, who seeks solace in their company. These hardcore criminals break loose from the prison and help Sakthi take revenge on his parent's killers. They decide to go to Mumbai where they plan to become big gangsters. But fate has other plans. After a successful murder in the town, Sardar takes these boys seriously. Meanwhile, Sakthi comes across a dumb girl  in the neighbourhood. After a few encounters, romance blossoms between them. But the girl's brother plans to push her to flesh trade. But all plans go haywire when they execute their murder plans. Sakthi loses a couple of friends in front of his eyes. An angry Sakthi turns to Sardar for help. But Sardar flees from the place.
