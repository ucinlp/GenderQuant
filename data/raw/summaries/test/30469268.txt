Jerry Florea  is planning a heist. The story begins with the events which led a young Florea  to become a crook. One day he is shot during a robbery and as a result an ameniable policeman and his wife take him under their wing.<ref nameLangman|first1Finn|first2A guide to American crime films of the forties and fifties|urlmqMYAQAAIAAJ|accessdate1995|publisher254}} As a young man he deludes them, and pretends to no longer have criminal intent and even gets a job at the Brinks. They are unaware he is preparing to rob the establishment. It is only after he and his gang pull off the heist that Florea reconsiders his actions and attempts to make amends for the crime.
