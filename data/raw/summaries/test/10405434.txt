Faten Hamama plays Fayza, a young student who lives with her family after the death of her father. Left with no money, her mother  is forced to turn her house into an illegal gambling house. Fayza opposes her mother's solution. Munir  is a writer who meets Fayza and falls in love with her but she rejects him. Fayza decides to leave to the countryside where she works as a teacher in a small school. Fayza gets into trouble in the school and, desperate and hopeless, decides to walk in her mother's path.
