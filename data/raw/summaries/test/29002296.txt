A young Irishman Patrick Shaw  enters the US illegally with his wayward friend Seamus . Landing in New York, the pair start with dreams of striking it rich but soon find themselves in dead-end jobs and drifting apart. As Seamus pursues various get-rich schemes, the more cautious Patrick finds himself devoting time to looking after Tom , an Irish author down on his luck, and courting Helen , a struggling actress caught in sordid relationship with a much older man .
