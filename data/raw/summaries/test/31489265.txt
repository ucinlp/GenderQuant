Narrated by Mandy , as she tells the story of her friend Billy Owens  an average boy who has just turned 11. But notes the strangeness of him being born at the stroke of 11 on November 11 which is later discovered to be a number of great power. Billy, along with know-it-all Mandy and their cowardly but loyal best friend Devon  discover that Billy's family has magical origins, and upon a chance encounter with a mysterious shop keeper  who Billy buys a wand from for $11, they also find out that he can use the wand to cast magic spells, and together they embark on a journey to save their town of Spirit River from a foretold prophecy and prevent the resurrection of a dragon under the influence of the ancient Viking trickster God Loki.
