A wealthy old scrooge disowns his son after he decides to marry a poor woman. After the son dies the old man still wants nothing to do with his daughter-in-law and grandson. One day his grandson is hit by one of the trucks belonging to his grandfather’s factory. The boy is taken to the old man’s home and unbeknownst to him that it’s his grandson the old man takes a liking to the boy.
