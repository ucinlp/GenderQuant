The unhappy marriage of Jane  and her alcoholic policeman husband Bonner  leads to divorce and Jane get the custody for their son. Soon, she meets a gentleman in her workplace, after a few weeks they fall in love and get married. Gentleman Douglas Patrick Brody  and his new wife Jane Brody attempt to build a new life, and move to a new state. However, the ex-husband follows them. This is just a beginning of the story filled with suspense and surprises.
