Chutki  is an enormous fan of Bollywood film star Madhuri Dixit and pines for the chance to follow in her idol's footsteps. As she makes her wishes known to her parents, she learns that her parents have other plans for her that include an arranged marriage. Fortunately, Chutki's best friend Raja  shares her dreams and offers to marry her so that the two can work together on Chutki's acting career. Just as soon as the two small-town innocents arrive in Mumbai, they learn some serious lessons about the hardships of the profession, as well as discover the numerous wannabes that are in direct competition with Chutki.
