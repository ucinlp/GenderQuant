Azhagar , son of a police constable, is tempted to take bad habits seeing his father in his younger days. Azhagar along with his friends A to Z  Azhagappa  and Inippu Murugan  lead a carefree life involving in petty crimes.Unfortunately they hold responsibility in the death of Viruman'ssister.In a bid to take revenge on them, Viruman hires a contract killer Pandi , who is in the business only to educate his young brother. Whats happens after this forms the climax.
