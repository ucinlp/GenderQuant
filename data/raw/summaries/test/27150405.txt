Vajram movie is about Devarajan , a Mafioso turned crane operator. Circumstances and the shenanigans of one of his relatives  see to that Devarajan is separated from his rich wife Nanda . In the event, he is also separated from his son Appu . Amidst all this, one of the many villains in the film aims for the fortune and money of Nandini.
