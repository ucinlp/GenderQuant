At the turn of the 20th century, circus owner Matt Masters  takes his troupe on a tour of Europe in search of his long-lost love, Lili Alfredo . With him are Lili's daughter, Toni , whom she abandoned years earlier and whom he has raised as his own, and his faithful friend, Cap Carson . The circus is lost in a sinking ship, but Masters manages to salvage part of it. With the help of some European acts, he puts together a new show. Masters ultimately finds Lili, so mother and daughter are reunited.
