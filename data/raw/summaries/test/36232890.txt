A criminally insane scientist, Ryuichi Sakamoto , transfers his mind into a cyborg and immediately commits a series of rapes and murders. Among his victims is female police officer Selena Lam . The scientist Dr. Sara  transfers Selena's brain into a cyborg named Eve-27, then copies her own persona into a robotic assistant named Ann . The cyborg-robot team pursue the criminal Sakamoto by investigating a series of murdered prostitutes.
