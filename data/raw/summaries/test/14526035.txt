The Stooges are poster hangers who manage to destroy one of the main posters just as their boss Herman  comes by to check on them. The boys soon realize that their pay consists of tickets to the circus, but when Curly finds a huge roll of tickets, the trio start scalping them at discount price. After being caught by the circus owner  and the local sheriff , Herman decides to hire the Stooges as human targets for the spear-throwing "Sultan of Abudaba" .
