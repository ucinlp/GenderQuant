This movie deals with the cause and consequences of teenage pregnancy. The heroine , hailing from a poor background, falls in love with an affluent young man . The young man impregnates her and leaves her in the lurch. The heroine's classmate  comes forward to marry her and give an identity to the child. Unfortunately, the classmate dies due to illness soon after. The young man comes back and unexpectedly meets his son. The classmate writes a letter that he had considered the heroine as his sister, and she was as pure as river Ganges. Finally the young man accepts the heroine. This movie has very good songs and great lyrics. This movie is an adaptation of the novel 'Avadhaana' by Dr. Dodderi Venkatagiri Rao.
