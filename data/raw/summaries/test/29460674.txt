Zachariah Dodd, a rich lawyer, hires Michael Nash ,a private investigator, to obtain evidences that is wife, Eve Dodd  is unfaithful so he can divorce her. Michael Nash discovers instead that Eve is rather physically abused by her husband and have committed no adultery. She attempts to commit suicide to escape from the torments of her marriage by drowning herself naked into the sea. Michael rescues Eve and fall in love with her. The two begin secretly a love affair. One night, Eve accidentally shoots her husband dead. Michael proposes his help to Eve to cover her and avoid being suspected for her husband's death.
