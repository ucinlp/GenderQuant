It's saga of a girl born in a poor family who is forced to suppress her ambitions for want of money. Her romantic life makes her face some embarrassing situations. The story revolves around Geetha, , a middle-class girl, who suppresses her ambitions due to poverty. She has to fear the society and also her drunkard brother  who always mistreats her. Though she likes Krishna , she elopes with Narendra , son of a rich man, for money. A ghastly incident makes her lead a miserable life unable to face society, but her lover Krishna comes to her help. The story takes many exciting turns with a brilliant climax. Totally, it is 'full feeling maga' story. The music of the film was super-hit because of tracks like "Hrudayave Bayaside Ninnane" sung by Sonu Nigam and "Neenaada Maathu" by Rajesh Krishnan.
