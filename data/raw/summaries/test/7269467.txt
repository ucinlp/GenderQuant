After feeling that he is being repeatedly humiliated and unwanted by his mother and brother, eight-year-old Naani goes to a Scientist who turns him into a 28 year old man. As an adult, he works in a Toy Company and is pursued by Priya , daughter of his superior. He gets married to her and has a child. His mother Devayani starts looking for him and lodges a complaint in police station that her son is missing. Naani learns of his mother's love for him and asks the scientist to turn him back into a child. But the scientist's reverse process does not work and he ends being an eight-year-old with his mother during the day and as an adult as Priya's husband at night.
