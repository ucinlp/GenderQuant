The plot focuses on a prominent concert pianist, Charlotte Andergast , who has been neglectful and dismissive of her children, whom she has not seen in over seven years. Charlotte decides to make a visit to her eldest daughter, Eva  at her remote house, where she lives with her husband, Viktor . Upon arrival, Charlotte discovers that her other daughter, Helena , who is mentally and physically disabled  is living with and being taken care of by Eva.
