Lottie Mason is a Los Angeles cop, working vice. She has a number of problems at work, being under Internal Affairs investigation after a shooting incident and being sexually harassed by Lt. Morgan, her superior officer. Forced by the department to see a psychiatrist, Mason reveals fantasies about the pleasures of losing control. She has begun a romantic relationship with a dependable FBI agent named Harris, but seems to yearn for a more thrilling experience. Mason goes undercover in Hollywood as a prostitute, dressing provocatively and living dangerously. A mob crime witness being searched for by fellow officers is picked up at a bar by Mason, then killed shortly after they have sex. Her own life becomes increasingly at risk as she sinks into the depths of her obsession.
