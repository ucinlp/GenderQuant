Appa Rao  is a career-oriented fashion designer who is in his 40s. He is still a bachelor due to lack of opportunities and career orientation. That is when a posh lady, Pravallika , approaches him and makes him fall in love with her. She offers him a new career and great business in the UK. Meantime, it is revealed that there is a dreaded ISI agent called John Alias Mastan  who looks just like Appa Rao. The rest of the story is all about what happens to Appa Rao when his look-alike John spots him.
