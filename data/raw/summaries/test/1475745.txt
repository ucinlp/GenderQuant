Agnes , in the midst of a struggle to overcome her own self destructive behaviour, returns to Sydney, Nova Scotia from Toronto, Ontario because of the failing health of her mother Rose . She is met by her older sister Theresa , a devout Catholic who has recently been dumped by her husband for a younger woman, and Louise ([[Stacy Smith , the middle child who has retreated from the outside world. Her arrival sets in motion a chain of events that allows the family to reconnect with the world and one another.
