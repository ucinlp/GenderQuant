An elderly baker named Miller  is murdered by a sinister stranger . A trail leads on to a nightclub singer, Leda Hamilton  who reveals that she and Miller have been in thrall to an organization of Nazi fifth columnists led by Ebbing . She is helped by a well-meaning sports promoter, Alfred "Gloves" Donahue , who himself is suspected of murdering a nightclub owner , and has to track down those responsible to prove his innocence.
