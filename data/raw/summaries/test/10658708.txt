Wafadaar  is a Bollywood action flick wherein Daya Sagar  plots to obtain the wealth of Rajgiri . Sager asks his servant Ranga  to woo Rajgiri's daughter Seeta  and marry her. Ranga is unaware that Daya's motive is to kill Seeta to possess her father's fortune. Once Ranga finds out Daya's intent, he revolts and foils Daya's murderous plans. Ranga and Seeta wed and happily live in married bliss.{{Cite web}}
