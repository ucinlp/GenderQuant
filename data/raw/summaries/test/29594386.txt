The sequel to Motives picks up three years after the original. Emery Simms is a rarity--an innocent man in prison. On the outside, his ex-wife  is married to his best friend , and they're attempting to make a normal life. But Simms is killed in prison, and his brother Donovan  returns to discover the real reason for his brother's death. The web of lies and murder may prove just as poisonous to Donovan as it was to his late brother.
