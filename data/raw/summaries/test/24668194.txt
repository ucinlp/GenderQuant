Leslie Hindenberg has just entered senior year, but going to the doctors to get a mole checked out she mistakenly believes she only has six weeks to live and goes about trying to lose her virginity. However it's quite hard when her father is the school's PE coach. While on the other side of the coin is Alan Holt, whose pals brag about their sexual encounters, which leaves him rather frustrated as he can't stop thinking about sex as he goes about trying to lose his virginity anyway possible.
