The film is set in a slum in Chennai. Balaji 's mother dies and his father Jothi , a drunkard, leaves him on the streets. He works hard and ensures a decent living for his younger brother Karthik ([[Nani . Karthik, who studies in an engineering college, spends all his time with his friend Vishnu , who is also brought up in the locality. There is Revathy  in the neighbourhood, who loves Karthik. Jothi takes to illegal ways and makes money working for a drug peddler Ammaji . Knowing that Vishnu is in love with Viji , a call girl in the locality, Jothi decides to exploit him for his selfish gain. He gives him an assignment - to transport drugs to Pondicherry, promising to give him Viji if he completes the work. Vishnu takes the help of Karthik and leaves for Pondicherry. However sensing that something is fishy, Vishnu returns to Chennai. What follows next is the story of 'Veppam'.
