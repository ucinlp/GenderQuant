This movie is a retelling of the Cinderella fairy tale in a modern setting, with Mary Santiago , a high school student with ambitions of becoming a dancer, taking the role of Cinderella; Tami  Mary's only friend as her fairy godmother; Dominique Blatt  taking the role of the stepmother; Britt  and Bree  as the two stepsisters; and Joey Parker, , now a famous celebrity that has returned to school for his senior year and to remember why he started dancing, as the prince. A school dance substitutes for the ball, with the role of the glass slipper filled by a Zune.{{cite web}}
