After the death of her father, Nina  is charged with fulfilling his last wish – bring her three brothers back together… Sam , a hardened cop currently on suspension; Leo , a loud-mouthed overbearing bail bondsman; and Douglas , a handsome 20-year-old thief fresh out of prison. When Leo discovers the con he pledged a lot of money for is suspected of kidnapping one of the wealthiest men in the country, he convinces his two brothers to join him on what will become a dangerous, yet exhilarating adventure.
