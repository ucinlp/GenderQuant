Stewart stars as Prof. Robert Leaf, an American college professor with a genius son, the precocious Erasmus . After using his skills for gambling at the racetrack, Erasmus becomes infatuated with model and actress Brigitte Bardot . Through his love letters, Erasmus is invited to visit Brigitte in France, and Stewart follows along. Prof. Leaf uses his son's talent to raise funds for liberal arts college scholarships with the assistance of con-man Peregrine Upjohn who plans to abscond with the funds.
