Pratap meets a young woman named Varuna. He falls in love with her and soon they get married. He later finds Varuna at midnight in a night-club, where she sings, dances and gets drunk. When he demands an explanation from Varuna's part, she denies ever doing such things. Then Pratap meets a young man - Dilip, who claims to be Varuna's lover, and states that she is actually his girlfriend Peggy. It later reveals that Varuna has a multiple personality disorder.
