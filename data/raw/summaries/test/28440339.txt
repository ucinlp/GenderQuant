Cassie Nightingale has settled comfortably in Middleton with a successful boutique and is now engaged to Police Chief Russell . But with only two short weeks till Christmas, Jake is desperate to find the perfect gift. With no wedding date set, Jake wants to give Cassie a family for Christmas this year and suggests they get married on Christmas Eve. But things do not always happen as planned and Cassie must use a bit of magic to give her new family that special Christmas.
