The inhabitants of Curalha, a small village in western Portugal, perform the Passion of Jesus every year according to text from about the 16th century, Oliveira stumbled upon this during the production of a film in 1963; the film is also remembered for "a furious apocalyptic montage that links Christ's death to the violence and lunacy of the Vietnam era".March 15 - 29 Manoel de Oliveira, or Cinema, the Art of Enigma, Harvard Film School, 1999, subsection "Rite of Spring"
