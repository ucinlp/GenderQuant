Lawrence Bourne III  is a spoiled rich kid with a large gambling debt in the 1960s. After his father, Lawrence Bourne Jr. , refuses to pay his son's debt, Lawrence escapes his angry debtors by trading places with his college roommate Kent  and jumping on a Peace Corps flight to Southeast Asia. There he is assigned to build a bridge for the local villagers with Washington State University graduate Tom Tuttle  and the beautiful, down-to earth Beth Wexler . What they do not realize is that the bridge is coveted by the United States Army, a local communist force, and the powerful drug lord Chung Mee .
