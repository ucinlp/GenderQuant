Vicky  is an ambitious young blonde bombshell living in Hollywood. On the run from her violent boyfriend, she gets hired to be the "Toy" of her favorite rock star, Stu , who is in desperate need of inspiration. Starry-eyed and reckless, Vicky enters a world of fantasy fueled by addiction and an abusive thirst for fame. Prey to the hidden agendas of those around him, Stu's fantasy world meets harsh reality in a head on collision that forces him to connect with Vicky. The record company needs a hit but Stu can't find the magic. Fate brought them together but will the music break them apart?
