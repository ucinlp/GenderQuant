After 'Trans Regional Airlines' is hit by a series of mysterious plane crashes, National Transportation Safety Board safety expert Renee Brennan  calls for grounding of the airline's panes, but is thwarted by Mark Ettinger , who repeatedly countermands her orders for grounding the planes. After Mark is himself killed in a crash, Brennan and FBI agent Scott Wallace  track doen the sabateur, Michael Ives , a former pilot seeking revenge against the airline, but are themselves caught in a trap he set for them at 33,000 feet.
