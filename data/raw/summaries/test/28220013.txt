In this film, Vijay Yesudas is playing the role of an established singer who takes part in a reality show of a popular channel. The specialty of this reality show is that it features a number of convicts taking part in the competition. Bala plays a goon who is a convict now and is also taking part in this competition. He develops a special relationship with the character played by Vijay Yesudas, which leads into many unexpected twists in the life of both.
