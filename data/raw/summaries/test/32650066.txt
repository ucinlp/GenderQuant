Anne, 9 years, is an energetic girl with more important affairs than falling in love. But when she meets Jørgen, the new boy in class she falls in love immediately. Some grown-ups do not think you can fall in love for real when you are 9 years old, but Anne know better: You can! Jørgen is moving into the mysterious and scary Bandit House. A house none of the kids dare to come close. But that is of no hinder for Anne, she is willing to go further than most to win him over. When done in the name of love, everything is allowed, isn't it?
