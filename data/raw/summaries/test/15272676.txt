Having broken up and separated with his previous girlfriend, law student Eun-sik is keen to consummate a new relationship with Kyung-ah, a popular swimming champion with a painful past. Kyung-ah was a patient with suicidal tendency because of an unfortunate event happened with her. Despite help from his friends, Eun-sik begins to doubt himself when Gi-joo, a suave prosecutor, tries to compete for Kyeong-ah's affections. Kyeong-ah has a dark past in which she was raped when she was in high school, this results in her not able to engage as much sexual contact towards Eun-sik, this then makes him very frustrated. In the end Kyeong-ah is about to leave for America with Gi-joo but then realizes that she only loves Eun-sik and nobody else.
