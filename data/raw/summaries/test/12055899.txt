Chubby high school student and Madonna-devotee Oh Dong-ku believes herself to be a woman trapped in a man's body. Living with her abusive, alcoholic father, Dong-ku works part-time and saves her money for the sex change surgery she craves. Despite being told that she has the perfect physique for ssireum, Dong-ku has no interest in taking up sports, but when she finds out about an upcoming ssireum tournament with a large cash prize going to the winner, she changes her mind and signs up for the team.
