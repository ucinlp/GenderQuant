Wally Saunders  wants to marry chorus girl Violet Dayne , but his uncle, Stephen Lee  thinks that all chorines are gold diggers and refuses to give his approval. Violet's friend Jerry La Mar  is not a gold digger, but she agrees to go after Lee so aggressively that Violet will look tame by comparison.
