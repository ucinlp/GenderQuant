Hopeless nobody; Harry Balbo Sean Patrick Flanery, is struggling with his job and social life. Until one day he witness' an attack by a vampire and becomes determined to kill the creature. In the basement of his apartment complex he builds a cage which he uses to trap the beautiful vampire Tatiana Charlotte Ayanna; but instead of killing her, he decides to let her live. Keeping her alive and slowly connecting with her but never knowing for sure if she really cares about him or if she just wants to eat him and eventually questioning if he should let her.
