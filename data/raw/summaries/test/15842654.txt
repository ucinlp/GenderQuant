The story tells of Harry Howells , a recent college graduate who's madly in love with his sweetheart Ethel  and hopes to marry her. His woman hating uncle, however, Fire chief Amos McCarthy , tells his nephew to avoid marriage because all women want is money. Even though Harry is determined to marry Ethel, it seems his uncle was right: Ethel is a gold-digger. Harry is crestfallen. Her sister, Mary Morgan , however, is very interested in Harry. Still, unhappy, Harry spends the night in the firehouse. That night the fire alarm goes off, and it gives hapless Harry a chance to prove his mettle.
