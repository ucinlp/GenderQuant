Vijay Verma  is a rich and zahid a selfish playboy who enjoys playing with women by giving a fake name to every girl. His friend, Gopal,  tells him several times to stop this act otherwise he will regret it. One day, Vijay meets Seema  and falls in love with her after a few meetings. During a separation, Seema sends Vijay a letter about her father agreeing to marriage with Vijay. He replies saying that he will leave immediately to get to her place. After the marriage, Seema finds out that the person who has married her is none other than Gopal and he was tricked into marrying her knowing Vijay loves her too.
