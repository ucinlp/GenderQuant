The story is about local warrior-freedom fighter Parmin  who fights against Dutch colonial army in West Java in 19th century. During his struggle, he has to face hired warriors and pagan magician Kohar  who are Dutch operatives with supernatural powers. The final showdown is between Jaka Sembung and Ki Item , a warrior with magical power - his organs  may be cut off his body, but as long as it touches the ground, the organs will return. Only by his wit and skills Jaka Sembung can finally defeat this deadly warrior.
