The plot involves a group of Wyoming State convicts who stage an escape with the help of a smokejumper turned arsonist on the outside. The story begins when Shaye, who had stolen 37 million dollars four years previously and hidden it in the Wyoming forest, escapes from prison with five other convicts to retrieve the money. They pose as Canadian firefighters and take Jennifer , a bird watcher, hostage along the way. Jesse Graves  is a smokejumper, who must find a way to stop Shaye and his group of convicts and save Jennifer at the same time.
