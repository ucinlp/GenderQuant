 Sofia's Last Ambulance opens on an ordinary working day of Krassi, Mila and Plamen, the paramedic crew on one of Sofia's dangerously dwindling fleet of emergency ambulances.{{Cite web}} Unusual for a work about medical services, patients remained tactfully outside of the frame and sensationalism was rigorously avoided.
