Robert Duvall plays the club coach, Gordon McLeod. Jackie McQuillan  is the team's striker who has recently been signed from Arsenal. McQuillan is a legendary ex-Celtic  player as well as the coach's son-in-law, and has a reputation for being troublesome. The two men put their personal problems aside as they try to prevent the small fishing town of Kilnockie from losing its club, which is owned by an American  who wants to move the team to Dublin in Ireland.
