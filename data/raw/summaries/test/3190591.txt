Bulldog Drummond  is injured when his car that has been sabotaged is involved in a crash. When Jack Pennington  agrees to masquerade as a sleuth, he is enlisted to help Ann Manders  find her jeweller grandfather who has been kidnapped by a gang of crooks who want him to copy a valuable necklace they want to steal. Their plan backfires in the British Museum and the film climaxes in an exciting chase on a runaway train in the London Underground.
