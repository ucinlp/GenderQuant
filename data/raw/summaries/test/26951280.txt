Described as "Angela Carter rewriting La Belle et la Bête as an episode of Buffy the Vampire Slayer",{{cite news}} the story follows, through a tapestry of dreamlike images, a girl  and her three friends—the characters' names are never revealed—as they find themselves stranded in a dark and surreal forest by someone—or something —who has obsessively loved, watched, and waited for the girl ever since childhood.
