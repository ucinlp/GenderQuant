The film tells the true story of the bisexual serial-killing con man and jewel thief Archibald Hall , who got himself hired as a butler named Roy Fontaine and then robbed and murdered numerous employers of his throughout England and Scotland. Hall gets hired as the butler of the upper-class family of Fiona Carrick-Smith , eventually becoming her confidant as she is intrigued by his wisdom. She tells him of her reservations and misgivings about her upcoming marriage and her desire for excitement and taking risks. When she acts on that desire, what follows is a string of reckless behaviour, betrayal, and disaster, as Hall leaves a bloody trail behind him.
