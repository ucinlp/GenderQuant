Vicki Lynn  is a waitress who is transformed into a fashion model by press agent Steve Christopher . When Vicki is murdered, detective Ed Cornell  tries to blame the crime on Christopher. In fact, the cop knows who the real killer is, but he is so hopelessly in love with the dead girl that he intends to railroad an innocent man to the electric chair. With the help of Vicki's sister Jill , Christopher tracks down the real killer and exposes the crooked cop Cornell.
