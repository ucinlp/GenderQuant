In rural Yugoslavia, Lenka  lives with her blind brother, Miscia . They were orphaned of their Greek Orthodox mother and their Jewish father, Rasco  is believed to have been killed in the war. As the Second World War continues to rage and fascism activity blights Europe, Lenka and her brother becoming increasingly vulnerable targets to anti-semitic sentiment. She finds support in Ivan , a partisan in love with her. Meanwhile Rasco returns alive, despite resports of his death. Rasco ultimately sacrifices himself to save the life of Ivan, who lies injured in the family's attic. The SS return to collect Lenka and Miscia, who do not reveal the whereabouts of Ivan.
