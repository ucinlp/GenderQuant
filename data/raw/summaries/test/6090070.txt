Seenu  is a loyal servant and a right-hand of faction leader Reddeppa . He and Sailaja  - daughter of Reddeppa - grew together. Nayudamma  is an arch rival of Reddeppa. Factional feuds between them have resulted in loss of number of lives among their groups. When Reddeppa finds out that his daughter and Seenu are in love, he swings into action by trying to kill Seenu. Rest of the story is all about how Seenu secures the hand of Sailaja  and how he unites the forces of followers in both the warring groups to teach their masters  a lesson or two.
