Mio is a young woman whose husband is a millionaire's son. When he becomes impotent following an automobile accident, she begins an affair with their gardener and chauffeur, Nitta. After her husband discovers Mio's infidelity, he forces her to leave.<ref nameWeisser|firstYuko Mihara Weisser|title1998|publisherMiami|isbn 223–224}}{{cite journal}}"Lady Chatterley in Tokyo".
