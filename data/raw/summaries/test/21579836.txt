Rahul Desai, an employee in a bank weds Swati through arranged marriage, as last wish of his ill grandmother who wants to see her grandson's wedding before she dies. The couple hardly know each other. They go to Mahabaleshshwar for honeymoon. He drinks and she does not like it. He likes English movies which she thinks are vulgar. They come across many differences. The film shows various moments in development of their relationship, adjustments with personalities and mood swings and how they finally live happily.
