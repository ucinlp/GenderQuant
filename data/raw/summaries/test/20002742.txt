The film focuses on a group of students from Los Angeles who travel to Palm Springs to spend the Easter weekend there. Student Jim Munroe  falls in love with Bunny Dixon , the daughter of an overprotective police chief . Munroe's room mate Biff Roberts  and plain-jane Amanda North  try to seduce each other. Spoiled rich playboy Eric Dean  and Doug Fortune , a Hollywood stuntman from Texas, compete for the attentions of a pretty girl  from Beverly Hills.
