The drama is centred on two of the leading players and their respective companies in the home computer market of the late 1970s and early 80s focusing on the race to become the provider of a home computer for the BBC's programming for schools.<ref namehttp://www.guardian.co.uk/technology/gamesblog/2009/oct/08/armstrong-freeman-micro-men-bbc | titleThe Guardian | date13 March 2012 | author=Arnott, Jack}} Certain parts of the drama are based on historical fact whilst others are dramatisation and a version of events at the time.
