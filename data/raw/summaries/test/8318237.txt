After their father and mother dies, played by Rance Howard, a group of family of children decide to live self-sufficiently in the backwoods of rural North Carolina rather than have the state realize what happened and then split them up by sending them to foster homes. Mary Call , while not the oldest of the children, does assume the "parent" role making adult decisions that she can't handle on her own, proving a formidable opponent for landlord played by Harry Dean Stanton, who is interested in marrying the oldest daughter, but can't because of a promise Mary Call made to her father before dying. Much to everyone's surprise, Stanton turns out to be one of the good guys, but what will he do if he gets sick and there is no one there to help him. There are troubles along the way, are they going to be abled to solve them. Based on the novel and movie.
