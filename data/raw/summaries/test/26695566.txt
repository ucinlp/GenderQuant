Sister Amala , a nun is found dead in the well of the convent where she was staying. The police discovers that she was murdered. Idamattom Palackal Easow Panickar and his team finds out the truth behind Sr. Amala's murder. The suspects include Fr. Clement Kaliyar and the local legislator Monayi and a hitman - Cardinal Carlos. Things get worse when one of police officers, Ezhuthachan gets killed and also Kaliyar Achan.
