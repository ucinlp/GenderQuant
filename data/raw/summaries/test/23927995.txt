The plot is based on an anti-war military science fiction story by Sever Gansovsky. In the not-too-distant future a scientist from a military great power wants revenge for his son  and thus invents a weapon capable of ending the conflict - an automated mind-reading tank that detects and reacts to human feelings of hostility and fear. After his revenge successfully takes place  he himself falls victim to his own creation. The film has an open ending.
