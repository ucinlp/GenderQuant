The ultimate New York City playboy Jack Frost  is shattered when he discovers his one true love Carolina  is about to marry another man. Still haunted by his mother's suicide, Jack spirals into a self-destructive cycle of whiskey and reckless behavior, while his best friends Ozzy  and Scotch  try to jar him back to reality. Strangely enough, it's Jack's eleven year-old neighbor, Sophie , who helps the drowning urbanite shake off the nostalgia and excess that threaten to consume him.
