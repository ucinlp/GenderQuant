Textile company heir James Wayland  is accused of murdering a prostitute named Elizabeth , whose body was found cut in two. The murder is investigated by tough detective Kennesaw  and his less experienced partner Braxton . Wayland is a heavy drinker and compulsive liar, prone to memory losses and periods of violence. He is rich enough to access necessary information, however, and he soon learns his interrogators' own dark secrets - Kennesaw had an affair with Elizabeth to get back at his wife  for cheating on him, while Braxton has gambling debts with a bookie named Mook , who is demanding payment.
