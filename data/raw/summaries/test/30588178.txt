When the older brother  he idolizes is run off the road by a ruthless drug dealer  during a nighttime street race known as Sepulveda Suicide, Rick Merchant  channels his grief into getting revenge behind the wheel. But to win, he'll need to modify his trusty 1988 Mazda RX-7/Nissan 240sx -- with help from a mechanic Kelly  -- to get the maximum performance out of his machinery.
