Adventure writer John Weatherby  is called in to investigate a series of brutal murders that has investigators confused. The clues are not clear and leave no clear trail. The tracks left at the murder site appear to be that of a wolf, or a man, or both. Lost in his search for answers, Weatherby contacts his old friend Byron , a mysterious hunter and a recluse, to help him track this unknown killer. His friend is not much help but may know more than what he's willing to say.
