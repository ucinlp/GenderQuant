Kol , an alien criminal, escapes from a spaceship into the woods of an American suburb. The commander  of the spaceship dispatches The Alienator  -- a deadly female android, to capture Kol . He meets up with some teenagers as they are all running from fiery death at the hands of The Alienator. She relentlessly pursues Kol and the teens through a series of action sequences and low budget special effects. Kol must face the toughest decision of his life: kill or be killed.
