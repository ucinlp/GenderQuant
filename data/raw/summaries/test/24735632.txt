The movie centers on two siblings, an 11-year-old boy named Chan-yi  and his little sister So-i . Abandoned by their mother, they are left to survive on their own. For his sister's 6th birthday, Chan-yi decides to give her a dog. He sneaks into a house of an old couple and steals a newly-born puppy, which she's been longing to have. Even though they are poor, they were happy. But everything changes after a tragic accident; Soi dies and Chan is left on his own to survive the streets
