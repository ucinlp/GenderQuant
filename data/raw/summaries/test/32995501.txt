A young Englishman  leaves his actress girlfriend  to seek an experience in Australia.{{cite news}} He works as a jackeroo on a property and falls in love with the daughter  of a wealthy squatter. They are happy until the actress arrives and joins forces with an evil overseer. They persuade some local aborigines to raid the squatter's home and capture the Englishman. The squatter's daughter rides to the rescue and a loyal aboriginal helps saves the day.
