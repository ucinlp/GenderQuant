The film tells the story of Nick Rinaldi , who has spent his life in one New Jersey city, getting a free ride from his well-connected father  and hearing the locals talk of his brother's death in Vietnam. He's usually high on drink and drugs. As Rinaldi searches for more self-control, he quits the contractor's job provided by his father, feeling that major events are about to happen in his life. By the film's end his life will change, as will the lives of many others.
