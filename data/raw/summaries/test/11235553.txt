Anandi Devi  is an old widow who lives in a village. One day she is visited by a distant relative, Arun Gupta , who persuades her to live in town with him, his wife Lata , and a small child. She later realizes that they were looking for a maid which results in her expulsion from the house and befriending a child beggar who takes her to his dilapidated home. Being good-natured and caring, she slowly earns the title of 'Nani Ma'  among groups of youths led by Shyam  and Chaino  who were in constant fight with each other. The movie ends with her death from an accidental gun fire in one such clash.
