Rosalind Dee  is a cafe singer striving to enter high society. Rosalind stumbles upon a large amount of money by lucky means. Rosalind forms a partnership with gambling-club owners Wade Evans  and Cyril Mace , building the establishment into a gathering place for the elite. Along the way, she attempts to rekindle a romance with randy playboy Doug Duryea , but she is soon lured in by Rev. Kelly Rand  who comes to save Rosalind from her troubles.
