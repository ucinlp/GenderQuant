Dave  has had some bad luck recently. His wife  lives in Washington, DC, his restaurant, the Auk is not doing good business. Phonse  helps Dave by making up a story about a rare bird sighting which begins to help Dave's business. Phonse has been working on a prototype Recreational Submarine Vehicle  and is concerned that the Winnebago company is conducting industrial espionage steal his plans. Phonse also finds ten kilos of cocaine and tries to get rid of it with the help of Dave. Dave falls in love with Alice , Phonse's sister-in-law, a girl from Gull Tickle.
