Sivan  is a medical college student better known in the campus as 'Sultan' as he was considered by all as the 'Sultan of Romance'. He and his four childhood close friends forms a gang in the campus and they become quite popular among the students. Sivan is in love with Nishitha , who is also a student in the same college. Their love has been accepted by their families and they have been engaged to marry after the completion of their studies. Things take a turn when Sivan's pet-name Sultan puts him in some totally unexpected situations.
