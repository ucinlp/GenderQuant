In colonial America, David Harvey , a recent widower living in the wilderness, decides that his young boy Davey ([[Gary Gray  needs a woman around to help raise him. He goes to the nearest settlement and consults Parson Jackson . David gets talked into buying the contract of an indentured servant named Rachel  and marrying her. Their marriage, however, is in name alone. Rachel serves more as a servant than a wife and Davey resents what he sees as an attempt to replace his dead mother Susan. Jim Fairways , a family friend , visits and falls in love with Rachel. When he offers to buy her, David must fight to keep her and discovers his love in the process.
