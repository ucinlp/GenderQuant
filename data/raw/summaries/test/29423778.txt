The film, set in Portland, opens with Doug  moving in with his sister, Gail . Doug has recently moved from Chicago, where he was studying forensic science and lived with now ex-girlfriend Rachel . After getting a job at an ice factory, he befriends Carlos , a co-worker who also DJs on the side. Rachel arrives in Portland to train at the home office of the Chicago law firm where she is employed, but suddenly disappears, leaving a trail of intriguing clues. Doug, Gail and Carlos begin investigating her whereabouts.
