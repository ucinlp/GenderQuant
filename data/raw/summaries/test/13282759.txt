A senior Royal Air Force officer  is at a dinner party in Hong Kong at which one of those present, , talks about a dream he had in which the Air Force Officer and a group of companions are flying in a Dakota which crashes on a rocky shore. The Air Marshal is due to fly to Tokyo the following day, but is not disturbed because many of the details differ from his planned voyage. However, as the flight proceeds circumstances change so that eventually all the details correspond to the dream. Moreover the plane does actually crash on a rocky shore in Japan.
