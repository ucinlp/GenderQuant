Goundamani is sold to Senthamarai and later deserted by his parents when he was a newborn kid. The Nurse adopts the deserted baby and he grows up in the Nurse's family with her son S. V. Shekhar. Jeevitha, SV Sekhar's sister plays Goundamani's love interest. Years later, unaware of the fact Goundamani goes to work for his own Father who is now a rich businessman and whose business rival turns out to be Senthamarai. Son and father duo bands together and takes on the villain Senthamarai.
