The film is about Abhiroop Sen , a Delhi-based transgender documentary filmmaker, whose bisexual lover is the cinematographer  of the film. They visit Kolkata to make a documentary on the life of the real-life legendary jatra actor Chapal Bhaduri, who in his heyday was known as 'Chapal Rani', noted for his portrayal of female roles on the stage at a time when women did not perform on stage.
