A man captures schoolgirls and takes them to an abandoned freight container in Shinjuku which he has decorated with enlarged black & white images of the faces of his previous victims. He smears the captive girls with paint and shaving cream, rapes, tortures and brutally murders them.Sharp, pp. 263, 268.<ref nameWeisser|firstYuko Mihara Weisser|title1998|publisherMiami|isbn 465}}
