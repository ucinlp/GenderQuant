Ann Sutton , the wife of a successful psychoanalyst , is arrested for shoplifting. Ann is saved from scandal by smooth-talking hypnotist David Korvo . Korvo is not what he seems to be, and Ann soon finds herself involved in blackmail and murder. She is unsure whether or not she committed a crime. Her distant but loyal husband stands up for her and eventually sets up the hypnotist, who he thinks is behind all the misdeeds.
