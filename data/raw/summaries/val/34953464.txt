Within the anonymity of a normal Algerian home, Yamina, Nabila and Imen are regularly beaten by their brother, Tarek. Their mother, a witness to their brother’s violent behavior, does nothing to end the drama suffered by her daughters. Khouya is the story of a drama that takes place behind closed doors and that will soon evolve into a tragedy. After Khti , Kouya  is the second part of the trilogy “Algerian women” created by Yanis Koussim.
