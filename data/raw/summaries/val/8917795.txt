The film is about a gay couple, Jørgen and Jacob, who live in a happy partnership. One day Jacob asks Jørgen to marry him, and he happily accepts. But then Jacob falls in love with the woman Caroline, who happens to be married to Tom - Jørgens brother! Jacob is torn, because he wants both Jørgen and Caroline. When he gets Caroline pregnant, he decides to marry her, but in the church, just before the "I do", he changes his mind.
