Set in 19th century Tiflis, the film details the day-to-day life of a poor but honest Armenian fisherman Pepo  who opposes a cunning trader Arutin Kirakozovich Zimzimov , who has robbed the former by trickery.<ref name http://www.imdb.com/title/tt0026862/combined| title2007-08-11}} The story comes to a conclusion of sorts when Pepo falls in love.
