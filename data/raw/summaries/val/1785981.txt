Malcolm King  is a wealthy, selfish, obnoxious businessman who is about to divorce his wife Renee . She plans to ruin him financially during the court proceedings, and King is willing to do anything to protect his fortune. He enlists his mistress, Peaches , and her brother, Herb , to stage a mock kidnapping. They are to make and receive a huge ransom demand, which would keep the money safe from his wife. Unfortunately for him, two other people have similar plans to kidnap him; Angela , an aggrieved employee and Corey , who lives in his grandmother's basement and needs $10,000 after being threatened by his adopted sister.
