Inderjit "Inder" Bansal  is a very rich young man. Although his parents  want him to settle down, he goes to another city to do his MBA and stays with friends. During this time, he meets Pallavi Sinh  and they fall in love. She reciprocates his feelings, but trouble comes in the form of her tyrannical trio of brothers, who are over-protective of their little sister and take an irrational dislike to Inder. They show hostility towards Inder and conflicts arise within the households and the couple decides to elope, but afterward realize they miss their families. They decide to return home, relinquishing their feelings, but ultimately their families accept their love, and they are reunited.
