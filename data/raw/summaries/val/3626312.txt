The film is about a rich, successful business man named Balajeet "Bala" Roy , who lives a blissful life with his beautiful wife, Nisha Roy . Nisha is expecting their first child together. Bala is suddenly captured for an unknown reason and kept alive in a cell without knowing who are his captives and the reason behind it. While in captivity, practices martial arts which he learns from watching T.V., with the intention of using it against the people who captured him. When he is released, 14 years later, he sets out for revenge.
