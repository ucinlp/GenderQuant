Ivy Lexton  is a woman with a hunger to seduce men. Though she already has a husband, Jervis , and is having an affair with Dr. Roger Gretorex , Ivy becomes obsessed with wealthy Miles Rushworth , and is determined to have him. However, Miles shows no interest because she's a married woman, which angers Lexton. Bored with her monotonous marriage, Ivy plans on poisoning her husband then pinning the blame on Roger so she may run off with Miles. Inspector Orpington  is called to investigate Jervis' mysterious death.
