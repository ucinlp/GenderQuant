Negar  has been kicked out of home by her mother. She is left with no choice but to spend the night walking around on the streets of Tehran where she meets three men who all have different stories.  One Night was screened at the following festivals: *Cannes film festival 2005 *Munich Film Festival *Osian's Connoisseur *Toronto Film Festival *Edition du Festival du film *Sienna Film Festival *Reykjavik International Film Festival 2005 *Brisbane International Film Festival 2006 *Vancouver Film Festival *Gangnam Tyme Park Festival
