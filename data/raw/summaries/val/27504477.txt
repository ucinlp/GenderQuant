The movie is a triangle love story between the lover, the heroine and her husband. Radha  is a typical girl from a lower middle-class family and falls deep in love with the new tenant upstairs Palghat Madhavan . Madhavan, aspires to be a great music director, but struggles to get on by everyday. He gets attracted towards Radha. Dr. Anand , a widower, is forced to marry Radha to fulfill the wish of his dying mother. He fully intends to keep his promise of returning Radha to her rightful owner and partner, Madhavan, after hearing to Radha's reason behind attempting suicide on the first night. Events lead to conflict in her mind between the husband and lover. Whom should Radha choose finally and why?
