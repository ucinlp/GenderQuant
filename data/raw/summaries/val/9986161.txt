The films tells of father, Juan Denevi , who gets the surprise of his life on his fiftieth birthday when he finds out that he's become a grandfather. The movie opens in Buenos Aires, Argentina, and Juan is cooking individual personal favorite dishes for his friends, the patrons of his restaurant. A young woman named Manuela  appears with a 3-year-old boy  and announces that Lucas is Juan's grandson. Juan hasn't seen his son Facundo  for many years, and during this time, Facundo has married and had a son. Facundo's wife and son find Juan at his birthday party and beg him to come help Facundo with a family crisis.
