The plot concerns playboy Virendra Pratap Singh . A clause in the will of his wealthy father forces him to enter a military academy in order to inherit. Virendra's repeated attempts to get himself discharged are foiled by Major Jasbir Singh Rana . Then Virendra falls in love with Nisha , the sister of a gangster named Shankar . Shankar wants Nisha to marry the son of his friend Parshuram Bihari and hatches a plot to separate the couple.
