The film is set in Seattle's mid-90s grunge scene where a female rock group, 'No Exits', is on the verge of signing a record deal. However, the lesbian relationship between the band's singer Suzy Marisa Ryan and guitarist Shelly Molly Gross is under threat when Shelly's ex-boyfriend Jimmy Jason Bortz reappears on the scene. The film deals with issues of love, sexuality, feminism, rape, lesbianism, subcultures, and alienation.
