When the evil alien Zanon comes to enslave earth, all hope seems lost. The Earth's resident superheroes, the Spacewomen, are powerless to stop him. They must enlist the help of a young boy who has a special connection with the giant turtle Gamera. The Friend of All Children then battles Gyaos , Zigra , Viras , Jiger , Guiron  and finally Barugon . Gamera then sacrifices himself to kill Zanon by destroying the space pirate's ship.
