Foodfight! takes place in the supermarket at night after everyone has left. The grocery store transforms into a city, and from every door of this city comes two types of characters: well-known marketing icons and new characters, including Dex Dogtective , Sunshine Goodness  and Daredevil Dan . They all hang out at the Copa-Banana in the produce section.
