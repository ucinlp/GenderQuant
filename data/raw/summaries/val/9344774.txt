Oso  is sent to prison for a robbery and a murder. After seven years, he is released from prison, and in a flashback, the robbery is seen that led to his arrest. His daughter, Alicia , was a year old on the day of the robbery. As a consequence, Alicia never got a chance to get to know her father. Oso returns to his hometown, a depressed suburb of Buenos Aires, fully aware that his wife, Natalia , is living with another man, Sergio . Oso is determined to establish a relationship with Alicia, and to collect money owed him by a sleazy crime boss known as the Turk . In the meantime, the Turk wants Oso to be the getaway driver on one last big job. For her part, Alicia seems fascinated with her father and makes him promise never to go away again.
