Thillainathan ([[Karthik , a young graduate, left his village to an interview. He got the job at the bank, found a house and fell in love with Jeyakodi. His favourite singer, his favourite poet and his favourite politician came, one by one, to his new house to see Thenmozhi. Thillainathan's amazed by the words of his mentor so he decided to find this unknown woman : Thenmozhi. With his sidekick, they went anywhere who she would be but they didn't find her. At his pre-marriage ritual, his detective said that he found Thenmozhi and he went here. But that wasn't her and he was attacked by some goons. The marriage was cancelled, he was fired by his bank company and was expelled from his house. He found a evidence at his house that Thenmozhi was in fact Jeyakodi. He went to her house and her office but she wasn't there. Thillainathan returned to his village and his father didn't consider him any more as his son. Pichamuthu, his friend whose his wife eloped, died and his wife's new husband helped Thillainathan to find a job. Thillainathan got the new job and went to the program. There, he saw Jeyakodi dancing with the famous singer Vasudevan . When he talked to her, she explains him that she was Thenmozhi and she was married to Vasudevan, Thillainathan made a suicide attempt. At the hospital, Vasudevan confirmed that she was Thenmozhi. The doctor asked to Vasudevan to find a person who had AB+ blood group in order to save Thillainathan. Jeyakodi accepted to give her blood but refused when she knew that the patient was her ex-lover, at this moment Thenmozhi came. They were all shocked to see their resemblances. Jeyakodi gave bloods to her ex-lover and saved him. Jeyakodi and Thillainathan decided to get married.
