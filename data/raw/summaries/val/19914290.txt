Magaly  enters a television contest looking for a Marilyn Monroe look-a-like for a $25.000 prize. Her husband, Arturo , believes is the best way to get through the financial crisis they are facing. As the challenge goes by, Magaly partners with Hector  who believes to be the Monroe's reincarnation in a "third world transsexual man". She begins to lose her identity and follows the same downfall of the late actress.
