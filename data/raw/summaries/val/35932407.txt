The Mommy Returns tells the story of a mother, Ruby , who dies right on the day of her 25th wedding anniversary with husband, William . With her untimely death, she leaves behind her three children children Amethyst , Topaz , and Sapphire . Ruby is also temporarily trapped in PURGA  with its guardian, Dyoga  and the pesky Manny, the chicken pet of San Pedro. When Catherine , a younger and prettier woman comes into the life of William, Ruby escapes from PURGA and returns to earth as a ghost to drive Catherine out of the family she left behind.
