Puneeth has acted in dual roles in this movie, father and Son. Father is a good man and helps the needed. Once in his family function he is killed by the Villains and at that time his Son was an Infant. Later part of the movie is about how Son take his father’s role of helping the society and in taking the revenge of his father’s death. This film was released simultaneously with Telugu movie 'Andhrawala' directed by Puri Jagannadh and the script are same.
