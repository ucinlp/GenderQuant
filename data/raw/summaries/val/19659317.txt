Mary  is a rich young lady that challenges problems in a traditional folk neighbourhood in Plaka, Athens, when she moves to play modern music in a store opposite a traditional taverna, where Fotis , a bouzouki player, works along with Kostas , Sofia  and others. The gang of the taverna soon turns against Mary, for the reason that she alterates the character of the neighbourhood. Eventually, Fotis falls in love with Mary, but his friends margin him as he seems willing to sacrifice everything for her in order to be accepted by her social circles. A second love story involves in the film, as Eleni , sister of Fotis, tries to attract Kostas using various ways.
