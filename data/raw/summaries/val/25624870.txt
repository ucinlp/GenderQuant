A group of scientists must stop a mysterious creature from attacking a small town. Sierra Deacon's  team, consisting of Dexter Maines  and Dallas Murphy , must help the locals led by Callie Calhoun  in saving the town from the creature that is killing the residents. After several deaths, a special Army Unit, led by General Jernigan , is called in to contain the creature, and if necessary, destroy the town. It becomes a race against time to stop the creature and prevent the town from being destroyed.
