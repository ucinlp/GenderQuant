Confederate veteran Matt Weaver  returns home to New Mexico after the Civil War and discovers his farm was sold by a banker named Brewster . His fiancee Ruth  has married another man in his absence. Ruth's husband and other Union sympathizers in town resent Weaver's allegiance to the Rebels during the war. His town turns against him, and soon Brewster hires a mulatto gunman named d'Estaing  to come to town and provoke Weaver into a fatal fight.
