The film, with a similar plot to High Noon, tells the story of Dan Ballard ([[John Payne  and Rose Evans  who are about to be married when Marshal Fred McCarty  and his deputies ride into town looking for Ballard. McCarty accuses Ballard of having murdered his brother and has come to arrest him.
