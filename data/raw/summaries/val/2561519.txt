Carole, a Parisian judge, meets Tina, a thief who has been arrested twice for shoplifting expensive shoes. The two women start up an unlikely friendship &mdash; both are the only child and each could be the sister the other always wanted &mdash; after Tina proves herself particularly valuable to a certain case. Carole then takes her on board for another case involving corrupt casino player, Mermot, and a local cop.
