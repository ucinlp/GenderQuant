Lovelorn Kim Hyeon-gon , who has fallen for coffee shop worker Seon-ah , learns about Cyrano Agency, a small organization set up by theatre actor Lee Byeong-hoon  that claims 100% success in making people fall in love. Kim signs on, and the Cyrano team — Byeong-hoon's onetime drama student Min-yeong , plus older Cheol-bin  and younger Jae-pil  — set to work, constructing elaborate scenarios in which Seon-ah is the unwitting target and feeding lines to Hyeon-gon through an earpiece. The operation is a success, but Cyrano Agency needs more clients as its finances are perilous. Their next client is fund manager Lee Sang-yong ([[Daniel Choi , who has fallen for Kim Hee-joong , a young woman he met at church. Byeong-hoon is not keen on taking the case, as it turns out that Hee-joong is an ex-girlfriend of his.
