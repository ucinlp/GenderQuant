Annie McGairy is an eighteen-year old Brooklynite who has moved to the Mid-West to marry Carl Brown, a law student. The couple must overcome many obstacles including, disapproval from their parents who knew each other before emigrating to America from Ireland, along with financial problems, and Annie's sexual insecurities. These problems are all compounded when Annie discovers she is pregnant. After a heated argument between the two, Annie leaves Carl to return to Brooklyn to live with her mother. She does not tell Carl she is pregnant however, due to the fact that she does not want to burden him while he is working on his education. Ultimately, Annie realizes her family should be together and eventually Carl forgives her for leaving him because he believes his child deserves happiness. The couple embrace at the train station at the end and vow to stay together.
