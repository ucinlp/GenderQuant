Prasanna  is nursed back to health by a classical singer Bhairavi  after a bloody street-side fight. The two are slowly drawn to each other and decide to marry in spite of their age difference and a past relationship that Bhairavi is still inextricably tied to. In the meantime, Prasanna's father  embarks on a relationship with the young Ranjini . We soon find out that Ranjini is Bhiaravi's daughter . Both relationships arrive at a dilemma with an unexpected twist when Bhairavi's long-lost husband  comes into picture.
