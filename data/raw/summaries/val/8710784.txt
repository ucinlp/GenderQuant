Clifford Godfrey is a doll house designer who is dumped by his fiance a few hours before they are to depart for a vacation in Mexico. Clare Enfield, a travel agent, informs Godfrey that his tickets are non-refundable. Upset, Godfrey goes to a bar where he meets Amanda Hughes, a drunk party girl, who agrees to travel along with Godfrey. When Hughes sobers, she continually flirts with and then rejects Godfrey. During a stay in a hotel, Godfrey meets up with Enfield again who is photographing a travel brochure. They begin a romance, only to have it disturbed by Hughes.
