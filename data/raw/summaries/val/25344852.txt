Martha Hoagland  is a 16 year old girl suffering through her parents  recent divorce, her mother working long hours and being recently dumped by her boyfriend, feeling lonely and seeking solace she turns to an internet chat room. Martha believes she's chatting with a guy around her age when in reality she's chatting with an internet predator involved in cyber kidnapping ring . Martha is lured to join her new "friend" who claims he never sent her a ticket out, she is then brought to "The Scanman" and its up to Martha's mother Connie to rescue her daughter.
