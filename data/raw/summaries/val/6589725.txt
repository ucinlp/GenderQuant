Azad , Ralf  and 2 other accomplices rob a suitcase of emeralds from a rich Greek citizen, M.Tasco. When Abel Zacharia , the Athenian police chief, surprises Azad's car in front of Tasco's house on the night of the robbing, he decides to let Azad and his team go, hoping he could retrieve the diamonds for himself. Follows a chase between Azad and the corrupt cop through Athens' streets which culminates to the final scene which will be later borrowed by Witness in 1982.
