Andrea Marr  is a bright, straight-A, mature, 18-year-old high school senior on the verge of womanhood who decides to abandon her sheltered, boring lifestyle and her bookish friend Darcy  for a look into the local rock and roll scene. There she meets local rock singer Todd Sparrow , whom she has a groupie fling with. Aspiring rock star Cybil , fellow student outgoing fellow groupie Rebecca  guide Andrea during her time as a groupie.
