G.P, as commonly known in the political circles, Parameshwaran is a top leftist political leader with strong criminal background. Unnikrishnan is now on the run. G.P. understands the potential of Unni, and decides to induct him into the party. G.P strikes a truce between Pappan and Unni. However, G.P had more sinister plans in stock.
