The story follows Kunal and Renuka, a happy-living couple on their first honeymoon. During their first intimate night together, porn-website creator Simi Roy  and her partner Johny  record them and turn them into porn-stars. Renuka can't take the embarrassment and commits suicide. Kunal is then arrested by police for illegal porn online. Kunal proves himself innocent, and teams up with his friend Ali  a modern-day punk, to find clues and to take down Simi Roy and Johny and take revenge for Renuka's murder.
