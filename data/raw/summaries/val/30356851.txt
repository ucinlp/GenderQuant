The story happens in a government school that had a glorious past but is now on the verge of being shut down as there are fewer than 50 students enrolled there. The teachers have lost all hope about making a difference to the situation and are busy with their personal activities. Into this situation comes a young teacher  who makes a difference. The film has a happy ending. The film opened with highly positive reviews, and became a hit. The soundtrack of the movie was also interesting.
