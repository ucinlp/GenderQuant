Universal Groove is an opportunity to experience the pre-millennium underground party scene through the eyes of eight people who all tap into the Universal Groove - the moment when a party connects everyone to each other. Each character starts the night with a personal mission, but all leave the party changed by their interactions. The film uses an after-hours backdrop to draw eight characters together at one event - Groove. Each is pulled through a night of inspiration and escape through drugs.
