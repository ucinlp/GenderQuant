In the aftermath of the war in Bosnia, a Colonel convicted of war crimes has taken command of the military of the former Soviet republic of Muldonia. In a Hezbollah terrorist camp,a US Army private is being held hostage. His interrogator decides to terrorise him with a revolver. Meanwhile, an elite Special Forces team infiltrates the camp to rescue him. Eventually, they're spotted, and are forced to open fire on the terrorists. The team rescues the private and flee in a boat back to friendly territory.
