The film tells three different stories about women struggling for identity in Iran.{{Citation | last  Eric | title  Houston Chronicle | date http://www.chron.com/disp/story.mpl/ent/movies/906030.html | accessdate  http://web.archive.org/web/20080319122412/http://www.chron.com/disp/story.mpl/ent/movies/906030.html  |archivedate "OS">{{cite web}} Ahoo is a married woman who is taking part in a cycling race. Ahead of the rest, Ahoo is pursued by her husband on horseback. He tries to persuade her to stop cycling and come home, and then threatens her with divorce. When Ahoo defiantly continues cycling, her husband brings a mullah who divorces them. Later, tribal superiors and male family members come to stop Ahoo as she tries to escape the social constraints of being a woman.
