Professor Viswambaran is working in Viswa Jyothi Engineering College, Thodupuzha. Over-consciousness about beauty and glamour gives him a "naughty professor" image in the college. His wife, a former actress, had decided to discontinue her acting career to settle down with her husband. Viswambaran often feels overshadowed by his wife's fame and name. It was during this time Viswambharan's old nemesis Chacko a.k.a David and his wife Tessa come as their neighbours. The twist and turns in Viswambharan's married life form the crux of the story.
