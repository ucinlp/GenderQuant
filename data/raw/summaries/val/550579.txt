This movie is about a gay university student Ricky  and Mark . Ricky comes out to his family and friends, but his "fag hag" Olivia  doesn't seem to be convinced. Being rejected by his parents, Ricky moves in with his friend Mark. Mark in his heart is gay too, but he has not yet come out to anyone, including his girlfriend Mabel . The movie focuses on the process of Mark's trying to face his sexual orientation.
