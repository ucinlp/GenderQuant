NTR and Rajini are siblings separated during childhood. NTR lives with his mother  in a village while, Rajini works becomes a policeman. Meanwhile, Rajini is employed by Prabhakar Reddy to kill NTR. While attempting to kill NTR, a serious fight takes place and both come to find out they are siblings. In the end they both kill Prabhakar Reddy, who is the main villain and reunite with their mother, played by Anjali Devi. Radha Saluja and Subhashini play the love interests of N. T. Rama Rao and Rajinikanth respectively.
