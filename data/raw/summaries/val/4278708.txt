Suraj  and Lala  were good friends before falling prey to the vicious tricks of Sundar , so much so that Lala believes his wife is dead and Suraj is to blame for that. They become greatest enemies and create chaos in the city by way of gang wars. Asst. Commissioner of Police  has 2 criminal psychologists, KD , and Shalini  weave a plot to bring Rahul , the lively and spirited son of Suraj, close to Priya , the ever-so graceful and elegant daughter of Lala, hoping to bring the two sides together.
