At sea Oliver is captured by Spaniards and made a galley slave, but when he escapes to the Moors he becomes Sakr-el-Bahr, the scourge of Christendom. Learning of Rosamund's  impending marriage to his half brother, he kidnaps both of them, but to avoid the risk of giving her to Asad-ed-Din , the Basha of Algiers, he surrenders to a British ship.
