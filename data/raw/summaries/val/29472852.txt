Drunken Doc Medley and his daughter Angie support themselves by Angie's gambling skills as her father steers travelers to her card table. Angie is in the romantic thrall of tough guy Slinger, though Slinger's rough ways bother her. When her father sells his almost non-existent practice to an unwitting Easterner, Doctor Merritt, Angie sets out to repay the young doctor. But Slinger senses the attraction Angie feels for Merritt and decides to make things difficult for both of them, a decision that leads to robbery and murder.
