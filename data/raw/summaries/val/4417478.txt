In the fictional town of Putnam's Landing, Harry Bannerman  is slowly going insane because of his wife Grace , who insists on attending every civic committee meeting. When the government selects their town for their new missile base, Grace joins a committee to stop it. Harry is made the liaison for the military, and Grace's activities cause him no end of trouble. Added to the dilemma is Angela Hoffa , whose efforts to get Harry for herself lead to dizzying recriminations and misunderstandings.
