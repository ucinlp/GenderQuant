Shishira starts with a discussion among friends about the existence of god and ghosts. The film's hero Suraj is an atheist and says that the responsibility of leading a happy life rests with an individual; that gods or ghosts does not exist. He accepts a challenge with friends to spend a night at a dilapidated bungalow, situated in a remote place. At the bungalow, Suraj tries to entertain himself by reading an old diary. While he learns about the tragic death of three people from the diary. Suddenly, Suraj feels the presence of the dead victims, and gets terrified.
