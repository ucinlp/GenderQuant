The plot concerns two twins who adore soccer but whose team plays badly. They find Rudy to be the best soccer player ever. When their dad, who is their coach, has to go away on a business trip, the twins' mother takes over as the team's new coach. With hard work and perseverance - and the help of their new mechanic friend, Rudy - the team just might win the championship game. But soon the twins find out that Rudy is actually George Patrick Owens, a famous professional Scottish soccer player who's gone incognito. The film was filmed at Mason Park in Chatsworth, CA.
