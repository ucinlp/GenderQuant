A 47-year-old Christian man  is on the other side of an unwanted divorce. Searching for answers to ease the pain and make sense of his life, he meets a woman  at a divorce recovery group. The two forge a friendship and find they have a common bond: both have been thinking about their lost first loves. As he reminisces about his old high school girlfriend , he regrets he ever broke up with her. Now, 30 years later, he wants to see her again.
