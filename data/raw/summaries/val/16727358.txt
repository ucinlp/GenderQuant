The film is about four young compulsive gamblers wasting their lives on booze, broads and bookies. David , the heavy-drinking ladies man Mike , a degenerate gambler Cory the Jersey Jinx  Brett , the guy who tells every girl he loves her on the first date. When they find themselves in debt to a psychotic Christopher Walken obsessed hit man  they come up with a radical plan to get out of debt with a fixed game.
