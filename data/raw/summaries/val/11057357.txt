Kashinath  and Imli  are childhood sweethearts and everyone expects them to marry. Then comes rich, wealthy, and sickly Anjali Chopra  into Kashinath's life, and then everything changes. Anjali gives up on her sickiliness and becomes bold, thanks to Kashinath's care. Anjali falls in love with Kashinath, and boldly tells her dad, Manmohan Chopra , that she will only marry Kashinath. But her dad wants her to wed Ajit . Imli reveals her secret that she has heart disease, and if not treated, she will die. Kashinath, unaware of Anlali's affections must find the money to save his true love. What sacrifices will he have to make in order to achieve this?
