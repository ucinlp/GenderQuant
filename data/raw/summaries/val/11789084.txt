At the end of The English Civil War, Sir Thomas Fairfax  celebrates the Parliamentarian victory over the royalist Cavaliers with his colleague and fellow commander, Oliver Cromwell . Meanwhile, King Charles I , who has been captured and is held prisoner by the victors, tries to appeal to Fairfax's wife, Lady Anne . Soon, the trial of the king takes place, and Cromwell & Fairfax disagree over what to do, and from there, tensions rise.
