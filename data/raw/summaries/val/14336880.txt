After an awkward yet memorable encounter at a stag party, Evo  and Grace  thought they would never see each other again. Little did they know that Grace is actually the girlfriend of Evo's best friend, Mark . As the arrogant player that he is, Evo takes advantage of what happened at the party to make Grace agree to his demands.
