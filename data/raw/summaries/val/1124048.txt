Taxi 2 tells the story of a Japanese minister of defence visiting Marseille to view the city police's anti-gang tactics. During the visit, however, he is kidnapped by a group working for the Japanese yakuza. Young officer Emilien  is determined to rescue the minister and officer Petra  , who was also kidnapped, and restore the honour of his department. Once again, speed demon taxi-driver Daniel  is called upon to save the day with his high-speed driving skills.
