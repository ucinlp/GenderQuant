As the U.S. Subic Bay naval base's operations slowly wind down and naval manpower begins to dwindle, Commander Hamilton  relies on three U.S. Navy Seals to help keep the base secure. William Hawk , a longtime American sailor nearing the end of a tour of duty, is involved with a Filipina, Lisa Velasquez , a representative of the mayor's office in nearby Olongapo City. Lisa has to deal with the economic crisis that the base's closing will bring to her community, as well as her own personal problems brought on by Hawk's imminent departure and the strained relationship of her mother, Anna , and stepfather, Ed .
