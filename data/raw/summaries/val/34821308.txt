Living in modern-day Eastern Serbia, still fertile ground for various forms of superstition and prejudice, Zorica  is a village girl who carries a curse. Ever since her first boyfriend mysteriously drowned in the river, men around her are dying in most bizarre ways. The plot thickens when stubborn policeman Mane  who doesn't believe in 'village tales' tries to investigate whether Zorica is a serial killer or a girl looking for love.
