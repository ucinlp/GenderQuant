The hero Akash believes in high thinking. He has a lot of dreams… He is very serious for his studies because he wants to bring a bright future for his mother and sister. Akash meets his love megha at the college. But all his dreams come to a dark side after he enters college. Akash falls into the trap laid by Mafia don Bhalu Bhai and his brother King. And the story of “Mate Ta Love Helare” moves around how akash comes out of the trap and punish the don and how he gets his love. {{cite web}}
