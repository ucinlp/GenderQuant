Anand  and Munish  are two best friends. Anand is a honest police pfficer whereas Munish is the most famous lawyer of the city. Munish's sister Nisha is in love with Anand. There are series of rapes and murders in the film. Anand discovers that all the victim girls were connected with Munish. One girl Shabnam, miraculously escapes from the hands of Munish. Anand arrests Munish on the charges of rape and murder but Shabnam identifies Anand as the killer and the rapist.
