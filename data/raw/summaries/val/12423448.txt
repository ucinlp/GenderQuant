Sex, drugs, alcohol and suicidal tendencies. This ensemble driven dramedy revolves around an eccentric New Year's Eve Party, where the Party-Goers are oblivious to the true atmosphere of pain amid the celebration of "life". As off-beat party-goers spontaneously ring in the New Year at a Hollywood Hills home, the paths of four hopeless people intertwine. This film explores the celebration of life, resolutions, and new beginnings amongst the true underlying atmosphere of pain and turmoil.
