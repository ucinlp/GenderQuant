It tells the adventurous story of rubber grower Jim Frazer and his wife Liz, whose plantation is destroyed by terrorists in the Malayan Emergency. Despite facing problems from Communist terrorists and his own wife wishing to leave him, the family unites to defend their plantation from an attack at the film's climax. {{cite news}} The film's director later made Swiss Family Robinson for Disney. In addition to the end battle, the film also features a fight to the death between a cobra and a mongoose.
