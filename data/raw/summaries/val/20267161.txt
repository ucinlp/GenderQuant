{{Expand section}} Billy  defies his mother  and father  and makes a secret trip from Long Island to Philadelphia to sneak his grandfather  out of a nursing home and bring him home for the holidays. The convelescent home's inhabitants distract the staff so Billy and his confused Grandfather can make their escape. The cross path with a street gang whose leader  commands a puruit. They are given a hiding place by concerned homeless man Buzzard Bracken , but their sanctuary is only temporary, as the gang invades the homeless camp and capture Billy and Gramps. Buzzard and his homeless friends rescue the two and raise money by panhandling in order to get them on their way back to Long Island and a Christmas reunion.
