Subrahmanyam  is the youngest son of the family and a careless guy always going around girls and enjoying. His father always scolds him but his brother  who is a boxer, and Jaanu  dote him. Meanwhile he falls in love with lovely  and for this he keeps on telling lies to her that he is very rich. Jaanu in the middle truly loves subrahmanyam and gives him everything he asks her for thinking that he is doing these for her but he does it for lovely .The incidents that led subrahmanyam to change, the way he realised the true love of jaanu and the final twist of him becoming a boxing champion is the real plot to be seen.
