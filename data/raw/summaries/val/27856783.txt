The retired government official Purshottama Rao  borrows money to get his eldest daughter Kalindi  married to an old-man Lingaraju . He is a money lender and already married twice. Kalindi does not like this marriage and commits suicide before the marriage can take place. Lingaraju refuses to return the dowry money. Purshottama's second daughter Kamala  agrees to marry him. She drags her husband Lingaraju to court of justice.
