Premante Inthe is a remake of the Hindi movie Socha Na Tha. Veeru  has a crush on Lizi . But he isn't bold enough to get across his emotions to her. His rich family meanwhile wants him to marry another rich girl Pavani . But she is also not positively inclined towards marriage. So Veeru and Pavani hatch a plan and scupper the match. Therefore there is some friction between the families. Meanwhile, Veeru and Pavani strike a good friendship. And he also opens his heart to Lizi. Initially his family opposes the arrangement as she is a Christian. Eventually they agree to the marriage. But on the day of the marriage, Veeru realizes that he is actually in love with Pavani.
