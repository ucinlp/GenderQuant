The film's story is similar to fantasy films like Yamaleela, Yamudiki Mogudu, Yama Gola and Yamadonga. Bullet Raja  is a small time crook with a good heart. He bumps into Swetha  at a function and falls in love with her. But Swetha is already engaged to Harbour Babu , a powerful local goon. Harbour Babu hatches a plan to finish off Bullet Raja and he succeeds in his efforts. Bullet Raja heads to Yamalokam where he realises that his life has unjustly been terminated by Chitragupta  and so he picks up a fight with Yamadharma Raja ([[Prabhu . A helpless Yamadharma Raja decides to send him back to earth into the body of Home Minister Ravindra , who is killed by his associates Balram , Shantaram  and Pavitrananda . After Bullet Raja enters into Ravinder's body, he confronts his enemies and fulfills the wishes of his mother Parvathamma  and his followers.
