Anjali  is a beautiful and idealistic girl from Jhansi who in the middle of meetings, parties, and classes in college, is trying to find her one true love. Is it the oddball Ginger? The professionally ambitious yet unclassy Steve? Or the mysterious guy, Rahul , who she has only seen in her dreams? Will Anjali find her soulmate, the perfect man for her? Meeting Se Meeting Tak is a witty and sexy take on what it takes to find true love.
