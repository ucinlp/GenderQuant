The story is told in five acts.{{cite news}} In 1915, German troops led by Captain von Epstein capture the peaceful town of Loos and start committing atrocities on the local population. Von Epstein lusts after a young peasant girl, Emilienne Moreau , but she escapes to the Allied lines. Inspired by the vision of Joan of Arc , she helps encourage the Allied troops in an attack to retake the town. She falls for a French dispatch rider  who is captured by the Germans and takes part in shooting German officers who are sniping on the Red Cross. Emilienne manages to engineer her lover's escape and winds up married to them. She is also awarded a military cross.{{cite news}}{{cite news}}
