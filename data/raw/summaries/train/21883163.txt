A popular actress  and a rock star  come to a small Canadian town with two competing hotels next door to each other. The rock star is escorted into one of the hotels and the actress checks into the other. Daniel , the owner of one of the two hotels, is trying to stay afloat. The other hotel owner, Mark , is trying to steal away Daniel's business and his fiancee . The paparazzi arrives in town and makes everybody wonder, who is sleeping with who?
