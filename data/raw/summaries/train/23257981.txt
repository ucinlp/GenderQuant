The film details the events surrounding the death of Las Vegas casino owner Ted Binion.<ref namehttp://www.dvdtalk.com/reviews/37436/sex-lies-in-sin-city-the-ted-binion-story/|titleMavis|first2009-04-28|publisher2009-06-17}} It follows the ensuing investigation of Binion's life and the people in it, such as Binion's sister Becky, his girlfriend Sandy Murphy, and Sandy's lover Rick Tabish.
