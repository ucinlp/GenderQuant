Srikanth is a marriage broker. He takes care of everything related to wedding from searching alliance to sending bride to her in-law's house. His brother Ravi Prakash is a sincere police officer. Doctor  and owner of a super specialty hospital who trades with the limbs of patients, fixes his daughter, Swathi's  with an M.P.'s  son chatrapathi . Srikanth is the matchmaker for that wedding. But, the girl receives Srikanth's photo by mistake. She starts to consider Srikanth as her fiance! M.P. attacks on Ravi Prakash who collects all the evidence against his felonies, and seizes him. Brahmanandam, Ali, Venu Madhav and Krishna Bhagavan, the victims of ruthless doctor become demons!! How those spirits take vengeance on doctor, how Srikanth could save his brother, does Swathi marry Srikanth are the rest of the stuff to be watched on screen.
