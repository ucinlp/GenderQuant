Dharti is family drama set in Punjab with the backdrop of Punjab politics. Baljit Singh Wadala  is the head of the ruling party in Punjab and has two sons. The elder son Vikramjit Singh Wadala  is following his father's footsteps and is a prominent political leader, while the younger son Jasdeep Singh Wadala  is a squadron leader in the Indian Air Force. The younger son has a conflict with his father as he had left his father's legacy to join the politics.
