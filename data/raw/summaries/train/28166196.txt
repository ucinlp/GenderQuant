When famed comedy rock duo 16 Buttons of Justice is informed of the tremendous failure that is the 16Buttons of Justice TV Show, the duo must come up with a plan to send ratings through the roof, before the show is canceled. Soon after hearing that Ready Espanosa, father to 16 Buttons of Justice front man Raccoon X, and a world famous explorer have gone missing, the duo decides to document their own rescue attempt to save the greatest adventurer of all time.
