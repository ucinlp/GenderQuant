An unorthodox Garda  named Boyle  joins forces with a straight-laced FBI agent  named Everett  to take on an international drug smuggling gang in Ireland's Connemara Gaeltacht. A murder which appears to be the work of an occult serial killer is the first incident, the relevance of which is then revealed by Boyle in a briefing by an FBI agent, sent to liaise with the Gardai. A web of bribery, blackmail and killings ensues. Boyle and Everett form an unlikely alliance and bring about a bloody climax.
