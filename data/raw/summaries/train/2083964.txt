Newly released from a mental hospital, Jessica , accompanied by her husband  and a friend , retreats to a Victorian farmhouse in an isolated part of rural Connecticut. The trio finds Emily , an enigmatic hippie, living in the house. Almost immediately, Jessica feels a resurgence of her madness. Increasing evidence suggests that Emily and all the other inhabitants of the island are ghosts and/or vampires pursuing Jessica and her companions. However, viewers are never sure whether the bizarre occurrences are real or only in Jessica's mind.
