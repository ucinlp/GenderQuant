Cyril  and Waldo , who are British and American, respectively, have both returned to France in search of the same woman  with whom they each had a rendezvous in 1944 . Cyril is accompanied by fellow veteran Amos , while Waldo has his petty daughter  and her henpecked husband  in tow. The two groups encounter one another, and after some conflict find common ground in old sorrows. Along the way they meet the recently widowed Lisa , who has come in search of her brother's grave.
