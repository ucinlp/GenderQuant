The story is centered in the life of three young people. Gonza, singer of a rock band, Sixto, son of a millionaire family and Piernitas, daughter of a civil employee of the government. They party regularly at Gay Night Club the Ship. There they intercross with the outlandish countess Pavlova (Imanol Arias, who is a transvestite, Laureano, the yuppie who handles the premises, and Arteche, manager of the band. All arrive at the ship convinced that this is their night. Some will discover that nothing can change their destiny.
