Gowri  is a motorcycle mechanic living in the Dhoolpet area of Hyderabad. Swetha  is a college student who is the daughter of media baron Chandra Sekhar . Gowri and Swetha fall in love with each other to the disapproval of Chandra Sekhar. He seeks the help of a mafia leader Sarkar  to separate Gowri and Swetha. The rest of the story is all about how Gowri overcomes Swetha's father and Sarkar to gain Swetha's hand in marriage.
