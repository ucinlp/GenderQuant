Terry Cuff  is a bartender who yearns to raise a family. When she marries Bobby Woodkin  she finds herself one step closer to her dream. However, Bobby has had a vasectomy , a con artist and unbeknownst to Terry, kills young Dana McThomas and steals her 3 month old daughter, Gail so he and Terry can have a child of their own. Bobby fakes a law firm and lawyer so his plan does not fail and that he and Terry can keep little "Angela". Later, Terry discovers what Bobby has done and returns baby Gail to her father, Andy. Bobby is arrested and sentenced to life in prison for Dana's murder and Gail's kidnapping.
