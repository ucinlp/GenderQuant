From the depths of the universe Earth can hear the radio signals of intelligent beings from a planet of the star system Shedar . A project is set up, proposed by the young inventor Vitya Sereda, to send a spaceship to reach the planet - but the flight will last for decades, so the crew of the spaceship "Dawn " , is to be recruited from teenage students. The project is all carefully thought out but student Fyodor Lobanov stows away aboard the starship and unwittingly causes it to transend the speed of light and so reaching its target 27 years ahead of schedule...
