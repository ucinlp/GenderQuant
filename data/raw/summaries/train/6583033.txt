Yadagiri  is a handicapped mafia leader in Hyderabad and KR  is a rich business man. Bhagyamati is daughter of Yadagiri. Bobby is son of KR. The story of this film deals with what happens when these two kids fall in love with each other. Bobby meets Bhagyamati in a club and falls in love with her. They later find put that their fathers are arch rivals. Bhagyamati's mother was killed in an accident involving their fathers. Knowing this Bobby and Bhagyamati elope. But due to unavoidable circumstances they come back and are injured in a bomb blast. Then their fathers apologize and make up and in the end bobby and Bhagyamati are married.
