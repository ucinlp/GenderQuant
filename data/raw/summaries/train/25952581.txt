Varghese  is a Kalari master. Shyam  is his favorite disciple. He comes to his master declaring his love for Maya . He says that her parents have kept her under house arrest and he needs his master's help to rescue her. Varghese kidnaps Maya and brings her to Shyam but Shyam tries to kill her. Varghese understands that Shyam is not her lover and she is the only witness in another homicide which involves Shyam. But Maya tells Varghese that Shyam is innocent. Varghese tries to help both of them.
