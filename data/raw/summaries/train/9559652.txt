Santosh Rai Pathaje has undertaken the topic of heart transplantation and has weaved around it a love story. This kind of a story was sure to go well with the average Kannada film audience. Keeping this in mind, it can be surely be appreciated as class film. He also tries to show the reasons behind marital dischord amongst urban couples and also proposes a solution of unconditional love and belief in one's partner is required to solve these marital problems.
