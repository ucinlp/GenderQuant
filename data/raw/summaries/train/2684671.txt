Fourteen-year-old Nadav is hopelessly in love with his aunt Nina , who has recently lost her husband . He is caught between the two worlds of his divorced parents: his mother  is a high-strung fashionista, while his father  has recently become devoutly Orthodox and withdrawn from the family in order to join a group of Hassidic Jews who tour Tel Aviv in a van, blasting the word of God through loudspeakers.
