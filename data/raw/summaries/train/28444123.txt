Parvathy  is a girl who lost her mother at birth. She is brought up along with her friend Lekshmi  by the latter's mother in their Agraharam. Both of them join a Music College, where Parvathy falls in love with Devanand , her lecturer. One day Devananth’s grandfather decides to get in touch with Agraharam in order to propose a marriage between Parvathy and Devananth. When he reaches Lakshmi’s house, Lekshmi's mother cleverly fixes the marriage of Devananth with Lekshmi instead of Parvathy. Understanding the situation, Parvathy leaves Agraharam. Many years later Lakshmi happens to meet Parvathy in a pilgrim center.
