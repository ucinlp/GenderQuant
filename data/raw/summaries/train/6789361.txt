El Hadj Abdoukader Beye, a Senegalese businessman, marries for the third time, thereby demonstrating his social and economic success. But on the wedding night he discovers that he is incapable of consummating the marriage; he has become impotent. At the beginning, he suspects his two wives, without realizing that he walks by the true guilty party every day. The film criticizes the African leaders' attitude after Independence, underlining their greed and their inability to step away from foreign influences.FCAT
