The story is about a victim of circumstances becoming an underworld functionary. Sathya , is a notorious Underworld Don who has conquered the Bangalore Mafia. He comes across Divya , a film actress, who is exploited by her own father  and a notorious Mumbai underworld kingpin . Sathya rescues the damsel in distress and the two fall in love. Then follows an inevitable misunderstanding. The story approaches its climax when the father becomes the target of the underworld kingpin.
