Having put aside their differences at the end of the previous film, Grandmaster Cash and Dr. Feekinstein have grown to prominence on the Irish music scene. While promoting his debut novel, Grandmaster Cash and the Lazerwolvez, Cash is approached by Cian 'Hollywood' Norris, a Cork native working as an agent in London. Inviting Feekinstein to join him, and enlisting Seamus Kelly to again document their adventures, Cash attempts to make it as an international hip-hop artist.{{cite web}}
