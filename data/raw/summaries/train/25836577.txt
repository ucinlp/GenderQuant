Nallathambi  a simple rural man inherits an estate from his deceased Zamindar relative. The Zamindar's daughter Pushpa  falls in love with Nallathambi. However he loves another girl . Bhoopathy  the estate manager has his eye on the wealth and tries to cheat Nallathambi out of it. He goads Pushpa into filing a suit that Nallathambi is mentally insane. In the end Nallathambi defeats Bhoopathy's machinations.
