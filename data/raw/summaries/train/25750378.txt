Joshua Mason  is a troubled 15-year-old who is abandoned by his mother and loves to paint, and is quite good at it. But he mimics other people's paintings. One day an artist finds him painting one of his paintings and chases him. He gets away but now has no money for food. He sneaks into a house and finds a room with paintings and finishes one perfectly. The man who owns the house, Everly, finds him and calls the police but then finds the painting and is amazed and takes Josh under his wing. Josh finds out Everly just copies works. Now he has to make a decision, help him, or hurt him.
