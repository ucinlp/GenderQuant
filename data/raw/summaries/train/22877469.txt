Cusack plays Mike Fletcher, a New York detective working on the streets of Buffalo, New York. He’s a policeman who doesn’t let go until he gets his man. Mike and his partner are on the hunt for a serial killer who is killing the local prostitutes. Then one day his own daughter is taken by the killer who thinks she is a ‘working’ girl. Mike has even more reason to track down and capture this killer before his daughter is killed. Forgetting all his work ethics, his only goal is to rescue his daughter and he doesn’t care what he has to do to achieve it.
