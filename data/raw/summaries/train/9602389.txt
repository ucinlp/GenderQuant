Robin  encourages her roommate Phoebe , an ambitious 21-year-old university student, to find a boyfriend. Phoebe does not manage to find a love interest among her fellow-university students, but instead takes interest in a 14-year-old boy named Frazer . Frazer's crazy next door neighbour Suzanna  is madly in love with him, and his younger quirky sister Bugs  provides Frazer with insightful advice on his love problems.
