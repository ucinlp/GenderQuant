The animated film is about Little Jack , a young fox who spends his time enjoying a wonderful life in the forest with his loving family. During the film, Little Jack's world changes drastically when his father, Jack, is captured and forced to join the circus.<ref name http://www.fantasticfilmsinternational.com/new_detailed.html#fox|titleFantastic Films International, LLC}} The film's villain, a shrewd circus owner named Anna Conda , desperately wants bigger and better performances. This leads her to kidnap many forest animals, including Little Jack's father. With the help of her magician husband, The Ringmaster , Anna is able to hypnotize the animals into performing in her shows.
