The story opens with Chakravarthy, a millionaire who has everything in his life. He is a successful businessman. He has achieved everything in his life but he doesn't have a successor to enjoy his wealth. He breaks up his relation with his wife suspecting her to have an affair with his colleague where he was working 20 years ago. During the breakup period his wife was a pregnant. He repents for losing her and his child. One fine day three people enter into his life saying that they are his children. The remaining story is how chakravarthy finds out his child from the three with the help of nagayya, his personal assistant.
