Set in Covington, Ohio, The People Next Door stars Jeffrey Jones, previously regarded as a character actor, as cartoonist Walter Kellogg, a man whose imagination was so vivid that many of the things which he imagined materialized immediately. His wife Abigail was portrayed by Saturday Night Live alumna Mary Gross. They had two children, and Abigail's sister "Cissy", portrayed by Christina Pickles, was also a regular, as was a meddlesome mailman, Thurman Fippa . The character Debbie was played by Jackie Swanson. The People Next Door was nevertheless a Nielsen ratings disaster and was cancelled after only five episodes had aired.
