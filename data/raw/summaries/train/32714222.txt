Socialite but penniless orphan Lily Bart is living with her wealthy aunt who insists that she takes upon herself a rich husband. Balking at this idea and remaining faithful to her impecunious sweetheart Lawrence Seldon , Lily is desirous of maintaining her luxurious lifestyle: she acceptes the financial "favors" of some married millionaires but refuses to surrender her virtue in return - until she discovers that her sainted Seldon has been fooling around with another man's wife.
