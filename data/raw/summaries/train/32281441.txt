Ami Shubhash Bolchi is the story Netaji Subhas Chandra Bose {{cite web}}{{cite web}} who comes into the life of struggling Debabrata  and through him, Netaji helps to awaken the Bengalis from "Sleep" and acts as a super hero. This movie is the Bengali version of Manjrekars earlier original Marathi film along the same lines called Me Shivajiraje Bhosale Boltoy.
