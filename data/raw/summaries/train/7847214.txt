Rosa Lynn an elderly woman, sends her daughter Loretta and her children to live with her brother-in-law in Mississippi. Loretta is an addict and does not want to go especially since uncle Earl lives in the dry and rural part of Mississippi. Uncle Earl has his hands already full with a business he is pressured with and a wife who suffers from Alzheimer's disease. Yet he has Loretta help him in his restaurant and the family begins to find strength in their roots, and start to rebuild their lives.
