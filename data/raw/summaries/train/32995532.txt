A convict, Dan Farrel , escapes from Van Dieman's Land and throws himself on a mercy of a farming couple, Mary  and Donald Cameron . The years pass and Dan becomes a school teacher. He marries and they have a daughter, Dierdre , but his wife dies. Dierdre grows up and agrees to marry a local pub keeper, McNab , to stop him revealing that Dan is a convict. McNab still goes to the police and Dan is arrested. Dierdre accidentally kills McNab.
