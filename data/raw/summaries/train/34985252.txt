Derelict is set in 2025 as the Water Wars ravage Europe. The film begins with a news report from BBC World's journalist Owen Thomas. Reporting outside the memorial service commemorating the 5th year of England's involvement in the water wars. A notorious prisoner-of-war is on the run after his escape comes at a great price. Pursued by trained killers he seeks refuge within London’s ghettoes and takes an old man hostage in a last ditch attempt at freedom. But with both national security and lives on the line, this high stakes hostage negotiation will be decided by one man’s past.
