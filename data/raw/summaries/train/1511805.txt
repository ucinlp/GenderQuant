The film consists of four stories in which human characters struggle to connect in a world of robot babies and android office workers. The stories include: "My Robot Baby," in which a couple  must care for a robot baby before adopting a human child; "The Robot Fixer," in which a mother  tries to connect with her dying son by completing his toy robot collection; "Machine Love," in which an office worker android  learns that he, too, needs love; and "Clay," in which an old sculptor  must choose between natural death and digital immortality.
