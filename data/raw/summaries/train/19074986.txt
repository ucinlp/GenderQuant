I.S.A.A.C. , played by former UFC Middleweight Champion Rich Franklin, is the first prototype of a secret "human weapon" program. Once a death-row inmate, he is now a genetically reconstructed, highly trained, deadly assassin whose body and mind have been modified to become a physically regenerative, intellectually superior human being. While on the run, I.S.A.A.C. takes Deputy Lindsay Rearden  hostage. Their lives are in jeopardy from military agents being led by robotics engineer Simon Hart . Deaputy Reardon and I.S.A.A.C. work together to expose the military group constructing the super beings.
