Lorna Blake,  is a widow with two daughters. She augments her slender income by using her children to extort money - visiting the houses of the rich to tell a pathetic story and beg for help. And Lorna makes a rich capture when Sir Halmar Bernard, , proposes to her. She tells him that she has only one daughter, Molly . When her other daughter, Jay , is arrested for forging a cheque, she refuses to help her.
