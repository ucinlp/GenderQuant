Vamsee  belongs to a disorganised but big family which includes a drunkard father , a bothersome mother , a gambler brother , a sister , a lazy brother in law  and a patient sister in law  who is the only saving grace in the family. Vamsee tries to keep the family going with the help of the sister in law but decides to stay as bachelor after getting disgusted with his own family. But when he meets Vasanthi  who is quite different from all his family members he falls in love. The girl too gives her nod very soon. But her grandfather Janakiramaiah  puts a condition for the marriage. He wants to stay with Vamsee, the aftermath of these events, forms the rest of the story.
