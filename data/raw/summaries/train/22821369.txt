The story revolves around cricket where a new star Sunny  has been included in the team in place of another famous star Ronny . All this creates hatred in the heart of Ronny against Sunny, although Sunny respects him. Ronny decides to take revenge on Sunny; meanwhile a terrorist decides to put a bomb in the field where a match is to be organised between India and Australia. DIG Vikram Singh  comes to know about the plan of the terrorists and his brother and goes on to prevent the attack, saving thousands of lives. Sunny also becomes a star by playing a match-winning stroke for the country.
