Florence "Chicklet" Forrest , a Gidget-like character, experiences inexplicable blackouts, and fears that she might be the one responsible for a series of mysterious deaths in her beach-side town. The deaths are investigated by Captain Monica Stark , who also suspects Chicklet's mother , Chicklet's best friend Berdine , surfing guru The Great Kanaka  and B-movie actress Bettina Barnes . Florence is determined to learn to surf, and earns the nickname "Chicklet" from the surfer guys, all the while displaying multiple personalities.
