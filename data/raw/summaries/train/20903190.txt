Vishal  and Aruna  are in love. Aruna learns that she is going to become blind. She don't want to be burden on Vishal who is already taking care of his handicapped mother. So Aruna marries her old age employer . The plot further thickens when the young daughter of Ashok Kumar falls in love with Vishal. The question "Will Vishal, who does not know the reasons for Aruna's defection, use this to take revenge on Aruna?" forms the rest of the story.
