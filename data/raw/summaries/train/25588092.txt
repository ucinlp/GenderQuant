Alice  meets the White Rabbit , the bad-tempered Cook  and the Duchess . She joins a mad tea-party with the Mad Hatter , the March Hare  and the Dormouse , while the Cheshire Cat  leaves his grin behind. The Caterpillar  becomes annoyed with her, and the Queen of Hearts  threatens to cut off her head.
