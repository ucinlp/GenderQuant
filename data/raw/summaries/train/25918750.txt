Maddy is the mother of ten-year-old twins Todd and Terry, at age 12 Terry commits a horrifying axe murder but Todd is set up by his brother. Todd gets arrested and sent to a mental institution. Ten years later, Maddy is about to get married again and plans to make the announcement on Thanksgiving Day. She receives a phone call saying that Todd has escaped, and the thrilled Terry begins to make plans to begin another killing spree for which Todd would almost certainly be blamed for.
