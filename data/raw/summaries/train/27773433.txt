Maxim Sarafanov  has just been fired from the orchestra he played clarinet in, his son Nikita  is in love with his schoolteacher Susan  and his daughter Lolita  is getting married to a US pilot Greg  and leaving home to go to Texas. But things get worse when a small time car thief Bo  looking for a hideout from the police tells Maxim that he is his son from Maxim's old girlfriend towards whom he still has feelings.
