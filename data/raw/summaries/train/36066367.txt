Freak Dance is a musical, dance comedy where rich girl Cocolonia  must escape her uptight mother  who won't let her dance. So Cocolonia hits the streets to join a dance crew who respond to every challenge with a dance. With the help of dance crew leader Funky Bunch , who dreams of being the world's greatest dancer. The crew must save the Fantaseez Community Center from the evil "Building Inspector General"  and the gang-banger dancers before it's too late. Will the Fantaseez Crew be able to save their home? Only love, and the Freak Dance can save them now.
