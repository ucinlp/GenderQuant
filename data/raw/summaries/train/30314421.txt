{{expand section}} In ancient China, a poor shoe repair man Wu Di  lives with his mother  and is obsessed with martial-arts picture books. Wu Di repairs the shoe of the swordswoman Yuelou  and later helps save her in a fight despite having no martial-arts training. She thanks him and says she can be found on Qin Mountain if she is needed. Yuelou is secretly a princess who is due to marry the emperor  but escape after setting the palace on fire.
