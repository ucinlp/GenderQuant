Gambling City's story centers on the exploits of master poker player and card sharp Luca Altieri ; Altieri begins working for underground illegal gambling kingpin "The President"{{cite web}} after running a scam at a high-stakes poker table in The President's casino; soon after entering The President's employ, however, Altieri enters into a violent competition with The President's hot-tempered, self-entitled son and heir, Corrado , for the hand of Corrado's girlfriend, Maria Luisa .{{cite news}}
