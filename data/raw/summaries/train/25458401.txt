Seung-young is a new recruit who finds that his commanding officer, Sergeant Tae-jung is an old school friend. Tae-jung looks after Seung-young, and tries to help the stubborn and contrary youngster adjust to the strict hierarchies and harshness of military life. As time passes, Seung-young’s resistance wears down, and he finds himself understanding, and even becoming more like the superior officers he previously struggled against. Matters come to a head when he is given command of Ji-hoon, a slovenly newcomer whose constant incompetence tests Seung-young’s patience, and eventually forces him to act.{{cite web}}
