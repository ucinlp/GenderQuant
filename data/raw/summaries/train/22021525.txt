Chinku is a young impish kid who lives with his greedy uncle. His life changes when he finds an enchanted talking book at a curio stall. It transports him to the magical land of Alibaba and 41 thieves. Chinku also makes a diabolic and dangerous enemy - Abu Hasan - the wicked and brutal leader of the pack. As good crosses paths with evil, characters from the real world  and the fantasy world of Alibaba cross over, leading to an explosion of fun, action of fantasy.
