After World War I, French infantry officer Capitaine Conan and the men under his command find it difficult to stay in Bucharest, mobilized yet ordered not to fight. While being tired of guard duty and state dinners they are subject to attacks from Romanian Bolsheviks but mustn't take pre-emptive action.
