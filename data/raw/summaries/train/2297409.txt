Capt. Patterson  and his space crew (Dave Willock, Patrick Waltz, and [[Paul Birch  crash land on Venus and are captured. They learn the planet is under the dictatorship of cruel Queen Yllana , a masked woman who has banished men from the planet. In the palace, the astronauts are aided by a beautiful courtier named Talleah  and her friends . The women long for the love of men again and plot to overthrow the evil Queen. When Patterson has the opportunity to remove the Queen's mask, he discovers she has been horribly disfigured by radiation burns caused by men and their wars. In a fury, the Queen decides to destroy Earth and its warlike peoples but she dies in the attempt. The Venusians are free again to enjoy the love of men.
