{{Expand section}} Mary is a young woman happily married to Nikos. However, her life is turned upside down when her best friend, Lilly, borrows Mary's car to pursue an extramarital fling, only to get involved in a car accident. Mary is drawn to the court, where she has to decide whether to refrain from exposing Lilly's infidelities, or sacrifice Lilly's confidentiality and save her own marriage in the process...
