16-year old Candy Philips  is brutally abducted and buried alive in the hills of Southern California by a trio of amateur criminals hoping for a hasty ransom exchange. When Candy's father  doesn't show at their agreed rendezvous, the threesome—sultry blonde Jessie , her sadistic brother Alan  and maladjusted Army vet Eddy —begin to grow anxious. Paranoia sets in; the kidnappers fear their meticulous plan is crumbling and begin a downward spiral into depravity, mutilation and cold-blooded murder—with Candy helplessly trapped in the middle.
