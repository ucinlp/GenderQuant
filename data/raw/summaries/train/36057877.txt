There is a war between Mastan Bhai's gang and Naaga's gang. Sena  is a local rowdy who works for Mastan Bhai . Naaga , the former right-hand of Mastan Bhai, tries to kill him. Sena has a little brother called Vikram  who hates rowdyism. Vikram loves Janu, sister of a police officer, Sathya. Sathya is killed by Naaga's henchmen and Vikram wants to punish the culprit.
