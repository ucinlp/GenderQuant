The central character Ghooli  is an uneducated, rude youngster, with whom a girl named Ramya  accidentally falls in love. Ramya continues to keep in touch with Ghooli despite opposition from her family and although Ghooli tries to keep Ramya at a distance, he finds he has also has fallen in love with her. They marry but separate shortly afterwards. Later Ghooli hears shocking news about the apparent death of Ramya, however she is not dead but in a mental asylum due to severe brain damage.
