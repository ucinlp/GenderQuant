Detective Jack Verdon  is investigating a series of brutal murders when he realizes that every one is a woman he has slept with or had a relationship with in the past. Verdon is taken off the case by FBI Agent Vukovich  and suspended by his Captain . Verdon is forced to work outside the law and confront his past to catch the killer , who is extracting details of Verdon's other lovers - and subsequent victims - from each victim.
