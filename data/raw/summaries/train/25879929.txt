A romantic musical comedy about a young man who always dreamt of coaching a soccer  team. Hima  and his ill sister  were left orphans when their parents were killed in a suspicious car crash and he now works as a school bus driver to support them. At work he is attentive to the children, always making sure they are well taken care of, garnering not only the love of the kids but also a special teacher . But a mysterious stranger has the key to what really happened to Hima. Will evil win, or will Hima overcome his circumstances and save both his sister and his love?
