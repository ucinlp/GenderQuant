The film focuses on a young African American who continues his family’s tradition of military service when he is drafted into the United States Army during World War II. Despite complications that arise during his basic training, including his jealousy following his girlfriend's flirtatious attention to his sergeant, the young soldier becomes a hero when he locates Japanese saboteurs operating a radio station outside of his military base.“Making Movies Black” by Thomas Cripps, Google BooksVideography of Vintage Black Film Collection at Duke University“The Movies, Race, and Ethnicity: African Americans,” University of California Berkeley
