The story is about Prem Kishan  coming to the hills of Kodaikkanal on work, meeting and falling in love with Prabha  whom he knows little about. Later he realises that she is married when her husband, Narendran  comes into the picture. Mohanlal plays the antagonist in this film, one of the many negative characters he has played in his career. The film ends on a sad note, with Prem Kishan committing suicide after killing Narendran, saddened by the loss of Prabha, who was killed by Narendran.
