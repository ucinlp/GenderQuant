The story follows Tim, Chris and Rand who are campus roommates. After hearing of a school policy, which says that anyone whose roommate commits suicide gets an automatic 4.0 GPA , Tim and Chris plot to kill Rand and make it look like suicide. After the suicide, Rand's girlfriend Natalie  is distraught and commits suicide as well. The story takes several plot twists as each persons motives come to light and the truth is revealed. The film was screened at the Sundance Film Festival but then went directly to video.{{Citation needed}}
