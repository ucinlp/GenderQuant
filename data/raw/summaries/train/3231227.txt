Dane Clark plays Danny Hawkins, the son of a murderer who was hanged for his crimes. Haunted by his father's past, the young man is tormented by the young people of the small southern town in which he lives. Hawkins' only friend is Gilly Johnson , a girl who is quickly falling in love with him. When Hawkins kills a bully in self-defense, he fears the same fate as his father. When the dead body is found and Sheriff Clem Otis  starts closing in, Danny becomes crazed. He jumps off a Ferris wheel and nearly strangles a harmless mute  who found Hawkins' pocket knife near the body. While hiding out in the swamps, Hawkins visits his Grandma  who tells him the truth about his father's crime. Hawkins realizes he's not tainted by "bad blood" and turns himself in to the police.
