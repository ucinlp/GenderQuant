An aspiring beauty queen Danielle Stevens  and her overbearing mother Cathy Stevens  may have resorted to murder to ensure a win in an upcoming pageant. After the murder of a beauty queen, an investigation reveals the suspects to be the former lover Riley Baxter's stepmother Patrice, rival contestant Danielle, and Danielle's highly ambitious mother Cathy. At the end, it turned out, that the murderer was Danielle Stevens, who was found guilty by the jury and sentenced lifelong imprisonment. In 1997 at the time of making the film, she was already serving her prison sentence.
