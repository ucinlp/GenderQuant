Laila, daughter of the wealthy Mourad Pasha, can't pass her exams in the Arabic language, and seeks the help of a tutor. Marzouk Afandy, who works for the Pasha, brings Mr. Hamam to help her with her studies. Hamam falls in love with Laila, but realises she will be better of with Wahid. The movie takes place in Egypt's beautiful cities.
