Mohan Kumar, an honest manager who works in the shipping company. J. K., Balwant and Bhandari works in the same company. They plan to smuggle goods. So they murder Company owner Dinesh Verma  and implicate Mohan Kumar in his murder. Mohan Kumar get arrested and imprisoned, and have him watch helplessly as his wife is killed, and son Ravi go missing. Coming-out, after 14 years, his only goal in life is to avenge his humiliation and wrongful arrest at the hands of the trio. With the help of R.D. Puri, he establishes himself again, and starts the process of his revenge.
