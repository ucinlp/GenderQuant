One day, radio DJ Dong-hyun  receives an anonymous package containing a Velvet Underground record. Dong-hyun hopes that the record was sent by his former lover. He decides to play the song "Pale Blue Eyes" off of that record. At the same time, a home shopping telemarketer, Su-heon  listens to the radio program while driving her car. The next day, Su-heon makes a request through the internet for Dong-hyun to play the song again. Dong-hyun then contacts Su-heon, hoping she is his former girlfriend or someone he knows ...
