This film is a compilation of scenes from eighteen films that Abbott and Costello made for Universal Pictures between 1941 and 1955. Comedian Jack E. Leonard provides the narration for the film, which incorporates scenes from the following films: *Abbott and Costello Go to Mars *Abbott and Costello in the Foreign Legion *Abbott and Costello Meet Frankenstein *Abbott and Costello Meet the Keystone Kops *Abbott and Costello Meet the Mummy *Buck Privates *Buck Privates Come Home *Comin' Round The Mountain *Hit The Ice *In Society' *In The Navy *The Wistful Widow of Wagon Gap *Little Giant *Lost in Alaska *Mexican Hayride *The Naughty Nineties *Ride 'Em Cowboy *Who Done It?<ref name | titleJim Mulholland | publisher254, 299 | year}}
