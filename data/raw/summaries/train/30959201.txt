A rich widow Sulakshana Devi  lives with her son Ravi  and care-taker Balwant . One day, she receives the news that her son has died in a car accident. Meanwhile, a lady named Kamini approaches her and informs her that she is carrying her dead son Ravi's child. Sulakshana does not believe Kamini and turns her away. Sulakshana later learns from Ravi's friend Sanjay Gupta  that Kamini is indeed the mother of Ravi's child. Sulakshana tries her best to look for Kamini and get her home, but to no avail. Years later, Sulakshana comes across a young man Arun  who retrieves her purse from a thief. Happy with his honesty, Sulakshana offers Arun a job. Hardly does she know that Arun is in fact a crook with his eyes and heart set on her multi-crore property. While Arun gets busy hatching plans to seize her property Seema  and her uncle played by Anupam Kher enter the scene. Then follows a series of comic events and a love triangle between Arun, Seema and Tara Jaisingh . Turns out that Ravi's death was not an accident in the first place. What is the truth that awaits Sulakshana?
