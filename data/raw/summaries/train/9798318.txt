Ramu  is a CBI officer who comes to investigate a case. Sandhya is a daughter of a scientist who invents a formula. Ranjeet  kidnaps the scientists and blackmails Sandhya, He uses Sandhya for his needs, and sends Sandhya to gather information about Ramu. Ranjeet also marries Ramu's sister to protect himself from Ramu. During this process Ramu and Sandhya falls in love and Ramu comes to know the truth about Sandhya.
