Raghu  is a government clerk lives happily with his wife Manjula  and two children. His mother Venkamma , sister Kamakshi  and her husband  lives on his salary. Venkamma appropriates his earnings and Kamakshi plots to have Manujala blamed for all that goes wrong in the household. Raghu unable to face the problems and abandons his family. Manjula had to work as housemaid and children to beg on the streets. His brother Venu , lives in the village. He comes to the city and traces Raghu, and finds him a job. He was teased by Kamala  and fell in love. He opens up the secret agenda of his mother and sister and finally joining the family happily.
