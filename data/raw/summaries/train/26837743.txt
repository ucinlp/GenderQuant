The brothers Antonio  and Peppino  Caponi are boorish landowners living in southern Italy. Antonio is lavish and steals his stingy brother's money. Gianni , their sister Lucia 's son, is studying medicine in Naples, when he falls in love with Marisa ([[Dorian Gray , a revue dancer, and follows her to Milan. The news is broken to the family with an anonymous letter, and the three brothers travel to Milan in an attempt to stop the relationship, which they consider dangerous. Antonio and Peppino try to bribe Marisa away from Gianni but he woos her back by moving her to tears with the song "Malafemmina", and Lucia realizes that she is a good girl. In the end, she leaves the revue world, moves to their village and marries Gianni.
