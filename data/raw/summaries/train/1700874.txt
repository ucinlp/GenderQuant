Poland has been invaded by Nazi Germany. Lotna, a beautiful mare that belonged to a wealthy nobleman, is given to Captain Chodakiewicz , the commander of a Polish Cavalry squadron, and immediately becomes a bone of contention for everyone in the unit. Lieutenant Wodnicki , Cadet Grabowski  and Sergeant Major Laton  jealously scheme among themselves to get their hands on the horse. However, the war takes the lives of Captain Chodakiewicz and Cadet Grabowski, and Lotna falls is passed to Wodnicki. Laton feels he should get the animal and so he steals Lotna and flees amidst the abandoned supply wagons and equipment of the retreating Polish Army.
