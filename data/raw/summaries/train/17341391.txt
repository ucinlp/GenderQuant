The Pink Panther begins to get irate when his neighbor, Harry borrows his hedge clippers and lawn mower without returning them. An off screen narrator  then badgers the panther into forcing Harry to return the goods, while also badgering Harry into defending himself against the seemingly ruthless panther. Eventually, a full-scale turf battle ensues, then revealing that the off screen narrator is the Devil.Beck, Jerry.
