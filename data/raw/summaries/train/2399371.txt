 Fay Forrester  is an attractive young woman who wants to escape from her abusive boyfriend Vince . She hires Jack Andrews , a second class private investigator, to arrange her "death". She wants to restart her life with a new identity and the money she got from helping Vince commit robberies. Due to Jack's financial problems, he joins Fay after her fake death. Unfortunately, Vince finds out that Fay is still alive. The hunt for Jack, Fay and the money begins. {{clear left}}
