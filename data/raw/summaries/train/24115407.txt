Fraternity brothers at the fictional Alpine College in the mountains recruit Ronnie Duke , a surfer friend from California to seduce Susan Benchley , head of the school sorority and secretary to Dean Carlton , because Susan has brainwashed the other female students on campus to avoid dating the boys. Ronnie sets out a plan to become captain of the ski team and win over Susan, who is engaged to John Harris , while he also attempts to save the school from its financial troubles.
