An Australian  sent to England for university education discovers that his mother has been victimised by a powerful squatter, Sir Edward Marriott . He returns to Australia and becomes a bushranger known as 'The Shadow', who only attacks Sir Edward's property. One day he rescues Sir Edward's fiancee, Dorothy , from real bushrangers and falls in love with her. Later on he discovers he is the real heir to Sir Edward's property."WEST'S PICTURES."
