In the film, Murphy plays Danny Lester, a hardened juvenile delinquent sent away by a judge  to a Variety Clubs Boys Ranch in Texas run by Marshall Brown  and his wife Maud  where the couple reform teenage boys, dozens of whom live on the ranch. Most of the boys adapt to life at the ranch successfully, but Danny resists all efforts at socializing him and plots his escape with the aid of a partner in crime on the outside.
