As a series of grotesque shotgun murders plagues the city, a young race car driver goes to the police  claiming that he has been having visions about the killer. He gets locked up as the suspect. A lady reporter  falls in love with the suspect and sets out prove his innocence and find the real killer. A rogue biker  who accosts the reporter and kidnaps her is thought to be the killer in question so after he has been slaughtered in a shoot-out, they figure they have solved the shotgun slayings. But have they?
