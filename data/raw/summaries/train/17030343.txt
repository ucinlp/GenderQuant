Kali  is a local ruffian who harasses students in his college. The principal Satyaprakash  is helpless to stop him because Kali is the son of Dabala  who is one of the biggest gangsters in town. The principal has two sons and a daughter - Vikas , Vijay  and Pooja. Vijay is in love with Priya  a colleague of his in his engineering company. Kali rapes Pooja, and then the corrupt inspector Shirke  puts the blame on Pooja's fiancee, and has him killed while in custody. This enrages Vijay and he embarks upon a bloody and violent fight to cleanse the city of ruffians like Kali and Dabala and Shirke. Mohan Joshi shows up in a cameo as an honest ACP.
