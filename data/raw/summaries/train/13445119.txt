The film tells the story of a young woman, Deborah Dainton , who has a limp as a result of suffering from polio. Her rigid and controlled life is transformed when she meets a handsome stranger called Leigh Hartley  at a party. After developing a friendship, romance soon blossoms and for the very first time in her life Deborah feels happy. This happiness does not last long as she soon discovers Leigh has been lying to her about his life. Leigh and his accomplices are planning to rob the antiques shop where Deborah works. They require her knowledge of the shop in order to pull the robbery off.
