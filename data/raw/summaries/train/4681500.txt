Julia Mann  and Kevin Vallick  are insomniac writers who fall in love, but their romance is thrown for a loop because both are writing speeches for rival candidates in a New Mexico election. Julia is working for the Democratic candidate and Kevin for the Republican candidate. Also complicating matters are Kevin's ex-wife , who is also on the Republican campaign trail, and "Mr. Flack Jacket," television reporter "Bagdad Bob" Freed , Julia's estranged fiance, who wants her back.
