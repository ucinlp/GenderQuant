Mathias  is an innkeeper in a village in Alsace, happily married to Catherine  and with a daughter Annette . However he is greatly in debt so on Christmas Day 1833 murders a Polish Jew  who visits the inn for his gold. He uses this to pay off his debts and rise in society, becoming the burgomeister of the town - however he is always tormented by guilt. Fifteen years later on Christmas Day, Mathias becomes delirious and hears the sound of the Jew's sleigh bells. He dreams he is being tried for the murder and is found guilty. He awakes and dies, leaving his family none the wiser.
