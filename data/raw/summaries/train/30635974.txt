Sandhya is in search of her prince charming and when Gopi comes to know about this he pretends to be a rich prince and Sandhya starts dating him. But soon she comes to know that Gopi is a poor man but nevertheless they start loving each other. Meanwhile Gopi's friend request Gopi to become an Aunty for a big favour. He does so with hilarious results, as two men  start loving him. What will he do now?
