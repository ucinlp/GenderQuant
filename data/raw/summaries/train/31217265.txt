The film is set during World War II and centres upon the letters written by Mohandas Gandhi  to Adolf Hitler , and around the relationship of Hitler with his long-term lover Eva Braun , whom he married in his final days in the Berlin bunker in which they died. The film depicts the difference between the ideologies of Gandhi and Hitler and claims the superiority of Gandhism over Nazism.<ref namehttp://www.dearfriendhitlerthefilm.com/synopsis.html | titledearfriendhitlerthefilm.com | accessdate=April 17, 2011}}
