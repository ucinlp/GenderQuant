Secret agent George Johnson  recalls the story of two orphan girls to a pair of cohorts at a Paris cafe. Angel  is adopted by crooks who teach her to steal, while Evelyn  is the criminally inclined girl adopted by a wealthy family. When Angel steals a watch from a passenger on the train, the man refuses to press charges and enlists her help in his confidence scheme. Evelyn is engaged to marry the promising author Robert Ellington , but the writer falls for Angel. Ellington is scheduled to leave on a ship but gives his ticket to Johnson, the secret agent and con man. Angel watches tearfully as the boat pulls away before Ellington reveals he loves her.
