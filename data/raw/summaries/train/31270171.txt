In Civil War-era Southern Missouri, Dr. Maggie McCullough  travels to the aid of her ailing Aunt Annie , the town's madame, in the lovely community of Sweetwater. With all of the men away at war, Maggie coordinates a truce between Aunt Annie's girls and the respectable women of the town.
