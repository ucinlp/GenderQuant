A comedian poses as an international spy to recover mysterious microfilm. Peanuts White, a burlesque comic, is recruited by U.S. agents to impersonate international spy Eric Augustine  in a mission to purchase a million-dollar microfilm in mysterious, exotic Tangier. There, he encounters the irresistible Lily Dalbray, an "old friend" of Augustine who is now dealing with his arch-enemy, Brubaker. But where is the real Eric? Comedy thriller with slapstick climax.
