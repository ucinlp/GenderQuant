Three friends, Ganesh , Diganth  and Kitty  come to Mugilupete. Ganesh falls in love with Sowmya , a widow, staying with her in-laws Kodandaram  and Padma  while Diganth and Kitty fall for Kodandaram's daughters Radha  and Pavani  respectively. While Diganth-Radha and Kitty-Pavani get the green signal for marriage, Sowmya is hesitant to accept Ganesh as her life partner. The director shows how Ganesh wins her heart in a unique way.
