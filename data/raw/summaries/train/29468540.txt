Mithun is an honest police officer of the department and he is in love with Somu Ali. Eventhough he is a strict person to outside world, he is a lenient with his younger brother Saif at home. Saif Ali Khan, a happy go lucky guy falls in love with Shweta. Meantime, a group of 5 criminals  plots a bank robbery and Saif gets implicated in the robbery and is now on a run from the police. Officially Mithun is assigned the task of arresting Saif at any cost and he makes all efforts to nab Saif, but fails. Another important character is a Governer, husband of sheila. Situation become even worse when the members of the group starts getting killed one by one. Mithun is now determined to catch Saif dead or alive. Meanwhile Mithun also reveals that his girlfriend Somy Ali is a bar dancer who works in the bar owned by the same criminals. On the other hand Saif also try to collect evidences for prove his innocece to his brother, but gets in a bigger problem as Somy Ali sees him coming out of the house of the criminal who gets killed. Who is behind these murders ? Is Saif a criminal ? Would Mithun find out the real culprit ? All these questions are answered in the climax.
