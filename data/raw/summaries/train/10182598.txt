 After being stereotyped in the girl-next-door image after her Bollywood debut film, Guddi , Banerjee, p. 93Jaya Bhaduri tried to break out of the mould with a glamourous role in the film.Gulzar, p. 457 Madhu  falls in love with Ravi Anand , son of an employee in her Thakur brother's  household. When they get married, the Thakur breaks all ties with them, she moves into Ravi's home, where they live with his younger brother, Vijay . Subsequently, Vijay meets a girl, Neeta , at school, with the usual problems with parents. She turns out to be the Thakur's daughter, who has been promised in marriage to Benny Sinha . Satyen Kappu gives a comical performance as the DJ friend of Randhir Kapoor in two scene-stealing pieces: first, when he impersonates Randhir Kapoor's father to meet the boy's teacher, and later when he meets Jaya's father.
