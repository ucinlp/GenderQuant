The film begins with Jake Singer  meeting up with Julia  in an attempt to rekindle their relationship; she however, informs him that she is recently engaged and has been wanting to call him to tell him. The wedding will be in Aspen but she invites him to her pre-wedding party in New York. Jake, the son of a retired physician Arnold Singer , is an English teacher and somewhat of a basketball coach at Coventry, a Manhattan private school. He becomes involved with Allegra Marshall  the widow of a wealthy gentleman who died suddenly from a cardiac embolism. Jake seeks treatment from psychoanalyst Dr. Ernesto Morales  who frequently surprises Jake in the form of hallucinations attempting to shape or modify his behavior.
