Gus , an electrician, wants to purchase a bowling alley with his friends. The problem is that he still pays alimony to his ex-wife, Leonora . Gus, realizing that if he can get Leonora to remarry the alimony stops, attempts to match-make Leonora with various men. James Gandolfini has a small part as one of Lenora's would-be suitors. In the course of his match-making, and after Gus breaks up with his girlfriend, Rita , Gus and Leonora discover that they still care about each other.
