After eight months of hyper-sleep, when Christopher 'Chris' McNiel  and Theta Kaplan  of a Mars cargo transport ship the Solar System Shipping Vessel No.17  awaken, they find out something has gone terribly wrong. They've woken up from hyper-sleep almost ten months past their scheduled time, find the ship's internals badly damaged, off course and almost no fuel. What's more, it's discovered the first shift killed each other. As the plot unfolds, something truly evil is discovered on board - an alien biological life form which can psychologically manipulate humans.
