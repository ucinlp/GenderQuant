Virginia Gardner, a flapper girl, is left out of a fashionable party thrown by her mother . To get revenge, she plays a joke by passing out notes to some of the guests reading, "Consult your conscience. Your secret is common gossip." This message sends a number of people into a panic during the party, and a shot rings out, wounding one of the men. When the mystery is cleared up, Marion Taylor  and Edward Mallory  have fallen in love.Synopsis at NY Times Movie Database
