The setting is a housing project apartment. Johnny Pope ([[Don Murray  is a soldier who returns from the Korean War, where a stay in a military hospital has left him secretly addicted to morphine. His emotional distance  alienate him from the apartment's other residents: Johnny's pregnant wife Celia  and his brother Polo . Celia is convinced that Johnny is having an affair, but of course the truth is far worse. Johnny and Polo's father, John Sr., arrives in town and stays with them in the small apartment, further complicating a tense situation and leading to a dynamic and dramatic climax.
