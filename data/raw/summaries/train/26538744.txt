The story is about a pro-communist leftist Zhou Heung-Kong  who grew up in the pre-1997 British colony of Hong Kong starting the from 1950s. He lives with his wife Ying  who mostly raises the family by herself. Zhou has fantasies of going to Tiananmen Square, but has always been too poor to do so. They eventually find themselves in a HK transferred over to the People's Republic of China. In the end Zhou realised he sacrificed everything for the communist cause, and his family is left with nothing.
