Pinky Palkar  is an aspiring skater who dreams of making it big through a television show. She is the archetypical Mumbai chawl girl who dreams big, despite her lowly surroundings. {{cite news}} Her dreams go awry when One-Shot-Nandu , a fighter who pummels his opponents in the ring blindfolded, knocks her down in a freak car accident turning her blind.‘Lafangey Parindey’ – edgy love story of street fighter, blind girl : WorldSnap Despite her handicap, Pinky is determined to participate in the show. Full of remorse, Nandu teaches her to "see" through her blindness. In return, she teaches him to skate; allowing Nandu to be her partner in the competition.They gradually fall in love.
