Xavier, a stenographer in a company tries the patience of the workaholic manager with his irregular hours of work. The manager warns him but he doesn't make any effort to improve. Once, he asks for leave to take his wife to the hospital. The manager later finds him at the beach playing with children and dismisses him from the job.
