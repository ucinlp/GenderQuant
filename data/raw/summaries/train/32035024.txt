Wyatt Earp sits down with a reporter in his home. The reporter and Earp talks in an interview of how he became a famous lawman. Earp tells the story of how the legend became a fearless US Marshal. A 27 year old Wyatt Earp  finds out that his first favorite girl, Dora Hand , was murdered by a man. He goes out of town to track down the man and teams up with his friend Doc Holliday , Bat Masterson , Charlie Bassett , and Bill Tilghman . The screenplay was penned by Darren Benjamin Shepherd.
