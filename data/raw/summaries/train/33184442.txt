Karuvarai Pookkal, the first Tamil film about transgenders, is a docu-feature film. It illustrates the pathetic life and folklore of transgenders in India especially in Tamil Nadu. It portrays the critical situation of the family where a transgender child is born, discusses the difficulties and taboos of bringing up that child, and the difficulties of the child itself. Julia Robert , a real Transgender, plays the part of Gopi, a transforming transgender from childhood to adulthood.
