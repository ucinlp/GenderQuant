Movie revolves around Viswanathan  Meera  and little Vasudev  who are form a very happy family. Meera is not Vasu's real mother. For Vasu, Meera was his mother and his best friend too. They live happily. In the meantime Meera gives birth to a son, and it is Vasu who is the happiest. He names his little brother Appu and cares much for him. But then, gradually, Vasu begins to feel that Appu is getting more attention from his parents and that he himself is being neglected. That begins to create problems. And then Vasu does something horrible that gets him imprisoned in the juvenile home.After completing the terms in juvenile home he came back to the home and he get a surprise from Meera,It is an another baby and he kisses the baby.
