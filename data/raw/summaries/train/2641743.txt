A beautiful woman, "Charming Jones"  is being escorted by a cowboy, "Handsome Stranger" . Jones claims money left her by her father, Parody Jones . However, bad guy Avery Simpson , who handed her the money, decides he wants it for himself. He hires an old cowboy, "Cactus Jack" , to rob them when they leave town. Cactus Jack's unsuccessful attempts to the steal the money resemble a Road-Runner cartoon, with Jack playing the part of Wile E. Coyote. The story has a surprise ending, with Charming finally chosing Cactus Jack to be her lover, over the much younger Handsome Stranger.
