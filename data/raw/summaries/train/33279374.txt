Several murders have taken place throughout the city of Kolkata. A serial killer is believed to be responsible. The modus operandi being that the killer always carries out the murder according to verses in Bengali poetry. He leaves behind the verses next to the victims. The police are baffled, with their lead detective struggling to solve the case. This leads his superior deciding to bring back an ex-cop to help solve the case. The ex-cop, who was earlier suspended for orchestrating a fake encounter, is also well known for his ill temperament. Now the ex-cop and the lead detective must work together to catch the serial killer before another innocent loses his life . Meanwhile the lead detective has broke up with his girlfriend .
