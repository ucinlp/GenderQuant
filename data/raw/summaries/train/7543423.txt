Robert L. Talbot , an American millionaire, arrives early for his annual vacation at his luxurious Italian villa. His long-time girlfriend Lisa  has given up waiting for him to "pop the question," and has decided to marry another man. Meanwhile, his sneaky major domo Maurice  secretly misappropriates the villa as a hotel while Talbot is away. The current guests of the "hotel" are a group of young American girls trying to fend off a gang of oversexed boys, led by Tony .
