Callie Carpenter  is a misguided teenager sent to stay for the summer with her aunt and uncle, Maggie and Mitchell Foster , on their horse ranch. Callie becomes friends with horse trainer Rafe Acuna , who teaches her to ride. She helps him discover that something is seriously amiss with Kia, a "wild" thoroughbred horse. When Snapper , a horse trainer gone bad, tries to sell Kia illegally, Callie relies on her street smarts to outwit Snapper. Rafe and Callie figure out that to save Kia, they must prove he really is a champion.
