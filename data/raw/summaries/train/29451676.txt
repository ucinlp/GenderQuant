Mithun Chakraborty's ambition is to become an upright Police officer, but unluckily he is not getting selected. His sole strength is his lady love Sridevi. As Mithun's ambition fails, he turns to alcohol and goondaism. He finally joins Amrish Puri's lethal gang to take revenge on Police force, which blocked his ambition. Sridevi , is Shakti Kapoor's important gang member. One day, Shakti Kapoor gets the confidential message about an under cover cop in his Gang. Who is the undercover agent? forms the Climax.
