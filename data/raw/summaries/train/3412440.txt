Zora Matthews , whose mother Sarah  conceived her with the aid of an anonymous sperm donor, discovers her father is a white man named Hal Jackson . This comes as a major shock to Sarah, who had explicitly requested a black donor. On top of that, Jackson is an obnoxious, sleazy car salesman. The film revolves around Zora and her mother's rocky relationship with Jackson. Jackson eventually comes to have feelings for his supposed daughter and her mother.
