The drama tells the story of spoiled and alcoholic millionaire Carson . During a trip to the Mojave Desert Carson breaks his leg after falling off his horse and is abandoned and left to die by Geraldine , his adulterous femme fatale wife, and his deceitful business partner Joseph Duncan . After the accident, Geraldine and Duncan supposedly drive off to seek medical aid for Carson. But, when Carson realizes the truth of his dilemma, he vows to live long enough to exact revenge against his wife and partner.
