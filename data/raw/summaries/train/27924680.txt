When bank teller Ryan  meets beautiful but icy business executive Angelica  he becomes instantly infatuated with her. He stumbles upon the perfect opportunity to spy on her when he is hired by Dolores  as a part-time maintenance man and moves into an apartment across the street from Angelica. He uses his position and his apartment's view to start spying on her, and slowly inserts himself into Angelica's life. Angelica turns on him and Ryan slits his wrists in the bathtub in despair. When items from Ryan's apartment begin explicitly appearing in Angelica's apartment, Dolores becomes suspicious of Angelica, especially when Angelica visits the apartment building and produces a memento from Dolores' late son.
