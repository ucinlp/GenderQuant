Baseball player-turned-insurance salesman Byung-woo is the cocky ace of his company until one of his clients commits suicide. Worse, the police suspect him of aiding and abetting the suicide. Byung-woo's career is on the block now, and he can't risk anyone else dying on him. Looking back at his clients, Byung-woo decides to take preventive measures by reaching out to those in danger of committing suicide, including a lonely husband , an indebted lounge singer , a homeless young man , and a widowed mother . The first film from director Jo Jin-mo, Suicide Forecast reaffirms life through an insurance salesman's hilarious, self-serving quest to save his clients. Ryu Seung-beom stars as the unlikely hero who turns around the lives of his troubled clients, and his own in the process.
