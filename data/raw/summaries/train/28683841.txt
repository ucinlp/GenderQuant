Balachandra Menon  is a professor who has remained a chronic bachelor out of choice. He has nothing against women and nurtures the picture of an ideal partner in his mind. His friends and well wishers want him to settle down before his retirement. Menon is about to get retired and at that point he meets Priyamvada  and gets married to her. Rahul , Menon's nephew, stays with him and the relationship that develops between him and Priyamvada makes Menon suspicious. This creates problems in Menon's married life.
