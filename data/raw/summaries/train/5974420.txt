The story is set in a suburb of Madurai. Suryan  and Chandran  are twins. Suryan, the elder of the two is soft spoken guy who works at a local lending library and he falls in love with Manimekala , daughter of Namasivayam  the local politician-don and MLA. Chandran, the younger one, works as a mortuary assistant in the local government hospital and he's one among Namasivayam’s bad guys who are in charge of his election campaign.
