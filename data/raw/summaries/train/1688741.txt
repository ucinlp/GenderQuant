John  is a bit of a day-dreamer, always drifting off during his lessons. He is sent home for not paying attention during a lesson on electricity. But on the tube on the way home suddenly the train and everyone in it turns yellow. The doctor  declares that it's harmless and should wear off soon but that evening John hears noises from his television set and meets Nic  . A strange adventure ensues which involves pet mice and John's threatened execution at the Tower of London. Or was it all a dream?
