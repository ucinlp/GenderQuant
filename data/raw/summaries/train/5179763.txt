Young Arjun  is brought up by his Daddu  . His Daddu decides for him to go to the city in search of a new job and life. While in the city he meets up with Bhairon  who guides him into an interview in a 5-star hotel owned by Raja . While in the hotel, Arjun meets Poonam  and falls in love with her. During this time, many attempts are made to kill Raja by Girdar Singh  and his son Ranjeet. The blame for these murders goes to Raja's mother , who is actually Arjun's mother. Realising all this Arjun takes an oath no matter what happens he will not let any harm come to Raja. As these attempts are being made, can Arjun thwart them all?
