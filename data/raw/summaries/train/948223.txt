The story begins on "the strangest day" of Larry Burrows  life consisting of a series of comic and dramatic misadventures. Larry, who blames all of his life's problems on the fact that he struck out during a key moment of a high school baseball game, wishes he had done things differently. His wish is granted by a guardian angel-like figure named Mike , and appears at various times as a bartender, a cab driver, and so on. Larry soon discovers that Mike has transferred Larry into an alternate reality in which he had won the pivotal high school game. He now finds himself rich and  powerful, and married to the boss's  sexy daughter Cindy Jo Bumpers . At first, his new life seems perfect, but he soon begins to miss his best friend Cliff Metzler  and wife Ellen  from his previous life; he also discovers that his alternate self has created many enemies, like Jewel Jagger , and as Larry's problems multiply, he finds himself wishing to be put back into his old life.
