Bo is the driver and Harley is the mechanic. They stop at a roadside diner to eat and meet Junelle, played by future soap legend Robin Mattson. Junelle, while attractive, is in a world of her own and after having an altercation with a customer, she is rescued by Bo and Harley. The hot-rodding friends leave quickly but find that Junelle  wants to travel with them. Their adventure on the road with Junelle turn dangerous after a misunderstanding at a grocery store, where Junelle is trying to raise funds for Bo and Harley's entrance fee to the drag race. Sgt. Whittaker  becomes obsessed with catching them, which leads to tragic results. Return to Macon County was not as popular as Macon County Line.
