Four college co-eds head south on a spring break trek to Fort Lauderdale, Florida seeking fun in the sun: Carole  taking a separate vacation from her steady boyfriend, winds up as a hot contender in a "Hot Bod Contest"; Jennie  is doubly lucky, courted by both a rich classical pianist and a devil-may-care rocker; Sandra  looking for the Mr. Right who will finally satisfy her; and Laurie  dreams of a night of unbridled passion with a real he-man.
