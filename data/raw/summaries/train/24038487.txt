On the beautiful island of Janitzio, in the middle of the Pátzcuaro Lake in Michoacán in Mexico lives Maclovia , the beautiful daughter of a leader of Tarasco Indian community . Maclovia loves José María Lopez , a young very poor Indian. Maclovia's father refuses to the marriage of the couple. José María hopes to win the favor of the old man. But the arrival of a battalion causing the conflict when the brutal sergeant  develops eyes for Maclovia.
