In New York in the 1920s, amorous opera star Augustino "Gus" Caraffa  crosses paths with Isabelle Perry , a naive music student from Mississippi who is his biggest fan. When a news photographer catches them in a kiss, it is proposed that they get married in name only to avoid a scandal.
