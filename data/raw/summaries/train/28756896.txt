Sukhamo Devi is the story of Nandan and Thara getting married as their personal love affairs failed. Nandan was in love with Devi, but circumstances forces Devi to choose another person. Thara was in love with Sunny, but he gets killed in an accident. The film also show the good side of friendship between Sunny and Vinod, who survives the accident, but living his a mechanical life. The film was a treat for campus lovers.
