The story is about a private financer Eldho, who lives in a hill station with his mother. His mother had become mentally challenged after she was raped by a police constable  during Eldho's childhood. His mother conceived from the rape and gave birth to a baby girl. Johnkutty , Eldho's father is avenging his wife's death by going after the police constable and unfortunately dies. After Eldho has grown up, he reaches Pathiramanal, a place in Kuttanad, in search of the constable to take revenge. There he meets Sara . He realizes that Sara is the daughter of the constable. The drama is what the film is about.{{cite news}}
