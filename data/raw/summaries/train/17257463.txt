The film tells the story of Chandrama Singh , who was raised by Baba , a Naxal leader based in the southern interiors of Bihar, after his family was brutally murdered. He later gets picked up under a covert Governmental program jointly conceived by RAW and Intelligence Bureau to carry out political assassinations. He falls in love with Shubhi , a kindergarten teacher, and decides to lead a reformed life but a chanced encounter entangles him in the world of crime once again.{{cite web}} {{cite web}}
