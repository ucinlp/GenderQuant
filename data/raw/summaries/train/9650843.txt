Siluvai  is the son of Cruz . They are Christians living in a fishing village. In another town, Perumaal  is the father to Sangeetha  and Gauri. Sangheeta comes to the small fishing village. Raghuvaran is proposed  to marry her. However, Gauri, falls in love with Siluvai's friend and after hearing that their father Jaiganesh doesn't want to accept the Hindu-Christian marriage, she decides to elope with her lover. This leads to the breakup of Sangeetha and Siluvai's love affair. However Raghuvaran, upon hearing Siluvai's story, gives a great speech to the villagers and unites Siluvai and Sangeetha.
