In this television special, the Muppets are visiting Kermit's family for their annual reunion. When the others learn that the swamp is right next to Walt Disney World, they sneak in, and are pursued by a security guard . Attractions featured include Big Thunder Mountain, the Indiana Jones Epic Stunt Spectacular!, Star Tours, the Mad Hatter Tea Cups, and World Showcase. The special is notable for making the three parks  seem like one connected area, despite the parks actually being apart from each other. The story ends with the Muppets having a friendly meeting at Mickey Mouse's office where the signature characters of Walt Disney and Jim Henson compare their companies' theme songs, "When You Wish Upon a Star" and "The Rainbow Connection"  respectively and the ideals behind them.
