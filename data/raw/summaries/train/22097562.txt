Beginning in the Congo, a group of mercenaries led by British Major Harry Grigsby  are due to be picked up by helicopters after completing a mission. As they board the choppers they are fired on from the helilcopters by another group of mercenaries led by American Kip Thompson  who has been hired to change sides. Recovering in England, Grigsby is recruited by HM Government to take out Thompson who has been hired by Red China to stir up trouble in the New Territories between Hong Kong and Red China. Grigsby recruits his surviving old crew including Rafer Johnson, John Thaw, Andrew Keir, and Julian Glover. In addition to fighting Thompson, Grigsby finds time to seduce the wife  of his liaison, a British General .
