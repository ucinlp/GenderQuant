Madhu aka Madhavan  is a successful playback singer. Chandramathi ([[Urvashi  is his illiterate, rustic, betel-chewing wife with a bad habit of spitting in wrong places, and they have four children. It can be described as incompatible matrimony but still a happy one. Madhu loves his wife even though they are out of sync because she has brought him luck. Here enters Lekha , a sophisticated urban model-cum-singer, who finds a place in the hearts of all the family members.
