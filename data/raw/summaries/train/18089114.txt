A seemingly down to earth guy Arun  by day leads a life of a computer hacker and thief by night. When he meets a girl  whom he fancies, he wishes to turn into a new leaf. But Arun's rogue brother who has been blackmailing him since young to do his dirty deeds does not think likewise. A deep love hate relationship between them which unfolded during their childhood days traps Arun into a life of crime. How Arun chooses between his family and his love forms the crux of the story.
