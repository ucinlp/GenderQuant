The film gets its title from the character's Mammooty role Johnnie Varghese. He has a brother Boby varghese who studies in Bangalore degree college for Economics. On a recent visit from his brother and friends changes Johnnie's mind and he decide to join college despite of his age. From there he clashes with drug Mafia  who rules the college and what happens to Johnnie and boby is the rest of the plot.
