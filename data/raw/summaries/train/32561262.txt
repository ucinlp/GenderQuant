A bourgeois Catalan family are in a state of disarray. They are Miguel, his wife Alicia, their son Lluís, and Miguel's elderly parents. Lluís collects stray cats and dogs that now dominate the family apartment, much to his father's chargrin. Alicia is incapable of challenging the whims of her son and ponders separating from her husband. Miguel's mother, Maria suffers from Alzheimer's and her husband believes suicide may be the solution to their problems. Meanwhile Alicia's friend, Raquel believes in a tough love method of parenting her daughter, albeit a method that leads to abuse.The Mosquito Net British Film Institute. Retrieved on 28 July 2011
