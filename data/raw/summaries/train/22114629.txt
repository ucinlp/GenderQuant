Srikanth  appears as the protagonist while Prithviraj Sukumaran  appears as the antagonist. Srikanth is a sincere cop who tries to remove the wrong doers, whereas Prithviraj is a corrupt police officer who has tie-ups with anti-social people. Srikanth finds Prithviraj to be the culprit but is unable to prove that. In the event he also loses his wife . How he manages to prove the corrupt police officer as the culprit forms the rest of the story.
