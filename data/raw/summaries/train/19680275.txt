The film is a comedy and a social commentary on the period and is set just after World War II. An officer's wife has a romantic dream about her husband's man  and comes to believe it is true. Meanwhile the husband has asked his servant to help him, after the war, to suggest ways to ignite the romance he and his wife had before the war, as well as find a way to make money in a post-war economy. Misdirection and misunderstandings ensue. It was a J. Arthur Rank presentation, a Sydney Box production and was released through General Film Distributors Ltd.
