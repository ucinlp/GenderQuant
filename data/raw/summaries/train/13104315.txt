The film is set in the period of the Hindutva mobilization of the 1980s, the consequent explosive communal conflict, and its impact on the lives of the young men of the minority Muslim community, represented by a working class Muslim family in Mumbai. Salim Pasha  is a small-time thief and goonda and lives with his parents and sister in a shady neighborhood where crime and illegal activities are accepted professions.Salim Langde Pe Mat Ro, New York Times.Salim Langde Pe Mat Ro NYU, Abu Dhabi.Salim Langde Pe Mat Ro  Review
