Vijay Choudhury lives with his wife, Laxmi, and step-brother, Ravi. Even after several years of being married, Vijay and Laxmi have no children. So Laxmi is taunted for her supposed barrenness by her fellow villagers, even though Vijay is the headman of the village. Ravi falls in love with Lalita, daughter of Milawatram and Bandharlekha. They get married and Lalita proves to be an ideal sister-in-law for Laxmi and Vijay. Soon, both Lalita and Laxmi get pregnant and give birth at the same time. However, the family goes through a traumatic experience as only one of the babies survives. What happens next forms the climax of the film.
