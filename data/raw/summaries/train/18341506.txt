Some years after their son is killed, a married couple  decide to adopt a child. One day a 7-year-old boy, Eli , unexpectedly arrives on their doorstep claiming to be from the adoption agency. Eli wears a suit everyday and is very well spoken for a child. He helps the adults rescue their failing toy business. The parents, who couldn't conceive a second child after their first child had died, eventually get pregnant. They then find out that Eli never was real, but instead an angel.
