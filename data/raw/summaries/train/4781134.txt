A young English woman named Nikky Ferris  takes a trip with her aunt  to the Greek island of Crete. She meets a wounded stranger  and the plot takes off, involving her in the man's attempt to recover stolen jewels while battling the thief  who is in hiding at his sister's  small inn called 'The Moon-Spinners'. The thief hopes to sell the jewels to a mysterious wealthy woman, played by silent film star Pola Negri, in her final film role.
