Aditya Verma, fondly called Adi Chachu or Adi Uncle  by the children of his brother, lives in a large mansion in Mumbai with his brother , sister-in-law, sister and grandmother. He is the only one working in the family. He falls in love with a local teacher, Priya . They confess their feelings to each other and plan a wedding. However, before the wedding day Adi is run over by a truck to save Parth  and dies. In afterlife, he meets The Hindu God of Death, Yamraj , a kind-hearted, emotional deity with designer clothes and an red old car. Yamraj allows Adi to go back to Earth as a ghost to stop his evil uncle  who wants to sell Adi's mansion to industrialist Hirachand . Adi enlists the aid of Shakti, a little boy, in order to save his family home. They succeed with some further help from Yamraj, who then allows both Shakti and Aditya to return to life. In the morning Adi and Shakti pretend that nothing has happened to them.
