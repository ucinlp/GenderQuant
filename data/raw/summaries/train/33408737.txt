Grace Roberts , marries rancher Edward Smith, who is revealed to be a crappy vice-ridden spouse. They have a daughter, Vivian. Dr. Franklin  whisks Grace away from this unhappy life, and they move to New York under aliases, pretending to be married . Grace and Franklin have a son, Walter . Vivian gets sick, however, and Grace and Franklin return to save her. Somehow this reunion, as Smith had assumed Grace to be dead, causes the death of Franklin.
