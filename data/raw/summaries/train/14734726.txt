Sweepstakes winner Joe is taken to the cleaners by con men G. Y. Prince  and R. O. Broad  by investing his winnings in Consolidated Fujiyama California Smog Bags. The Stooges head to their offices to get Joe's money back. Instead, they find two sympathetic businessmen  who offer to pay back the losses if Moe, Larry and Joe will pose as juvenile wards for a rich and eccentric millionaire, Montgomery M. Montgomery . But Montgomery is actually Prince's and Broad's gang leader, and plotting to kill the Stooges.
