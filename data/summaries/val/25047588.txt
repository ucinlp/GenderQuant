A Cardinal  is falsely accused of treason because he represents a sect that is a range to the new regime. The Interrogator , an old friend of the Cardinal's, is given the task of persuading him to make a public confession of treason. The Interrogator eventually breaks though by showing how the Cardinal became a priest to escape from his childhood. To purge his sin, the Cardinal confesses to every lie of which he is accused, and is released to face a silent, bewildered crowd.
