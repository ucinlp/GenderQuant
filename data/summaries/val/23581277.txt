Seeta , a teenage girl, lives with her mother and elder sister  who runs the house with her harikatha performances. Seeta falls in love with Chandram , but evil Gopalakrishna  has an eye on her and hires goons to bash Chandram, who dies on the spot. Seeta marries Madhava Rao , father of Gopalakrishna, and makes Gopalakrishna realize his mistakes.
