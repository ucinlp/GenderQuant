{{Expand section}} Joey King Sr.  was part of a successful singing group in the 1950s and now works at a gas station and is a borderline alcoholic. His son, Joey Jr.  has started a rock band with his teenage friends which triggers anger in his father and he takes away his son's guitar. The father is jealous of the son's talent until the father starts his old band back up and both Kings separately participate in the Royal New York Doo-Wopp Show at Radio City Music Hall and accept one another.
