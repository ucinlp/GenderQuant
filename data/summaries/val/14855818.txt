In the 1880s, a rich nobleman from London, Charles de Beaumont , meets "Frank", an adolescent, and offers hospitality to the young orphan. According to the usages of his caste, he decides to educate him by himself. But Frank is not an obedient pupil and is soon caned... Charles is surprised when he discovers that Frank  is not a boy as he believed, but a beautiful young girl with a dark secret ...DVD liner notes
