Booked Out follows the quirky exploits of the Polaroid loving artist Ailidh  as she spies and photographs the occupants of her block of flats. Jacob , the boy next door who comes and goes quicker than Ailidh can take pictures. Jacqueline , the mysterious girl that Jacob is visiting and the slightly crazy Mrs Nicholls  who Ailidh helps cope with her husbands continuing existence after his death.
