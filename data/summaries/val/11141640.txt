Sathyamurthy  is an educated but unemployed youngster, dividing his time between hanging around with his friends and being verbally abused by his stepmother . Because of these problems, he along with his friends started dealing with the problems around them. A subsequent clash with a local rowdy brings Sathya to the attention of Dhandapani , a rich philanthropist. So Sathya becomes his right-hand man, carrying out his orders while Dhandapani uses Sathya as his weapon in the political war he is carrying on with Mariappan , the local MLA.
