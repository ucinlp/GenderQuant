Brick Bardo  is an alien cop who comes to Earth in pursuit of criminals. Though regular in size on his home planet, he is considered doll-sized on Earth, as are the fugitives he is chasing. Brick enlists the help of an impoverished woman and her son. The bad guys team up with a local gang. When they find out where Brick is hiding, all hell breaks loose. Brick and the family helping him is besieged by angry gang members and his own doll-sized enemies. He must protect the family helping him and get off the planet alive.
