The story centers around the relationship of the three main characters. Volodia, out of work and in need of a place to live, comes to stay with his friend Kolia and wife Liudmila, sleeping on the sofa. However, when Kolia is away on business, the other two begin an affair. Although Kolia is initially outraged, the three settle into a polygamous state, though with Kolia now relegated to the sofa. Liudmila eventually becomes pregnant and the men argue over how to solve the situation, as they are unsure of who the father is.
