Hudugaata is a fun-filled story with light-hearted comedy throughout. Balu Mahendar  is a crime reporter at a popular newspaper. His division gets shifted to a sports reporter because of his hudugaata. He is sent to interview a sports personality in Kanyakumari. From here, the romantic journey starts. He meets the heroine who needs help in the bus. He promises to help her in return for an exclusive news item about her in his newspaper. Will the heroine be able to get what she needs? Will Balu Mahender complete his assigned job? Look out for a comedy edition of this story on the screen.
