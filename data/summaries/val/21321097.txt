Kayam tells the story of Choonda  and Thamara . Choonda is a fisherman. He is also the leader of the Kabaddi Team of the village. Thamara is a native of the neighboring village. She has watched her mother being brutally raped and murdered. Tired with every man out there vying for her body, she flees with Choonda to his village.
