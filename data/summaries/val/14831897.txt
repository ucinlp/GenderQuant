Veteran cop Mike Turner  is brought in to help solve a string of baffling satanic homicides. He soon finds himself along with Capt. Hodges  seeking to catch a murderous psycho called Bishop  who's gone on a killing spree during New Orleans' Mardi Gras. Only problem is that this killer has lost his soul to the devil and is no longer human... but a seemingly indestructible demonic being, intent on destroying the lives of everyone around Mike; and shall not stop until he has cost Mike not just his life... but his soul.
