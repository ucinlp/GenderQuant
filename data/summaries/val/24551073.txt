New York cartoonist Duffy Bergman  marries gourmet chef Meg Lloyd . Meg decides she wants to have a baby, and Duffy eventually agrees. After unsuccessfully trying for some time impregnate Meg, to comfort her, Duffy encourages her to focus on her career and come back to the issue of having a child later. After his mother's death, Duffy becomes fixated on wanting to have a child, which Meg no longer sees as a priority, as she's trying to open her own restaurant. The two start to have marital problems, leading to the two separating. When Daphne moves to New York to work as a network sports reporter, their attraction develops into a relationship. Eventually she becomes pregnant, and although Duffy is happy to be having a baby, he become uncomfortable with how fast their relationship is progressing. Daphne eventually has a miscarriage. Duffy decides to stay with her, but Daphne breaks up with him, stating that they were really staying in the relationship for the baby. At his father's wedding, he hears news about Meg and goes to her restaurant and tries to reconcile with her, stating that he doesn't care if they don't have a baby and that he wants to be with her. He discovers that she has adopted a baby boy, and the two get back together.
