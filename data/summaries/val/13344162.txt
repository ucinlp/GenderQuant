The films is about Pontus  who likes his "every other week" life. One week he is a responsible father to his daughter, and the other week when his ex-wife has his daughter, he parties all the time. Pontus bigger brother, Jens , is the complete opposite. He has three children and has been happily married for 20 years. But one day, the whole marriage collapses for Jens, and he is forced to move to Pontus. Over one night, their lives change radically.
