The narrative takes place in the 15th century, when Louis XI was at odds with Charles of Burgundy.<ref name Mordaunt |lasthttp://movies.nytimes.com/movie/review?resLe Miracle des Loups  |publisher February 24, 1925}} The king's forces are attacked by the Burgundian troops and Beauvais must be defended until reinforcements can arrive. The defence of the city is led by Jeanne Hachette. There are realistic scenes in which wolves attack some of the opponents of Louis XI.
