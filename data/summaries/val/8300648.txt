The story follows a young boy named Leo, who is invited by a school friend to spend his summer holidays at a Norfolk country house during the early 1900s. While Leo stays there, he finds himself a 'go-between' caught up between the daughter of his host and a poor neighbor she loves. Dominic Guard plays the young Leo and Michael Redgrave plays the same character in old age.
