Betsy  and Michael  are only two of the people who ride the Manila Metro Rail Transit System everyday. Betsy is a hopeless romantic while Michael is a non-believer when it comes to love and romance. Betsy is overcome with excitement when her best friend  and her boyfriend set Betsy up on a date with Michael. However, when they finally meet, Michael turns out to be the exact opposite of her ideal man. But Betsy doesn't let herself become disheartened, instead she allows herself to fall in love with the real Michael.
