Kiran is the only child of a violent father. She was born and grew up in a way, so that she is not known of her father's unlawful habits. When Kiran visits one of the Police Inspector's son Suraj, they both fall in love. When Kiran's father finds out that his child is in love with an honest police officers son, who has been assigned to find him, he gets shocked, but decided to give away all his unlawful activities for daughter's future. However, his own goons not liking this, succeeds killing the honest police officer by framing Kiran's father. Now Suraj joins the police department and gets things done in his manner.
