Ravi  is an aspiring film director who works as an assistant director to Idly Vishwanath . Mallik  is the hero of the film and Mumaith  is the heroine for the project. Sandhya , the film's junior artiste, falls in love with Ravi. But Ravi is goal oriented like Kamal Hasan in Sagarasangamam. Also, Ravi's mother  is fighting against cancer. Amidst all this, how Ravi reaches his goal by impressing heroes and producers with his talent forms the main theme. Krishna Bhagawan portrays the role of a villain alongside Supreet.
