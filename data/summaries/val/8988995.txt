This is a story set to pre-independence period in India. A village landlord Kanakayya  marries Seetha , who is young enough to be his daughter, with the help of village karaNam . However, he restricts her a lot after the marriage. Seetha's brother Bangaram  comes from the town and eyes Bangari , Kanakayya's deaf and dumb servant Devudu's  sister. Bangari is in love with Narasimha , another servant of Kanakayya. Bangaram rapes Bangari. Kanakayya suspects that Seetha is in love with Devudu, and in a fit of rage, he kills them both one day. People revolt against Kanakayya and kill him at the end.
