Martine and Michel love each other very much and have decided to get married, but in one evening, their love is put to the test. Two messengers, a demon and an angel, come to their house. Ben Atkinson  comes first and offers Michel a good job and money. Martine suspects a trick, but Michel is ready to accept the demon's offer. The angel tries to go for help but is stopped by devil, who uses policemen to back him up. At the end, the angel prevents the demon's plans.
