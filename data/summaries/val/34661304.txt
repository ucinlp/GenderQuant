Jim  and Roy are friends living together. Jim had recently broke up with his girlfriend and decided to meet new girls through a social networking website ICQ. There, he meets a girl Hiroko  and becoming friends. Both Jim and Roy find Hiroko attractive and Roy plans to have a on-night stand with her. He uses Jim's ICQ account and asks Hiroko out where then they meet in Ma Liu Shui. However, Roy never came back from his meeting with Hiroko and is later found to be dead. This case is handled by Inspector Lai. Hiroko is the prime suspect but there is no evidence. Lai begins to track down old files and find something very shocking.
