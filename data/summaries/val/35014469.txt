Congolese cinema came to light by means of propaganda and educational films during the colonial era. Nowadays, local productions have a hard time keeping their above water, and Congolese filmmakers wonder about the future of a cinema lacking any kind of support. The film is a documentary about Congolese three technicians  and Ferdinad Kanza, a director who made films in the years 1970-1980 and work at the National Radio Television of Congo.{{cite web}}
