Koroku Inumura's original war romance light novel revolves around Charles Karino, a Levam Kingdom mercenary aerial pilot who mans the twin-seater reconnaissance seaplane Santa Cruz. One day, he receives a preposterous assignment: to fly solo over 12,000 kilometers of enemy waters to protect a girl named Fana del Moral. Fana happens to be the next in line to the empire's throne. This mission is actually covert operation as the original escort was taken down by enemy. Charles soon finds out that both original operation and covert operation have been figured by enemy by decoding military telegraphs sent by the crown prince to Fana as part of his love letter."The Princess and the Pilot 9 Minute Digest Streamed" Anime News Network.
