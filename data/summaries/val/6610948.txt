Joshua Lazarus  is a telepath who has been raised in an NSA foster home. Lazarus helps the government by using his abilities. He is told by the agency that the telepathy is a side effect of Widmann's Disease, and that he will become insane in time and eventually die from the illness. However, Lazarus meets a woman with similar powers  who does not have any sign of the disease, launching Lazarus to confront the lies he has been told.{{cite news}}
