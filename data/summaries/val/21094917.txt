The film starts off with the first tale of The Downfall Of Johnny Garrett, which has a woman who was raped by a gang-banger getting revenge using the resurrected corpse of her murdered friend. The film switches to the second tale of Szamota's Mistress, a man who is obsessed and eventually has an affair with a mysterious woman only to find a dastardly secret in the finale. The final tale of the film, called Stalk, is about a busty stripper named Misty  who is being stalked by a crazy and obsessive fan .
