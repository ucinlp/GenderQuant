Below-average pre-medical student Jeffrey Marx , after being rejected by prestigious medical schools, is sent by his father  to a seemingly sub-standard medical school in Central America. Jeffrey eventually discovers the plight of local villagers in need of medical assistance. With the help of fellow students , he illegally provides medical services to the villagers. Eventually, the school's authoritarian dean  discovers the students' activities and decides to prosecute. However, the dean dismisses the charges at the urging of the villagers.
