Caroline Bender  is an ambitious young secretary in a publishing firm who, when jilted, finds consolation in the arms of editor Mike Rice . Gregg Adams  is a typist and an aspiring actress romantically involved with stage director David Savage . When the director dumps her, she is devastated, falls from a fire escape and dies. April Morrison  winds up pregnant, and jumps from a car when her unborn infant's father Dexter Key  urges an abortion. All three women are under the supervision of editor Amanda Farrow , an exacting professional and a frustrated woman who marries, leaves the firm, and returns when she finds the simple life of home and marriage not to her liking.
