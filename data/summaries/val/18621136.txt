The story- A local thug and criminal who kills people like mosquitoes Masanam  slowly rises up the ranks to be a Minister. We have Shakthivel  a die-hard fan of actor Vijay who studies in a city college. He falls in love with the wicked minister's sister Thulasi  and challenges him  and a cat and mouse game ensues. What follows is a series of incidents between the two that leads to a melodramatic climax.About the film
