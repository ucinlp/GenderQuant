In the Mexican city of Guanajuato, in the 1800s, the young and beautiful Amalia de los Robles  there wakes up the passion of all the men of the region, what provokes the fury of his widower father, Don Fernando  that feels for his own daughter a kind of obsession, since he sees in she the face of his dead wife. But Amalia falls in love with the smart Ricardo , but the circumstances prevent them from being united.
