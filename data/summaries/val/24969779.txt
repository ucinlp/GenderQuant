Seetha Kalyanam is the story of Seetha's  swayamvara. Sita's father King Janaka  arranges a swayamvara for his daughter. He announces a contest and declares that whoever can wield Shiva Dhanush  will be given Seetha's hand in marriage. After several kings and princes fail to do so, Rama , the prince of Ayodhya, wields the bow and marries Sita.
