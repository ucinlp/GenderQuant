Ruby Weaver  is weary of her long history of failed relationships with men when she meets Sam Deed  in a park. But after the two fall in love, Ruby becomes suspicious of Sam's past, his obsession with a "Chrystie Delancey", and "causal effect." Under pressure from her, he finally explains that he is really from the year 2470 and is what he calls a "back traveler." Ruby initially ignores this story, considering it yet another case of male nerdy weirdness, but after Sam's persistence, apparent conviction, and growing agitation, she begins to wonder. Finally she takes him to see her therapist . Ruby becomes worried as to Sam's sanity when he reveals that everything he has done was a deliberate attempt to change her life.
