A teenager girl named Thatha  is living with her blacksmith brother Vakkathi Vasu  and his children after the death of her sister in law. Style Raj  is a guy who moves from place to place with a projector and shows movie to public on the run for a living. He meets Thatha and falls in love. Vasu discovers that Raj is actually the murderer of the landlord who killed his father. In the end Raj is sent to jail.
