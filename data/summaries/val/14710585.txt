Tanya  is a female model who lives with her boyfriend Lobo , a surrealist painter who is extreamly violent. Subjected to Lobo's constant abuse, Tanya dreams of escaping to a desert island, which comes true. The only other being on her island is an enormous blue-eyed man-ape (performed by [[Don McLeod  who was born from one of Lobo's paintings. Tanya befriends the beast and nicknames him "Blue", she soon begins to feels a strange attraction to the creature, which makes Lobo increasingly jealous in the real world. He becomes determined to capture this monster and put it in a cage.
