The film is a matrimonial comedy, that featured the dramatic reunion of lovers on London Bridge.Orbituary: Ramsay, Helen Patricia Two men go to great length to ensure that their wives are not cheating on them. Sparing no expense and going to any extreme, the men hire, not one, but three private eyes to spy on the wives. The bungling of these private investigators provides the film's slight humor. In the end, both men realize their wives are faithful and all's well that ends well.
