Kali Prasad aka Kali  is the man of masses in Bezawada.His group has a strong hold on temple city.Vijay Krishna  is right hand to Kali. On the flip side Kali’s brother Shankar Prasad aka Shankar  does not like the supremacy of Vijay Krishna. Due to this personal grudge, Shankar plays spoil sport of brutally killing his brother Kali and then spreads the rumor in Bezawada about Vijay Krishna as man behind this murder.With the help of politician Ramana , even Shankar plans to become a politician but fails due to Vijay Krishna’s authority on Student and Labor Unions. Vijay Krishna has two brothers Jaya Krishna ([[Ajay  and Siva Krishna . Slowly and steadily, Shankar successfully assassinates Vijay Krishna for building his own identity. It is time now for cool, daring and dashing Siva Krishna to enter the scene for completing revenge saga. How did love life of Siva Krishna with college girl friend Geeta , daughter of top brass policeman Adi Vishnu  is affected in this retaliation process forms another angle in story.
