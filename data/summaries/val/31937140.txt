Set in a futuristic post-apocalyptic Los Angeles of 2057, Hawkins Coselow, a crippled soldier, along with his ex-lover, Chandra Kerkorian, share vivid psychosexual dreams. Dr. Alexis Zalazny, who is working on a cybernetic program that will help him to walk again and the program does work somewhat, it goes deadly wrong when the man becomes a killing machine that can interface with any computer and will cause him to kill people that are part of a resistance movement, rebels led by Omegas, cybernetically altered humans, battled government forces in the crime-ridden streets. In time, the Omegas reprogrammed themselves and carried out their own deadly agenda. In the end, the Omegas were destroyed, except one - implanted in a child, Chandra Kerkorian. Now grown, she is ready to lead the rebellion again.
