The year is 1830. The American clipper ship, the Queen, is attacked by pirates in the Hebrides . The ship's mate Kirk Hamilton  is wounded and heads to Queensland Colony in Australia for medical treatment. While at the hospital, he meets and falls in love with Elaine Jeffries , the fiancee of Martin Shannon  a rancher. A romantic rivalry develops and the pirates, who attacked Kirk and his ship kidnap her along with her friend, Nancy Holden . Kirk and Shannon pursue the pirates and they soon wind up on a volcanic island, inhabited by dinosaurs.
