Dangerous mobster Rocky Mosely robs a young engaged couple of their last few dollars for the fun of it. When the mobster's boss Gene Fenmore hears of the robbery, he gives them back their money and takes them under his wing. Rocky, resentful of the couple and tired of Gene's moral code, frames the young couple for a robbery gone wrong. Fenmore must find a way to prove their innocence and protect himself  {{Expand section}}
