The plot consists of 41 scenes.{{cite news}} On a station west of the Darling River, Harry Earl is in love with the station owner's daughter. The evil manager makes advances on her but Earl beats him up. The overseer urges some local Aborigines to kill Earl but one of them, Budgerie, alerts the station men by writing a message on a spear. The stockmen ride to the rescue and save Earl just as the Aborigines are about to perform a "Death Dance" around him. The overseer is killed and Earl is reunited with his love.<ref namehttp://nla.gov.au/nla.news-article65317004 |titleGippsland Times |location21 December 1911 |accessdate3 Edition: MORNINGS. |publisher=National Library of Australia}}
