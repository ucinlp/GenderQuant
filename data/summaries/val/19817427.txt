Future film star Stephen McNally  appears in this Our Gang episode as Bill Patterson, ace director at Mammoth Studios. On the say-so of studio casting director J.D. Broderick, Patterson agrees to give a screen test to the Our Gang kids' talented dog Rover. Alas, the petulant pooch does not take direction well, nor does he respond positively when the cameraman announces that he's "ready to shoot." <ref namehttp://movies.nytimes.com/movie/226211/Rover's-Big-Chance/overview |title2008-10-08|work=NY Times}}
