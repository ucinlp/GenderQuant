Omanakkuttan  is twenty-two years old and too lazy to hold down a job, which angers his father . His life takes a turn when he goes for his friend Sudi's  sister's marriage and he marries her ; this angers her father, Govindan but he allows them to stay in his home. Malu becomes pregnant and both Omanakkuttan and Malu are thrown out of the house since Omanakkuttan doesn't agree to an abortion according to Thilakan's wishes. Omanakkuttan lives to save his child and wife.
