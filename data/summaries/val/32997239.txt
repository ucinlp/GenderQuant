Laurie Larand  returns from the war and finds himself jilted and broke. He goes to work for a horse trainer who he discovers to be in league with a book maker to fleece the horse owners. He also comes across a Shakesperean actor, Poverty Point , who becomes his friend, and the beautiful Doiya , who he falls in love with. In the finale, Larland exposes the villains and is united with Doris.{{cite news}}
