Viswanath , a famous lawyer, stays with his widowed mother  and physically handicapped sister . He sends Jegan, a rich magnate's son  and Lokaiya, two criminals to jail on the charge of rape and murder of an innocent girl. Jagan’s father, GMK  uses his influence and starts creating trouble in Viswanath’s life. GMK succeeds in getting Viswanath imprisoned on a trumped up charge. His heartbroken mother dies. Once out of jail, Viswanath sets out to punish the culprits when he temporarily loses his eyesight…
