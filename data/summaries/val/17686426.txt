Happening after the events in How to Train Your Dragon, the short shows Hiccup, Astrid, Fishlegs, Toothless and Gobber telling the legend behind the Book of Dragons with the misadventures of its author, Bork the Bold. In addition to Bork's discoveries, the young Vikings reveal their own training methods they developed with new, never before seen dragons. The short shows a total of 14 dragons, each spread into 7 classes: Stoker , Boulder , Fear , Sharp , Tidal , Mystery  and Strike .<ref nameHow to Train Your Dragon Continues On DVD|url81819|accessdateComingSoon.net|dateDreamWorks Animation}}
