Danielle "Daisy" Brookes is an orphan who leaves her ranch and her boyfriend to pursue her college degree. She is "tapped" by the Ivy Society because the leader of the Ivy Society wants an internship that Daisy is in line for. Later, she finds out that they have a killing history that has been covered up by the dean, who is also an Ivy. The girls attempt to make Daisy one of them fully... What will happen?
