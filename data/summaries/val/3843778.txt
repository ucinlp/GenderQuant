Yearning to be a respectful cop and vanquish the evil since he was young, Byung-ki  is a patrolman of few words in a village, helping out trivial tasks like distributing promotional papers. One day, he runs into Min-kyong , an employee of the neighborhood bowling alley, and falls for her at first sight. His attempts to woo her, however, are hardly noticeable and even seem to be gloomy as the boss of the local gang Sang-doo, who also swoons over Min-kyong, strikes to get her virginity on Christmas Eve! Watch for the goofy tricks of the two to compete for Min-kyong and finally come to blows!
