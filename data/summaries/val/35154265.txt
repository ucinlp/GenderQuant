In the age of manscaping, metrosexuals, and grooming products galore-what does it mean to be a man? Oscar nominee Morgan Spurlock  and executive producers Will Arnett, Jason Bateman and Ben Silverman present a documentary featuring candid interviews from Will Arnett, Jason Bateman, Paul Rudd, Zach Galifianakis, Adam Carolla, and everyday people weighing in on everything from the obsession with facial hair to body dysmorphic disorder.
