Wannabe Los Angeles rap artists Postmaster P. , Stray Bullet  and Butch  accidentally free a Leprechaun that was imprisoned by record producer Mac Daddy O'Nassas  20 years earlier. The film follows the three as they attempt to evade the powerful Leprechaun and violent Mack Daddy, both of whom seek a magical flute with mind-altering capabilities, possessed by the young trio.
