Warren Nefron  is a klutz who cannot do anything right. He tells his psychiatrist, Dr. Pletchick , his problems. Through a series of flashbacks the viewer sees Nefron's life story. Warren is such a failure that even his many attempts to commit suicide fail. Eventually the psychiatrist uses hypnosis to cure Warren. However, although Warren is now cured, the psychiatrist has experienced transference and now has all of Nefron's problems.
