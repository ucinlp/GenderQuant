Heather Lofton  is nine years old, she comes from a very rich family, but somehow she's not so lucky, in fact her stepmother can't stand her. Heather is then forced to escape away from home, searching for her real mother. The woman the little girl has been put under the care of charges a private investigator in order to find her. But in the process, Heather's rotten stepmother frames him, he becomes wanted for kidnapping her.
