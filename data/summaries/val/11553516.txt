Before the church holiday celebrating St. Jorgen, the thief Korkis  escapes from prison and mingles with the gathering celebrants. He sees the large amounts of money being made by those hosting the celebration, who are mainly clergy. Korkis cannot refrain from getting involved in this venture. Together with an accomplice  they conceive and realize a way to fraudulently extract at least a small part of the money flowing into the hosts' coffers.
