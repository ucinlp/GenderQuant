Shaukeen has 3 lecherous old men  who decide to go away for a while to have some enjoyments in their late lifetime. They hire a driver Mithun who ends up convincing them to go to Goa where he had his lover, who works as a singer/dancer at a local club. While in Goa, the three old men get in hilarious situations with Rati as they try to impress her and try to get lucky with her, oblivious to the fact that she is their driver's lover.. Mithun Chakraborty and Rati Agnihotri also star in the film but the old men dominate this delightful comedy.
