Annarakkannanum Thannalayathu movie tells the story of Changampuzha Pavithran  who is a do-gooder who hails from the land of the great poet. Pavithran is bound to be around if a soul finds itself in distress. A bachelor who dreams about meeting his lady love some day, Pavithran's life goes topsy-turvy when an astrologer predicts that he would get married to a widow who would transform his entire life.
