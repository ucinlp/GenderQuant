Pelli Chesukundham is a family-based movie in which Venkatesh is a broad-minded company owner. Soundarya works for a local company. She accidentally sees a murder done by a city villain. She comes forward as witness for the murder. The villain rapes her and when her family learns about this, they throw her out of their house. Venkatesh responds and gives shelter in his house and helps her to forget the incident. They start to fall in love. But Venkatesh's relative Laila shows up. She is in love with him, and their parents want them to get married. The rest of the story is whom will Venkatesh marry and how.{{cite web}}
