Burglars Molly Brian  and Joe Hagney ([[John Patrick  break into the Vickers mansion on Long Island and loot the safe but are caught in the act by another crook, Jerry Winters , who takes the money from them. The three are confronted by Pious Joe McDowell  and his wife Mamie , also crooks, but who assert themselves as friends of the Vikers family. Moly, Joe, and Jerry introduce themselves in turn as Vickers' household servants. A doctor  arrives with his patient  and quarantines the house. Unknown to the first five, the Doctor and patient are also crooks who use the ruse of a "quarantine" as part of their own methodology. During the brief quarantine, Molly ends up fallng in love with Jerry and the two pledge to go straight. When the police  finally arrive, Pious Joe takes responsibility for the robbery so that Molly and Jerry can escape.
