Jeff Carter  is testing a vaccine for influenza. He is working for tycoon, Roger Graham , who takes the credit and the profit for Jeff's discovery. Roger cares more about profits than safety and tests it on Jeff's son. Jeff resigns and is blacklisted by his boss. He heads to South America to perfect the formula. Graham has used this opportunity to release the drug and romance Jeff's attractive wife, Mary . When Jeff hears that his son has died, he takes revenge.
