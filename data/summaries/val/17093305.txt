The film revolves around a group of high school and university students during their school break, and the relationships that develop . Four threads are followed: Pu  and Mai  are in competition for Nana , class geek Jo  is in love with a popular girl C  while Oh Lek  is wild about Taiwanese pop sensation Didi , and Hern  is thinking of cheating on his girlfriend Nuan  when he meets Japanese tourist Aoi .
