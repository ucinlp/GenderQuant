A criminology student, Peping , trying to make enough money for his family takes a lucrative job that is offered by a friend of his, Abyong . It turns out that he actually joined a syndicate. During a night together, to his horror the syndicate abduct, rapes and kills a woman named Madonna , butchers the body into pieces, and throws the pieces in various places out of the car. In the morning he politely asks his boss to allow him to go home. The boss understands that he has to get used to the practices, gives him some money, and lets him go.
