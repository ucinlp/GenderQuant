Visu , is a computer science student, who loves to spend time with his friends . Though he is initially portrayed as a book-worm, it is made known in later scenes that he is a happy-go-lucky youngster. Hailing from an affluent family, he lives his own life. He comes across Anjali , a computer science student. Visu falls in love with her. After locating her whereabouts, he moves close to her residence. But problems begin after he goes behind her. A series of events forces Anjali to elope. She takes the bus in which Visu travels. It appears that Visu and Anjali had eloped together, but that isn't. There are a host of interesting events and encounters between them that eventually ends on a positive note.
