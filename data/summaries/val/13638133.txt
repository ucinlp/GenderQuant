Vidal  is about to be bankrupt, so he sets up a fake car robbery with a small-time mobster to collect insurance and split the money. But his perfect plan becomes entangled by his increasingly dangerous ties to the mob and the meddling of a rogue insurance investigator , who eventually strikes a deal with Vidal and decides to team up with him and his wife  to bring down the mob and expose the corruption that links a high-ranking executive to the mafia.
