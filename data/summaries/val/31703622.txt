François  is a South African man in his forties who lives an apparently happy life. He's openly racist and homophobic, but at the same time he's sexually attracted to men and has periodic sex encounters with other white, married men. During his daughter's wedding, he meets Christian , a handsome young man and quickly becomes obsessed with him. François starts chasing Christian, learning everything he can about him. Eventually, his attraction for the young guy turns into hatred, that seems poised to explode into violence.
