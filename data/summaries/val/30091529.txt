Young and broke couple Dina  and Ron  put their unborn baby up for adoption online. They get a response from a wealthy married couple, Paul  and Maria , who are willing to pay up front for the adoption. Dina and Ron arrive at their home and are welcomed in. But, Ron and Dina have a plan in their mind. They are to take their money to buy a new home and not give them the baby. However, Maria and Paul have a plan of their own.
