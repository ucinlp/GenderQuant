Lalmohan Ganguly, alias Jatayu - a sidekick of Feluda - gets invited to Mumbai  to watch the shooting of a film based on a novel written by him. Once in Mumbai, he, along with Feluda and Topshe get embroiled in the underworld kingpins.He is seemed to have transported the Nana Sahib's necklace.But fortunately because of Feluda the real thief is caught and convicted. Although the original story was written in the 1970s, the movie is set in more recent times and depict the real problem of underworld involvement in Bollywood.
