Rick Belrow Livingston , in love with self-obsessed Broadway star Lisa , is sentenced to 30 days in jail for speeding through a small town. Quite by accident, he meets the daughter of the judge, Cindy Kimbell . He persuades her to let him out for one night, so that he can visit Lisa on the premise he is seeing his "poor sick mother"  on her birthday.
