The film focuses on rural life. Nandu is heavily indebted farmer. Rajaram, Nandu's best friend, comes to village to earn bread and butter. Rajaram, a graduated in agriculture science, feels disappointed as he did not find good job in city. He wants to make some money by selling land but his mother prevents him from doing this and the story continues. The film establishes how poor farmers are being wronged by society and government too.<ref http://www.indianexpress.com/news/touching-tale/503489/ |titleIndian Express |date6 Jan 2012 |author=Namita Niwas}} Nana Patekar lent his voice to narrate few lines at the end of this film.
