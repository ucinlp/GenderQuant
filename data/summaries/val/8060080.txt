After resigning from the CIA, Malone's car breaks down in a remote town. He takes refuge in the home of young Jo Barlow and her dad, who run a service station where the car is being repaired. Malone is soon harassed by local thugs and corrupt law officers, all of whom apparently answer to Delaney, a ruthless land baron with political ambitions. Delaney believes that Malone was sent there to kill him. Delaney's men murder a friend of Malone's, forcing a final violent showdown.
