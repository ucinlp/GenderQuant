Nariko, who works in an art gallery, becomes acquainted with Takashi, a high school student, when he helps her find her luggage. The two become friends, and Takashi falls in love with Nariko, who is involved with Kiryu. Takashi must leave when his father moves for business reasons. Two years later, Takashi returns to Tokyo to attend university, and Nariko has broken up with Kiryu.
