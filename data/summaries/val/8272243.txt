On Christmas Eve, Armand , a terrorist who has a hidden nuclear device in Washington D.C., forces the president of the United States government  to round up seven geniuses with IQs over 200. The group consists of a casino owner , a biochemist , a pro-baseball player , a seminary student , an economics professor, a painter , and a pizza delivery guy . The government is to place them in a bomb shelter and explain to the group that they are to solve the world's problems in one night; if they fail to gather a thousand point till the morning, the terrorist will detonate the hidden nuclear device planted in the basement of the 'genius lair'.
