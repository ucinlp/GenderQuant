The film follows Grace , a young woman whose irresponsible mother, Rhonda , blows her college fund on herself.Rhonda uses the money to finance Grace's younger sister, Taylor's , modeling campaign. While working a summer job, Grace meets the lonely Dorian Spitz  and they start selling drugs together for extra cash. A love triangle forms when Dorian falls for Grace, but Grace is smitten with Ben Pretzler , and Taylor develops feelings for Dorian.
