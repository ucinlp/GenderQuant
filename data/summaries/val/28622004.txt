Willy is terrified about moving into the family's new home. He's convinced it's filled with two headed space aliens out to get him. In response to his wish for someone to save him, a teddybear Gooby comes to life as a loveable, furry creature who in fact seems more frightened of the world than Willy. The two have hair-raising adventures. In the end, Gooby fulfills Willy's initial wish by bringing Willy and his father together.
