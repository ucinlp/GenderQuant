The story portrays about the King Karma Dev who gives a lot of importance to physical beauty. His mother Rajmata requests the Mahamantri Munjal to find him a nice wife. Karma Dev is often dreams of marrying an unknown beautiful woman he had once seen in a Somnath temple. Meanwhile Mrinalla is the princess of Karnataka who also dreams of marrying a king she had once seen in a Somnath temple. When Munjal happens to see a beautiful woman Mrinalla, he shows the King paints to her and she is happy to finally find the man of her dreams. So she runs away from the palace and searches Karma Dev to marry him. Soon after, Munjal shows Mrinalla\'s painting to Karma Dev, who agrees to marry her. On the day of the marriage, the King finds out she is dark and refuses to marry her. After several incidents Karma Dev realizes his mistake and at last he happily accepts Mrinalla as his Patrani.
