The kids of small-time crook Jack Charles  are put in a corrupt state-run home when their mother dies of cancer. Jack's teenage daughter Kelly  escapes the abusive home where she lives, and convinces Jack to pick up her younger brother Eddie . At first, Jack tries to dump them with their grandmother, but she is a gambler and a cheat, so the cops are after her, too. He then takes the kids to New Orleans with him, where he plans to pull off a big heist that could set him up for life.
