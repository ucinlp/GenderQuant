Simon Miller  outwardly appears to be a geologist who must frequently take business trips, which has strained the relationship with his family, including his wife Meredith  and teenage children Sarah  and Kevin . When Simon suddenly goes missing, his family searches his office and discovers several passports, each with different aliases. They soon learn that Simon was in fact a spy who kept his true career secret from them. Simon mysteriously calls home and warns his family not to tell anyone about his disappearances, then abruptly hangs up. His family begins a search for Simon, which takes them to locations around the world.
