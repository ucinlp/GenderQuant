Divakaran, son of martyr Karunakaran is well accepted in his village. He takes part in the election and becomes an MLA under the panel of Communist party. MLA Divakaran was poignant about some of his party tactics. He expressed it and firmly opposed the move to stage strikes involving students. This provoked the party leaders and become the filth in their eyes. He started fighting against the party misdeeds and soon he become alone.
