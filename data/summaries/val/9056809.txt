Honest police officer Indrajeet's life turns for the worse when he finds out that he must arrest his girlfriend's  father, Seth Din Dayal. When his girlfriend stands up for her father, Indrajeet gets offended. He eventually gets transferred. Several years later, a retired Indrajeet arranges for the marriage of his adopted daughter Neelu with Vijay. Tragically, both Vijay and Neelu are killed by Shanti's brother. Indrajeet vows to avenge Neelu's death and sets off to kill those responsible.
