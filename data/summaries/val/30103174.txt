Shroff plays Ranbir Saigal and Chaudry plays Anjali, a happily married couple who find themselves in trouble when visited by an escapee from a mental asylum, Ria Deshmukh . SOUNDTRACK- The music of Dobara, was composed by Anu Malik and all lyrics were penned by Javed Akhter. It is one of the melodious music of 2004.
