Vairam tells the story of Sivarajan  who fights for justice, as he loses his daughter Vairamani  who is raped and killed. When the law is blinded by political and monetary power, he has no other option than to hunt down Thalikkulam Josekutty  who hails from an influential Achayan family in Thodupuzha. An investigative journalist Annie Jacob  of a popular magazine digs up the whole story, which becomes a sensation. An offbeat advocate Ravi Varma  takes up the case for re-investigation.
