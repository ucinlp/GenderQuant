Mike Gallagher is one of Los Angeles' top cops, who is acting head of Hollywood's homicide division. But Gallagher is soon wrenched out of his orderly existence into a dirty world of killing and corruption. Stumbling across a vicious murder on L.A. highway Rainbow Drive, Gallagher senses something strange when reinforcements arrive even before he has called them. When his superiors freeze him out of the case, Gallagher begins his own investigations which propel him into a web of drugs and vice, corruption and cover-up - an intricate web that implicates some of the city's most senior figures.
