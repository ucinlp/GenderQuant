Within the most storied city in the world lives Jake , a homeless man who calls all of Manhattan his home. Jake discovers Cameron , a man down on his luck and sleeping in a tree in Central Park. Taking Cameron under his wing, Jake teaches him how to survive on the streets. But Jake's friendship with Cameron winds up threatening Jake's way of life, a life no one ever thought could possibly exist. A life lived with heart and spirit, and a charming embrace of the city. In this heartwarming and beautifully shot film, homelessness is shown in a new light, illustrated with a stellar performance by Ernie Hudson, alongside a number of star-studded cameos.
