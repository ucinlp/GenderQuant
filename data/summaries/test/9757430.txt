The film tells of Clara Goldstein  a Jewish woman who places a personal ad in the Buenos Aires newspaper requesting the company of an older Jewish man. Yet, her sole respondent Raul Ferraro  turns out to be a Gentile from Uruguay. Clara then spurns him, but soon she realizes she needs him. Her brother is about to visit her from Boston and she has been lying to him about being in a romantic relationship. Fortunately, Raul goes along with the ruse. Not long after, the couple fall in love.
