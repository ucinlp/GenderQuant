The main character Saša Gordić  is a talented shooter who went footsteps of older brother Igor Gordić , a 1991 Junior European shooting gold medallist. However, Igor has voluntarily gone to war in former Yugoslavia, from which he returned as a drug addict. In order to repay debt of local dealers, Igor is forced to sale their property sales, which their parents left them. So Saša decides to get revenge on everyone who destroyed life of his brother.
