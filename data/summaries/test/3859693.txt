Tati’s use of the word “trafic” instead of the usual French word for car traffic  may derive from a desire to use the same franglais he used when he called his previous film Play Time, and the primary meaning of trafic is “exchange of goods,” rather than "traffic" per se.Judith Crist, “A Honey of a Jam,” New York Magazine, Dec 11, 1972. Vol. 5, No. 50.
