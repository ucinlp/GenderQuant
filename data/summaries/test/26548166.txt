After inheriting his father's estate in the bayous of Louisiana, Corey Thorton's life is about to change forever. Through instructions left for him, Corey learns of his father's bizarre plan and wish to be resurrected from the grave. With the help of a local prostitute and voodoo temptress, Delores, Corey learns the ways of Black Magic. Now, haunted by his father and experiencing bizarre illusions, Corey is about to enter the world beyond his own imagination..the Netherworld.
