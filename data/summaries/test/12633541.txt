A young British Bohemian , who lives in Paris, marries a stage dancer . He persuades her to give up her stage career, and they take a cottage in the country. She accepts an invitation from her former manager  to attend a party. She performs a dance at the party. She quarrels with her husband, but starts searching for him in the countryside on a stormy night. As a result, she catches pneumonia and nearly dies. After being nursed back to health by her husband, she decides to give up the stage for good.
