Gnorm is just an average gnome, but he wants to impress the lady Gnomes by doing something heroic. So he steals the gnomes' magic stones and exposes them to sunlight to recharge them. When he gets to the surface , he witnesses a murder and the killer ends up with his stones. Detective Casey , who was working a sting operation with the murdered man  is blamed for botching the sting, and causing the death. Wanting to catch the killer to clear his name, he teams up with Gnorm, whom he accidentally discovers. He is going to need Samantha's  help, but she thinks he is a nut.
