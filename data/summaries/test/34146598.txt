Sid , a bachelor works as a divorce managing officer. He meets Sonali , who joins him in his business of splitting couples and they earn well in the process. Together, they are known as the Jodi  Breakers. Sometimes, they secretly make husband and wife fight with each other, that will result in their divorce. This happens with Marc  and Maggie . But when Marc finds out the reason that got him and Maggie divorced, hell breaks loose.
