Kunju Lakshmi  is the young daughter of the aristocratic Kavumpattu family in Peruvannapuram. She is pampered by her brothers and is a little arrogant. Sivasankaran  comes to her college as a clerk to replace the inefficient Padmanabhan . Kunju gets into a fight with Sivasankaran. Her brothers join the fight and Sivasankaran declares that he will marry Kunju in 14 days. Although Kunju hates Sivasankaran at that point, she later falls in love. Her brothers try to pressurize her to marry their maid's son Achu  who is actually the son of the late Vamadeva Kurupu of Kavumpattu. Ultimately, Sivasankaran and Kunju marry with Achu's help.
