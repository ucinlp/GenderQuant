Benni , Sandra , and Jule  are on a camping holiday at a sea. They meet a sinister but charming young man named Marco  while playing at the sea. Later at night, Marco is beaten by two guys for an unknown reason before Sandra comes to the rescue unintentionally. Soon, the four of them leave the camping site at night thanks to Marco's spontaneous idea. Crossing the Germany border to Poland, they have no idea what they are going to do there.
