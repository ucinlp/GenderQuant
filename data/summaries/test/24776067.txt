Ljubljana 2008. The time of the Slovenian presidency of the European Union. Aleksandra  is a 23-year-old English language student. She comes from a small town. Her parents are divorced. No one knows that Aleksandra runs personal ads under the nickname, “Slovenka”  and that prostitution is her secret source of income. She’s very good at manipulating others , is an accomplished liar and a bit of a thief. She resents her mother and regards her as being selfish. Her ambition is to escape the banality of her home town and settle in the big city, but her clandestine job leads her to a dangerous encounter with local criminals.
