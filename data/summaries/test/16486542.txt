A religious drama on the life of St. Francis of Assisi . St. Francis' early life as the son of a wealthy cloth merchant in Assisi, who gives up all his worldly goods to dedicate himself to God. Clare  is a young aristocratic woman who was so taken with St. Francis that she left her family and became a nun. By this time , St. Francis had a well-established reputation for his vows of poverty.
