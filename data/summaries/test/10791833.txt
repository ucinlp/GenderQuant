The entire movie occurs in Tirunelveli. Mandhiran , an orphan is forced to become a gangster. He works for a bigwig Sadayan . He, along with his gang, mercilessly kills innocent people, loots them of cash and jewels and kills people for money. A special team of police headed by Vellaithurai  is on their trail. Mandhiran comes across Gayathri , a Brahmin girl. After a few encounters, they both fall in love. Mandhiran helps her family win a property dispute and he earns the goodwill of Gayathri's father . The rest is a cat and mouse game between the police and Mandhiran's gang. Whether he marries Gayathri or not forms the climax.
