After the passing of their mother three years prior, Ben and Emily have driven their father David crazy by going through one nanny after another. Finally Kate Hewitt  accepts the position, mostly because the schedule allows her to pursue her dream of becoming a teacher. Primadonna ballerina Emily begins to lash out more, especially when Kate seems to fit in well with David and Ben. Soon Emily realizes how much Kate and she have in common and that Kate may be the key to having a happy family again.
