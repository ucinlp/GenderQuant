Several groups of Italians experience amusing adventures and tests of character on the Caribbean island of Antigua. Some of the characters are there simply for rest and relaxation, while others act with different motivations. Multiple plot lines unfold simultaneously as different characters' stories are presented.Mereghetti, Paolo. “Un'estate ai Caraibi nel cine-cocomero.” Corriere della Sera, June 10, 2009. In Italian.
