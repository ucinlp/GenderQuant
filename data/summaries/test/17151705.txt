Holland  is a former CIA assassin who lives quietly and peacefully on a West Indies island. He is persuaded out of retirement by the death of Jorge Hidalgo , a friend and dissident journalist. Hidalgo was murdered by Clement Molloch , a Welsh doctor who lives in Guatemala. Molloch  has made a science of the practice of torture and sells his knowledge and skills to any government that can pay his price. Holland is hired by Hector Lomelin , a professor and friend of Hidalgo. He poses as a family man  and is accompanied to Guatemala by Hidalgo's widow Rhiana  and daughter Sarah .
