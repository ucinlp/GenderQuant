A young comic book fan Todd Dougherty is upset about his widowed father re-marrying. When a series of neighbors turn up dead, Todd suspects his stepmother-to-be and accuses her of being a monster. Everyone dismisses Todd's accusations as the result of an over-active imagination. As the wedding date approaches, it becomes a race against time to reveal the truth before his father ties the knot.[allmovie website: http://www.allmovie.com/work/stepmonster-46838]
