Srirama Raju  is the zamindar of the village. Raheem , his classmate and a friend, also works as his carriage-driver. The zamindar has two daughters Savitri  and Lalitha  and a son Ravi . Savitri is a born mute, while Tulasi goes to college. Tulasi meets Kiran  who traps her in the name of love and later marries Savitri for money. Raheem's son Saleem  rescues Lalitha from Kiran. A couple of villagers who had grudges on Raheem create Hindu-Muslim clashes between the otherwise peaceful villages. How all issues were resolved and what price they had to pay to regain peace in the village as well as in their lives forms the rest of the story.
