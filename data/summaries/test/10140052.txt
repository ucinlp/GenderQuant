Coming from small-town Chiaroscuro to big city Bombay, conman and compulsive liar, Raj Malhotra , a lawyer by profession, sets up shop with fellow lawyer, Mohan. He falls in love with Sonam , daughter of a wealthy lawyer Tejpal. He devises a scheme to marry into this wealthy family, and he succeeds. Years later, he has a son, who wishes that his dad would stop lying; and a mistress, Tara. The son's wish comes true, and all utterances from Raj's mouth are truthful. This creates a crisis in his personal life, as his confesses about his mistress; as well as his professional life, when he starts telling the truth in court, especially with his underworld clients, who are now out to kill him.
