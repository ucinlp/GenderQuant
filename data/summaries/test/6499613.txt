The film stars Rik Mayall as a rock manager who recruits Jane Horrocks . The film also features Danny Aiello and Ross Boatman. Music producer Marty Starr , is infuriated to discover that the woman he turned into a household name, Marla Dorland  , is rapidly losing her fame. With debts up to his ears, Marty believes the only way to secure Marla's fame is for her to die, and thus earn Marty a fortune. The only problem is that Marla refuses to die. During her tour in the film, Mavis Davis  sings "I'm Alright" previously sung by Kenny Loggins. The title and plot reference Peckinpah's Bring Me the Head of Alfredo Garcia.
