Uphill battle faces a mixed-race couple from Boston trying to make it in the big city, with Debbie , a black woman who wants to be a singer and Tony , a white young man, who is a struggling actor, both in their early twenties. Although they both believe that race should not be an issue if two people love and respect one another, they are confronted with Debbie's family strongly disapproving the inter-racial affair and to make matters worse, Tony becomes the target of a radical Black Nationalist group who believes inter-racial relationships are a slap in the face to African-American culture.
