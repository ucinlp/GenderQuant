The first half of the movie centers around Elaine Cheng (played by [[Cindy Cheung  a single mother from Hong Kong living in the Boston suburbs and the life she provides for her two children . As a way to cope and entertain themselves the kids invent things. The mother is drawn to pyramid scheme as a way to get ahead and repeatedly loses money. Eventually her two young children are left to fend for themselves when their mother is arrested by the police. The film is loosely based on Tze Chun's own childhood,{{cite web}}
