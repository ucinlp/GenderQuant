A foreign dignitary, Wa-Lee  and his partner Chou-Sing  are shown how to pilot the last active Mech on Earth, the MRAS-2 . They later hijack it and use it to threaten the Eastern Alliance into war. The only thing standing in their way is Drake , a renegade pilot, and his friend Stumpy , who find a humanoid robot long forgotten under the city, the MEGA-1. Along with Archeologist Leda , they use the MEGA-1 to combat the MRAS-2 and prevent war.
