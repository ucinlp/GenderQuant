A Solomon Islands plantation owner, David Sheldon  becomes ill from blackwater fever following the death of many of his fieldhands die from the disease. Joan Lackland , a female soldier of fortune, arrives by schooner in the islands. Enlisting the aid of her Kanaka crew, she defends Sheldon from an attack by the natives, led by Googomy . Joan becomes David's business partner after nursing back to health and helps protect his mortgaged property from two greedy moneylenders.
