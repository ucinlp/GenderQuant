One night in Detroit during a shoot-out at a chop shop, Detroit police officer Axel Foley  sees his boss Inspector Douglas Todd  fatally shot by a well-dressed man. Axel learns that the killer's vehicle was rented using a credit card reported stolen from a man on vacation at Wonder World, a theme park in Beverly Hills, California. Axel checks out Wonder World, which is owned by "Uncle" Dave Thornton . After being spotted by security, Axel is shot at and taken to see the park's head of security, Ellis DeWald , whom Axel immediately recognizes as Inspector Todd's killer. Rosewood and Flint refuse to believe that DeWald is a killer because he works closely with the LAPD and is a close friend of Flint's. DeWald runs a counterfeiting ring that uses Wonder World as a front and was at the chop shop in Detroit to meet with associates to purchase blank printing paper. Uncle Dave is shot by DeWald and Axel is framed for his shooting. With the help of Rosewood and Flint, Axel sets out to prove his innocence by storming the park.
