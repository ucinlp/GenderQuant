A middle-aged housewife, Maria del Carmen , suddenly finds she has a gift for assembling puzzles. Unbeknownst to her husband  and two college-age sons, she begins practicing for a tournament with a man  she met through an ad in a puzzle shop. The thrill "of a woman discovering her special gift and rejoicing in it"<ref namehttp://movies.nytimes.com/2011/05/27/movies/puzzle-with-maria-onetto-review.html?refSolving Puzzles to Help with the Riddle of Midlife|lastStephen|datemovie review|publisher1|language14 January 2012|location=New York}} is just one of the surprises in store for Maria, as she starts to look differently at the pieces of her life, and to try new things.
