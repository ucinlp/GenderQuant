In this teen romance, a young neglected English boy  runs away and becomes friends with a French girl  on the same flight. Together, they go to an idyllic marsh location in France , become lovers, set up housekeeping, have a baby, and play at being responsible adults, discovering along the way many of the troubles involved, before ultimately becoming separated by police responding to a missing persons report.  {{Main}} In the 1974 sequel, Paul and Michelle, the young family is reunited and has to cope with a new love interest for the girl, and the difficulties of work and college while trying to maintain a family.
