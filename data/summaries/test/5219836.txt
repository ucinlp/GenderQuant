{{expand section}} The story begins when Leroy the Donkey, T.R. the Rooster, Rover Joe the Hound Dog and Catgut the Pussycat run away from their abusive owners Mordecai Sledge, Mean Floyd, Caleb Siles, and Farmer Lardpork and become traveling musicians. At the end of the film, their owners turn out to be robbers. Rover Joe, Leroy, T.R. and Catgut catch them and live together happily in their barn. An audio version of the story was also released.
