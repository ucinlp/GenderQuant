Shukno Lanka is the story of a Junior artist living in a tinsel town, played by Mithun Chakraborty, who is yet to taste success after a struggle of 30 years of acting career. Sabyasachi Chakraborty plays the character of Joy Sundar Sen, who is inspired by Ritwik Ghatak casts junior junior artiste Chinu Nandi a chance in the lead role in his next venture.<ref nameShukno Lanka Mumbai Mantra|urlMumbai Mantra|accessdate=13 August 2012}} The film motivates thousands of junior artistes, when the character played by Mithun gets signed for an International project as the leading man.
