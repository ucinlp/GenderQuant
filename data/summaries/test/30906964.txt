The title symbolizes everlasting love and is set in the backdrop of a music college. It tells the story of a student named Vasu , who hails from a slum. The people in the slum sponsor his education. Vrindha  comes from a rich family and she falls in love with Vasu. Newcomers Ramesh Raveendran, Niji Mary, Pradeep Chandran and Jyothi Chatterji also play important roles in the film. The story takes a dramatic turn when a girl goes missing from the college hostel.
