Ex-lawman Matt Austin  accidentally kills an innocent man  while hunting an outlaw who killed his family. He pledges to the dying man that he will take his body to his sister Amaya . Austin wrestles with the idea of telling her he caused the death, but is faced with bigger battles once at the farm. There he gets involved with land baron Horn , who is trying to take Amaya's land. Their lives are more intertwined than he thought, when he takes on Horn and discovers that Horn has hired the man he was looking for all along.
