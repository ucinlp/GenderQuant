The film tells the tale of Gabrielle Winters , a brain-damaged and institutionalized tutor and proofreader who elaborately recounts the disappearance of the sixteen-year-old girl she was babysitting. Flourish was shot in Los Angeles in May and June 2005, and had its world premiere at the 2006 Cinequest Film Festival. It was released on DVD worldwide on November 14, 2006.
