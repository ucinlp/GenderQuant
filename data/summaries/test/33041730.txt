"The Dingo" is a nickname for Harry Selby, a drunken thief from the city. He marries a country girl despite knowing that she loves someone else, Dr John Stirling. Harry breaks into Dr Stirling's house and shoots his servant dead when disturbed. His wife birth to a daughter but dies when Harry is arrested for murder. Harry spends 18 years in gaol then gets out to find his daughter has been raised by Dr Stirling. Seeing how happy his daughter is, Harry does not tell her that he is her real father and she marries a nice young man.{{cite news}}
