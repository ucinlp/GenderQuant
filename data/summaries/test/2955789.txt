A college professor, Dr. Douglas Newton , develops an experimental drug named Tryptophan  that has the unfortunate side effect of turning his students into blood thirsty zombie-like creatures leading them to eventually explode. It's up to a couple of grad students Jenny Wayne  and Joe Larsen , along with the local newspaper reporter Ray Elsworth  to uncover Dr. Newton's dark secret and thwart his diabolical plans before time runs out.
