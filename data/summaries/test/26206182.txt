Bhama falls in love with Arun , a doctor who works with her. Arun has a Muslim father and a Hindu mother who agrees to have Bhadra as their daughter in law, but Achuthankutty hates him because his father is a Muslim, causing Arun and Bhama to break up. Bhadra and Bhama are left to be take care of by their sister-in-law. She meets Krishakumar's sister Sridevi who was paralyzed after the death of her parents.
