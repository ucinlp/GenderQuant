A group of Australians are investigating a reported discovery of wolframite, a material needed for the manufacture of armaments. A plane carrying the group is sabotaged by a German agent, Mark Heinrich, and is forced down in the desert. The group survives and encounter with some aboriginals but the young pilot is killed when trying to fly the damaged plane. The others return to Sydney and find Heinrich has killed the owner of the wolfram field and kidnapped his daughter. The girl is rescued and Heinrich is arrested.
