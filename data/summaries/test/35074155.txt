Bo'ri loses his father at a young age in the 1920s. He grows up in a city and studies to become an electrician. He then returns to his village. While every girl in the village would gladly marry him, he falls in love with Khumor who is already engaged to another guy. As the Soviet-German war breaks out, Bo'ri, along with his fellow villagers, leave the village to fight in the war. He never returns.
