Victor Sartorius  is an ailing doctor working in Morocco. He teams up with Lady Yvonne Clifford  in a plot to poison her husband, Sir Charles Clifford , so he can collect the 20,000 pounds necessary to save his experiments and his funding. Roger Clifford , the son of Sir Charles has also been marked for death. The only one who can stop the murder plot of Sartorius is Nurse Eve Rowe .
