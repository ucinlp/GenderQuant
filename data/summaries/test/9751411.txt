Running from his pursuers, Cat Stevens  hides in a circus where Thomas  is working. Cat manages to escape, but Thomas' son is killed by a murderous gunman. Seeking revenge, Thomas searches for Cat, and then together go to meet with Hutch Bessy  and his mute friend Babydoll. Here Cat explains to Hutch the situation in which their friend Sharp is found. A gold mining community is oppressed by a criminal organisation who claim the gold and property for themselves when a yearly visit from the Judge arrives. Aided by Mami's circus, the four manage to finish off the criminals and inform the judge of the situation.
