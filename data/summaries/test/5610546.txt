A small group of Roman Catholics believe that Satan intends to become man just as God did in the person of Jesus. Writings from a seemingly possessed mental patient lead them to Peter Kelson . The group suspect that it is his body Satan has chosen to occupy. The youngest of the group Maya Larkin , meets Peter to investigate further and to try to convince him of embodied evil. Other signs come to him as he and Maya take a journey full of strange occurrences, self discovery and an ultimate showdown.
