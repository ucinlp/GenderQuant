Main Julian England is about a young man named Aftab , who is getting married in England and plans to stay there. It carries on from part one  where Aftab is rejected by his cousin Tina who wants to marry one of his friends Shahid "Shedu"  instead. Then Aftab's aunt  promises to Aftab and his mother that she will get him married in England, and so Main Julian England shows "Shedu" and Tina getting married and Aftab getting married to a girl living in England, and another friend of theirs, Mithu , also getting married and planning to move to England. Great comedy brews as the three childhood friends begin planning their move to England.
