Four episodes set in/around a Tuscan farm. The narrator  daydreams about the many men he's longed for: Mark and Pietro in the first flashback, Jean Franko and Alexy Tyler cruising each other by the pool, Roberto Giorgio and Ethan Clarke messing around in their bedroom, Lucas Andrades and Glenn Santoro at the trattoria. In the final flashback, Samuel reminisces about his affairs with Mark Federico.
