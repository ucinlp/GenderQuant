A inmate named Salem  escapes from an insane asylum in the dead of winter. His destination is the house of his sister, Ester , and her husband, Anton . His quest is revenge as he sets about framing Anton because Salem feels he was set up for a horrendous axe murder two years previously. On the case is an Inspector , who must determine if Anton is really crazy for claiming he saw Salem outside of the asylum and discover if Salem was really framed.
