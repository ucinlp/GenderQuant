The film takes place in 1978 in Málaga , and depicts the life of teenager Miguelito Dávila , who, after suffering from kidney disease and spending some time at the hospital, has learned such classic poetry as Dante's Divine Comedy and dreams of leaving his job at a hardware store and pursuing his dream of becoming a poet. One summer, he hangs out with his childhood friends Babirusa , Paco Frontón  and Moratalla , until he meets a girl called Luli  at the swimming pool, and the two start dating. Luli would love to become a professional dancer, and is best friends with "La cuerpo" , who fancies Miguelito's posh friend Paco. The two couples spend time together swimming, and gradually they experiment with other distractions. Miguelito meets later an older teacher , who is interested in his talent, and begins an affair with her around the same time that Cardona ([[Antonio Garrido , an older and apparently richer man, starts courting Luli with the promise of helping her career as a dancer.
