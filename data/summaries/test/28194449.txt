The film is set in Italy in 1984, 1991, 2001 and 2009 and jumps back and forth in time. Mattia and Alice  are both traumatized, and considered weird by the people around them. When he was a child, Mattia's retarded twin sister Michela died while he was supposed to look after her. He is intelligent and becomes a successful scientist, but he sometimes cuts himself on purpose. When Alice was a child she had a skiing accident; she has a scar on her thigh. As a teenager she is bullied by female classmates. They meet while teenagers and become friends, but lose contact when Mattia goes to work in Germany. Eight years later Alice writes him a very brief request to come back to Italy, which he does, and they reunite.
