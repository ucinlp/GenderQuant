Frank , a librarian in the United Kingdom, falls in love with a mysterious American dancer named Miranda . She suddenly disappears, and he tracks her down in London, finding out that she is actually a con artist. He leaves her, returning to Northern England. Miranda and her boss  are in business selling buildings that they don't own to unwitting customers. After making a big score, her boss leaves her, and the customer  seeks revenge against Miranda. Frank realizes that he should not have left her, and returns to London, saving her.
