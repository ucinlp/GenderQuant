Twelve girls are abandoned by their parents because they are too poor to take care of them. The twelve daughters are rescued by an ogress  in disguise who promises to take care about them as her own daughters. Phra Rodasan , the only surviving son of the twelve sisters, goes on a quest to the ogre kingdom in order to heal his mother and his aunts' blindness. There he falls in love with the ogress' daughter, Meree.Phra Rot Meri  movie poster
