The story unfolds in London, where Sanjay , narrates about his 'kind' of girl, and talks about love. Cut to flashback, the hero meets Pooja. He tries to impress her in all possible ways. But his hopes are shattered when he comes to know that she is getting ready for a marriage with a NRI youth. Then comes Aarthi , a dance tutor. She is a girl with traditional ideas but modern outlook. Unfortunately, the hero's attempts to settle down with her gets jinxed. Then enters Anu, Sanjay's colleague. Does he finds her apt for him forms the rest.
