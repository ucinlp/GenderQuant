During the time Hong Kong was under British rule, there was a dark age when corruption and bribery were the order of the day. Chinese Chief Inspector Lak  together with his gang, Unicorn ([[Anthony Wong , Gale  and Gold  laundered massive sums thereby making Hong Kong an empire of graft. Whenever they failed to apprehend the felons, Unicorn would get innocent victims, like Bong ([[Alex Fong , to admit to the crimes. Gale had nine "wives", all of them were actually mistresses of other constables, only Lily  was loyal to him, but Gale was fascinated by the female drug lord, Rose . Lak was found having an affair with Unicorn's mistress . Unicorn beat up Lak and was demoted to stand guard at a reservoir. In the early 1970s, the Governor of Hong Kong decided to clean up the police force. The ICAC was established, whose operation branch was headed by Yim . Bong and Unicorn also joined the ICAC. Despite threats of violence and intimidation, they managed to bring about the downfall of the empire of graft.
