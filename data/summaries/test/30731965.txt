Mark Anderson who was a recent victim of a stroke and his best friend Thomas a drag queen embark on a search for a new partner for Mark's son James Anderson.While Billy a young teen who just turned 18 gets his very first kiss from his friend Rick gets caught by his abusive father and is thrown out from his house.With no place to go and no money Billy hitch hikes to the big city. Billy who is confused, lost and scared, Billy soon finds a help wanted ad posted in a local cafe and lands himself a job as a caretaker. Billy soon realizes the responsibility of being on his own as well as the living as a homosexual male.
