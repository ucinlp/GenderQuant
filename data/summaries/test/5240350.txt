In 1932 Texas, Wilma McClatchie  takes over her late lover's bootlegging business, but it isn't lucrative. She meets up with a bank robber, named Fred , who invites her and her two daughters in on his next big heist. After meeting a refined-yet-dishonest gambler , Wilma recruits him along with Fred and her daughters to kidnap the daughter of a millionaire in the hopes of getting rich off the ransom.
