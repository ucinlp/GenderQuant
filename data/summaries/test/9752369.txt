The film tells of Alfredo Díaz  a cop who falls in depression after discovering that his wife has been cheating on him. Mariano Silverstein , a psychologist, tries to help Alfredo deal with his problem. When Alfredo is invited to dinner to Silverstein's home, he unmasks Silverstein's wife, who also has an affair, which causes the psychologist and the cop to switch roles. Díaz takes Silverstein with him on many adventures and both have to unravel a conspiracy of national proportions.
