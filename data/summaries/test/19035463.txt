Jay Verma  lives in Bombay. He is an honest, unemployed youth who cannot arrange for his mothers treatment. He happens to meet underworld goons looking for such frustrated people and they force Jay into the world of terrorism. R.B the uncrowned king of terrorrism, orders Jay to be killed when he revolts. Jay flees to Sharjah and undergoes plastic surgery to begin a new life. He returns to India as Ajay Kahnna , meets his ex-lover Radha  and marries her. They have a baby boy, Ravi Khanna.
