A young woman named Elaine  and her husband, David Walker  meet a lonely fifteen year-old, Chet Becker,  in an ice-cream shop. Over time, the couple becomes friendly with Chet, who develops a crush on Elaine that culminates in a one night stand. Elaine later confesses her infidelity to David, and their marriage is destroyed by the affair. The movie also represented the debut of Bryce Dallas Howard.
