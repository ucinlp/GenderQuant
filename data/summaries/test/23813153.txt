It concerns Antoine , who must leave Lyon to help his mother  who runs the family grocery store in a village in southern France. His father , stricken with a heart attack, can no longer drive the truck that supplies the isolated hamlets. Antoine discovers the charm of these people, "bons vivants"  ways and gradually comes to love his eccentric customers and finds the country of his childhood. He also discovers the joy of living and perhaps the joy of love with Claire . The film uses the beautiful landscape of Mont Sainte-Victoire as a regular backdrop.
