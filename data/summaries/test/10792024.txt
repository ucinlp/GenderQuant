Sri Devi is born into a rich royal family. One day she goes to the temple with her mother and father. At that time her uncle kills her parents but she escapes but falls from a bridge into a river. After 25 years Sri Devi's grandmother is in search of her granddaughter because she wants to give all her money before she dies and keep the royal family going. The uncle gets Rajni to find a girl like Sri Devi to inherit.
