Hugo Dugay  runs a small company, Hugo Pool, that cleans swimming pools in Los Angeles. The film covers one day in her life, during which she must clean many pools in the midst of a drought that interferes with her usual water supply. In addition to dealing with several eccentric customers, including mobster Chick Chicalini ([[Richard Lewis  and filmmaker Franz Mazur , Hugo must care for her needy parents Minerva  and Henry . Also, Hugo may be falling in love with Floyd Gaylen , a customer of hers who has ALS.
