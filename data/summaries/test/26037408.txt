Ms Liu, a compulsive gambler, meets Mrs Bud Lung's son, Bud Pit, on a cruise ship. He tries to help her win but his girlfriend Moon is unhappy, because Liu neglects her dying grandmother due to her gambling addiction. Liu visits the Bud family for fortune-telling and help with winning, but Mrs Bud Lung gives her other alternatives instead. She is told to go to her parents' graves to clear weeds, but she put the rubbish on nearby graves. The spirits of the dead are angered and they haunt her.
