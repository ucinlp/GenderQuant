Four men are seen in a car apparently about to commit a serious crime. How each of the previously law abiding men came to be in this position is then explored. Mike  is an ageing boxer who is injured and unable to find work. Joe  is an out-of-work clerk who needs to fly to the United States with his young wife  to escape her clinging and unstable mother.
