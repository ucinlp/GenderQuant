Joe One-Way  is serving a life sentence for the passion murder of his teenage bride Kimba . At the urging of his cellmate Clinique , he composes the play White Man: Black Hole about his crime. Joe had been incarcerated 18 years, before Hollywood agent Markie Mark , who wishes to make a film of the play, arranges that Joe gets an early release from prison. Wanting to create a great deal of media buzz, he brings Joe into the his offices to rewrite the stage play in order to give it greater audience appeal. There, Joe meets Fleur , Markie's arm candy. Learning she had been a fan of his for some time, the two begin a torrid affair. Joe then searches for a way to reconcile the crime for which he had never forgiven himself.
