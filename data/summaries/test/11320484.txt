Kasi  comes to Mumbai to track his parents' killers. He is successful in finding the first two. In order to find the third one he joins a goon Ravi Bhai . This Ravi Bhai is in loggerheads with his own brother, Mani Bhai . Mani Bhai runs another gang and the leading goonda in this gang is Siva . Arjun and Siva knew each other from a meeting in jail. But now they both work for enemies hence they are now enemies. Arjun gets into an argument with Ravi Bhai and Siva gets into an argument with Mani Bhai and they both leave the gangs and unite.
