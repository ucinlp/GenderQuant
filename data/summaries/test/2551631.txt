Tracy  Stokes  is a 23-year old undercover cop and goes to the wealthy private Westbury School to figure out the death of a student. He becomes friends with Rob Donovan  and soon figures out that Donovan has been stealing the cars at parties. Near the end, it is revealed that the principal  of the school had been blackmailing Donovan, thus he is the ringmaster of the crimes. After being kicked off the force by his father-figure police chief , Trey solves the case  and is reinstated.
