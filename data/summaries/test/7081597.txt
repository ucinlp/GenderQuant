Sang-Do  is a fast talking crystal meth dealer who considers himself more of a businessman than a criminal. Sang-Do has been involved in the drug business all of his life. His uncle was a drug dealer , specializing in manufacturing crystal meth. Sangdo’s mother died when one of his uncle’s meth labs blew up in an accident. Do Jin-Gwang  is a hard nosed cop that doesn’t always play by the rules. Doh’s partner died 4 years ago, while trying to take down the top drug dealer  in Busan. Since his partner's death, Do has been driven to arrest Jang-Chul and this time he plans to use Sang-Do to finally take him down.
