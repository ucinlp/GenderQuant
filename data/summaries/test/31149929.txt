The film tells the story of Rudran Gurukkal, the lone male descendant of the Chathothu family. The family represents the Yogi community, in which Lord Shiva is believed to have been born. Rudran is haunted by a strange kind of destiny. This forms the crux of the story.Unni R. Nair. . "Karmayogi: Hamlet retold". Screen India. Retrieved April 26, 2011.
