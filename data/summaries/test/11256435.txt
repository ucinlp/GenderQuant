He also discovers that some of his police officers in his station have been corrupted and are secretly working with Brijbhan. To help him in his battle against Brijbhan he sets out to bring together two warring gang leaders Karim  and Hari  and . Karim and Hari are longtime enemies who constantly fight in gang wars because of their different religion backgrounds. Once Amar persuades them to put aside their differences and they join him.
