An American reporter, Sara Scott  is working in Europe during World War II and begins an affair with a British reporter named Mark Trevor . Sara is conflicted on whether to marry her rich boss or the charming young reporter she is having an affair with. Finally, she chooses Mark, only to find that he is married and has a son. The two separate shortly thereafter, then decide to stay together and work out their problems. After the war ends, Mark Trevor is killed in an accident, sending Sara into mourning. Her boss, after a few months, convinces her to come back to the United States and work for him. Before she goes, she visits her old lover, Mark's hometown and ends up living with her ex-lover's wife and child.
