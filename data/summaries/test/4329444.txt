The film follows a diverse group of mostly middle-class Los Angelenos through the emotional ups and downs in their flawed yet very human lives, each loosely connected to each other through a restaurant. In the first story, Mamie  reluctantly agrees to work with a would-be young filmmaker  in order to locate the now grown son she secretly gave up for adoption after becoming pregnant from her stepbrother Charley  &ndash; who is later revealed to be gay &ndash; 19 years earlier. In the second story arc, her stepbrother, and his domestic partner, Gil  are deciding whether or not to confront their friends, a lesbian couple , regarding the paternity of their son. And in the third, a young man, Otis,  is involved with a band and trying to keep his father, Frank ([[Tom Arnold , from learning that he is gay, while also dealing with the seemingly gold-digging woman, Jude , who inserts herself into their lives.
