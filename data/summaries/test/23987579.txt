Drylanders is set in Canada at the turn of the century. Daniel Greer  returns home after the Boer War to find city life not to his liking. Instead,he opts for the life of a wheat farmer. At first, his farm is prosperous, but he becomes victim to a nationwide drought. He struggles to keep his farm afloat, but dies before he could see the end of the drought.
