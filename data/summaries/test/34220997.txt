The story starts with the death of an army officer  occurs and it is believed to be the act of a terrorist group headed by Changezi . Colonel Balbir Singh Sodhi  is asked to investigate this matter and he ends up discovering that minister Veerbhadra Singh is involved in this conspiracy. Colonel Sodhi stages his own death in an attempt to kill Changezi and starts living as Dadabhai/ Devraj Hathoda in Mumbai. Major Ajit Arya  is sent in the guise of a Bengali journalist to find out the true identity of Dadabhai/Devraj Hathoda. Once Arya discovers the truth, he joins hands with Colonel Sodhi to bring the minister to justice and terminate the terrorist group.
