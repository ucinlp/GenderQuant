Faten Hamama plays Mona a university student who falls in love with her professor, Mahmoud , who is also a gifted writer. Mahmoud proposes to Mona but her mother refuses and Mona marries another man and leave Egypt. Years later after she returns to Egypt, Mona visits a sick Mahmoud in a hospital.
