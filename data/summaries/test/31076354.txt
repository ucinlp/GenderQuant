Betty Andrews , although innocent, is convicted of a theft of department stores and, despite the efforts of his lawyer Mary Ellis  and a young journalist, Jim Brent , Betty is sentenced to a year in the Curtiss House of Correction. The Chief Matron  is very severe. Betty has the support of the prisoners "The Duchess" Frankie Mason  and Nita Lavoro . When ten girls are allowed to go home for Thanksgiving Day, promising that they have to return, "The Duchess" Frankie, is angry about losing her privilege to Betty, and blames her for it. "The Duchess" arranges for Betty to be kidnapped, and Betty can not return at the appointed time.
