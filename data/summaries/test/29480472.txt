Antony Starr stars as John Drean, a park ranger who's marriage to his wife Ana  begins to suffer when their daughter, Pearl  goes missing under his care. Four years later, John is still looking for his little girl and his life seems to be at a standstill, while Ana is in a relationship with John's best friend, and the policeman behind Pearl's disappearance, David . Things begin to change for the better, however, when John's father, George  believes he saw Pearl walking past a shop window.
