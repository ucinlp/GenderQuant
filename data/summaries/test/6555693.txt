Emmannuelle Prevert  relieves the boredom of a flight on Concorde by seducing timid Theodore Valentine . She returns home to London to surprise her husband, the French Ambassador, Emile Prevert  but first surprises the butler, Lyons ([[Jack Douglas . He removes her coat only to find she has left her dress on the 'plane! The chauffeur, Leyland , housekeeper, Mrs Dangle  and aged boot-boy, Richmond  sense saucy times ahead ... and they are right! Emile is dedicated to his bodybuilding, leaving a sexually frustrated Emmannuelle to find pleasure with everyone from the Lord Chief Justice  to chat show host, Harold Hump . Theodore is spurned by Emmannuelle, who has genuinely forgotten their airborne encounter and despite reassurances from his mother , Theodore exacts revenge by revealing Emmannuelle's antics to the Press. However, after a visit to her doctor , she discovers she is pregnant and decides to settle down to a faithful marriage with Emile ... and dozens of children.
