Two Adivasi tribes are in conflict for some local economic rights. The daughter of the Nagi tribe's chief, Mala  vows to kill the son of the Ragi tribe's chief, Sanatan  as revenge. Trespassing the enemy territory, she is mesmerized by music of a flute . Coming closer, she finds out that the singer is Sanatan. They fall in love, but find it difficult to appease the enemity between the two tribes and to resist to the attempts of the villain Prabir  to marry the heroine.
