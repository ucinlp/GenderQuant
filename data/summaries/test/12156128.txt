After a group of jewel thieves pull off a daring robbery, their getaway car runs out of fuel in the middle of the countryside. They wander into a backwoods farm, hoping to hide out for the time being. However, when the farmer returns home only to find the thieves taking over the house, he hatches a deadly plan. One confrontation leads to another between the gang and the farmer, in a plot which combines action, comedy, and horror. Who will survive a weekend on the Demented Death Farm? The city-slick jewel thieves or the country-smart farmers? The answer is sure to shock you!
