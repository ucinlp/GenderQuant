The film is set in the mountainous regions of the western Hunan province in the early 1980s. At the film's start, a young man  begins his first journey as a postman at the mountainous rural areas of the aforesaid regions. His father , a veteran postman forced to retire due to a bad knee, decides to accompany him together with the family's faithful dog, Buddy. The father walks his son through the nitty-gritty of the job, and the son realizes the mailman job entails not just the sending of letters. He witnesses his father's deep friendship with the villagers, and participates in a wedding celebration with the Dong people. The film includes a number of memory flashbacks, as well as many pop songs played on the son's transistor radio .
