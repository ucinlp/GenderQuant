Bobby Ray Jacobs , a Southern man, is the sole remaining Caucasian dishwasher in America, and is surprisingly famous for it. The dishwasher career is a legacy for his family, starting with his grandfather. The film depicts several interviews with Bobby Ray and many of his friends, including Rudy Valentine , his agent, and Tamara Swanson , his British girlfriend, which explain how he got his job and where he got to be where he is today.
