Bheem and his friends are invited to spend their vacations in Kathmandu, the capital city of Nepal, by a cousin of Jaggu- the talking monkey in hi best friend circle. Their trip is not without mishap, as they soon find themselves in a situation where a young ape separated from its father, is being pursued by an evil group of jaguars who intend to capture it to take advantage of its spiritual powers. Bheem and his friends, with help from a group of wild deers, defeat the jaguars, rescue the ape and unites it with its father, who was on a rampage because of his child being kidnapped. Critics' Take
