Petty thief Nick Robey  botches a robbery, leaving his partner Al  severely wounded as Nick escapes with over $10,000. Meeting bakery worker Peg Dobbs  in friendly conversation, when Peg takes Nick to her family's apartment, he decides to take the family hostage until he can escape. As a manhunt for Nick begins outside, the robber becomes increasingly paranoid. Meanwhile, Peg schemes to sacrifice herself for the safety of her family.
