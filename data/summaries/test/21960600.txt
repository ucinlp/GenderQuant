Zetterstrøm  once had a love affair with a woman , then left her and forgot all about his past, in favour of his musical career. While he is away from Copenhagen, a supernatural incident occurs, making the central part of Copenhagen inaccessible, over time it becomes impossible to enter the area due to some invisible shield. When Zetterstrøm returns to Copenhagen, he is oblivious of this incident, and is guided by a mysterious man  into the zone. Inside the zone, Zetterstrøm must re-experience his unresolved and traumatic love affair.
