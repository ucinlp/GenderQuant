Won Ryu-hwan , an undercover North Korean spy, is dispatched by his bosses to South Korea as part of a group of three agents who pose as handsome high school students aspiring to be singers, and they embark on a somewhat absurd project to debut as an idol boy band "Flower Boy Idol Trio."{{cite web}}{{cite web}} The three spies undergo an identity crisis after adjusting to the new environment and becoming part of a poor neighborhood in Seoul, while their North Korean army instructor Kim Tae-won crosses the border to eliminate spies who refuse to follow the government's orders.{{cite web}}
