The story is a thriller that revolves around the Lebanon family who live at Mark’s Priory. Lady Lebanon  tells her son, Lord William Lebanon  that he must marry his cousin Isla Crane  to continue the family line. However, William has no intention of marrying Isla and matters are made more complicated due to Isla falling in love with architect, Richard Ferraby , who has come to Mark’s Priory to draw up renovation plans. At the same time, the strange behavior of two footmen and the family physician  add to the mystery surrounding the family and eventually rumor and speculation lead to a murderous conclusion. In 2008, the film was released on DVD by Odeon Entertainment as part of their 'Best of British' collection. Prior to this release, the film had not been seen in public since its original release.
