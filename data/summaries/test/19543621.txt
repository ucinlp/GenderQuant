The film focuses on a group of college students on a hiking trip who are visited at an evening campfire by a strange man who tells them a bizarre ghost story. The tale involves a 19th century farmer whose skull was crushed in a brutal assault. According to the stranger's story, the farmer was rescued by a scientist who made him the subject of a medical experiment involving the reconstruction of his skull. But the experiment went awry and the farmer became an indestructible homicidal maniac.AllMovie Guide/New York Times overview
