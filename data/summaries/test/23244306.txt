The film depicts an evil scientist's  campaign to achieve eternal youth, through synthesizing a drug derived from human pituitary fluid. In extracting the fluid, he creates mindless zombies from the donors. Because the local town residents are in on the plot, to achieve immortality, they help the scientist, by abducting visitors who come through town.<ref nameMick Martin, Marsha Porter |titleBallantine Books |yearrevised |isbn9780345391964}}
