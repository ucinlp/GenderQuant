Sri  is a playboy. All he does is to seduce the girls into his bed and leave them frustrated by doing nothing. He is a woman hater and he has a few strong reasons to be one. After encounters with loads of girls, he meets Shanthi . Shanthi is a tough nut to crack and she always gives him retorts by not getting attracted to him. The entire film revolves around how Sri tries to seduce Shanthi and how she gives a slip to Sri and make him beg for more. In the final scene it is revealed that Shanthi has a cousin sister  who was cheated by Sri in the past. And it ends with Sri marrying that girl.
