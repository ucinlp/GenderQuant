The powers of darkness converge to claim the soul of a young girl who may have made a deal with the devil. After a devastating car accident claims the life of her father, Michelle wakes up in a hospital with no recollection of the events leading up to the traumatic tragedy. Subsequently haunted by a fiery series of hellish nightmares and terrified by the Mephistophelean force that commences to take the lives of all who surround her, Michelle desperately attempts to unlock the mystery of her malevolent past before it's too late and the legions of the damned come calling her name.
