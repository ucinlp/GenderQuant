The film's plot involves Danny Mitchell , a young kid in the American town of Lawtonville, who is grieving over the loss of his dog. He is also struggling to adjust to his new stepmother, Ann , and has a difficult relationship with his father  - causing him to call on a psychiatrist  for assistance. However, Danny befriends Rusty, a ferocious German shepherd who was brought to america from Germany during World War II. Having worked a police dog for the Gestapo, however, Rusty is ill-tempered and Danny struggles to train him. A subplot involves two Nazi saboteurs  who arrive in Lawtonville, attempting to evade the Coast Guard and blow up an installation. They ultimately try to take Rusty by speaking to him in German.
