The film stars Saskia Burmeister, as Erica "Yuk" Yurken, an adolescent brunette who fantasises about a better life and stardom; and Delta Goodrem as her school rival Alison Ashley. At school, Erica is not very popular. She sits alone in class, but when Alison arrives, it all changes. Erica at first is desperate to be Alison's friend but soon changes her mind, and they then become rivals. They continuously have arguments throughout the year, especially when Erica kisses Barry, Alison's love interest, but, eventually, the two become friends at camp when Erica discovers that Alison does not live the perfect life she imagined.
