Lorna Denver  manages Kangaroo Flat sheep station and is pursued by two men, evil Oswald  and handsome young surveyor, Tom Wilson . Lorna gives shelter to a baby that has survived an attack by aboriginals, but Tom thinks the baby is hers. This upsets Lorna who breaks it off with him. Oswald is murdered and Tom is arrested. A Chinese cook reveals that the real killer was the father of a woman who had been seduced by Oswald.
