In 1888, Jack the Ripper is on his killing spree. Scotland Yard Inspector O'Neill  welcomes a visit from his old friend New York City detective Sam Lowry  who agrees to assist with the investigation. Sam becomes attracted to modern woman Anne Ford  but her guardian, Dr. Tranter  doesn't approve. The police slowly close in the killer as the public becomes more alarmed. The killer's identity is revealed and the killer meets a ghastly end.
