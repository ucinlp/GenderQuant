The story centers around the rivalry between two families headed by Bijay Mohanty & Snigdha Mohanty. hero Raj (SabyaSachi Misra is the only son of Bijoy Mohanty and heroine Debajani (Megha Ghosh is the daughter of Snigdha Mohanty. Raj & Debajani are studying in the same college. Both pretend to fall in love in order to seek revenge for their own families. But gradually both found they are indeed in love each other & decided to marry.
