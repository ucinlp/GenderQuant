Rajni is a big-time thief robbing money from the rich. He is capable of opening any lock however big it is. His girlfriend Madhavi is a singer and a dancer in a club. One day Rajni get caught by Sivaji, a police officer. He goes to prison for one year. There he meets Vijaykumar who lost all his wealth by a Sundarsan, who used to be poor but used his brains to make Vijaykumar's sister fall for him. Vijaykumar wants Rajni to rob the house where Sundarsan has hidden the money.
