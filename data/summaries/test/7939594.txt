Pampered and sickly young millionaire Dan Forrester  joins the army, helped and hindered by his gardener Stan Laurel and his chauffeur Oliver Hardy. While there, he falls in love with a woman, Ginger Hammond , who runs a photography store, but is equally the attraction of Forrester's commanding officer, Sgt. Hippo .
