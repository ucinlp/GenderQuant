 :Life in Portales, New Mexico isn’t easy for high school bad girl Lily . Weekends mean endless cruising until the cough syrup kicks in. The boy she loves won’t commit, she’s surrounded by strident born-again Christians, and her budding sexuality is starting to earn her a reputation. :To top it off, while others in her Sunday school class report visions of Jesus as Ronald Reagan or Leonardo DiCaprio, Lily’s personal savior appears to her as a gangly, Spanish-speaking migrant worker. Oh well, she figures, any boyfriend in a pinch.
