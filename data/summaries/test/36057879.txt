Subash , a good-for-nothing youth, enjoys his life as much as possible. His brother, Rajasekhar ([[Siddique , is a minister and his father  is a court judge. Subash falls in love with Savitri , a middle class Brahmin girl. Savitri loves him but she wants to marry with a braveman so Subash joins the army and becomes a military officer. Upon his return, his family reveals that Savitri and her father die in an accident. Arumugasamy , an honest politician, thought being Rajasekhar's opposite party is Rajasekhar's best friend, dies mysteriously. Subash suspects Thangamani , Arumugasamy's right hand, for Arumugasamy's death. Later, he finds Savitri in Thangamani's house and saves her. Savitri discloses that Rajasekhar is a corrupted politician and is linked to the terrorists. Rajasekhar's henchmen killed Arumugasamy and Thangamani began to sequester her. Subash swears to punish his brother and saves his country.
