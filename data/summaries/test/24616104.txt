An advertising executive, Aditya/Adi  has a beautiful wife  at home and a beautiful mistress . This secret affair is the catalyst for much of the plot. Adi ends up getting into a scuffle with a bunch of gangsters led by Ramanbhai, . To add to all the tension, Ramanbhai himself is under quite some duress because his colleague, Vilas Pandey is after his life.
