{{advert}} A secret government lab run by Dr. Stravinsky  has been trying to create the ultimate killer using the DNA of infamous killers Jeffrey Dahmer  and John Wayne Gacy , but there's one big problem: they've escaped. Bloody mayhem stretches across the United States as they go on the ultimate killing spree.
