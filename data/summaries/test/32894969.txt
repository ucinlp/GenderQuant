An adventuress, Cora Williams  destroys the happy marriage of Howard  and Mabel Wilson  and drives them to divorce. Howard gets custody of their children Jessie  and Frankie  but Mabel winds up abducting them. Five years later Cora discovers Mabel living in poverty with the children. She tries to poison Mabel and frame Jessie on a charge of theft but is unsuccessful. Howard and Mabel eventually reconcile and live with their children.
