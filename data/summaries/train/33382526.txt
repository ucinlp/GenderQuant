Kony is an inspiritational story of a coach and his trainee Kony who fight all odds to achieve their aims. Khidda  is a swimming coach who teaches swimming to underprivileged kids. He picks Kony  from a slum of Calcutta and grooms her to be part of the Bengal Swimming team to compete at the National Swimming Championship. However, politics, poverty and social stigma emerges as distinct roadblocks in their path.<ref nameLessons for the Indian Left from Kony|urlhttp://www.pragoti.in/|accessdate=11 April 2012}}
