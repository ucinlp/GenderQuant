Tommy Spinelli , is a wiseguy hired by a pair of dimwitted hit men to transport a duffel bag full of decapitated heads across the country to a crime boss . While on a commercial flight, his bag is accidentally switched with that of Charlie Pritchett , a friendly, talkative, young American tourist who is going to Mexico to see his girlfriend Laurie  and her parents ([[George Hamilton . The film revolves around Spinelli harassing Charlie's friends Ernie  and Steve  for information, while Charlie and Laurie attempt to get rid of their rather unfortunate luggage. The film ends with Charlie and Laurie bringing a severed head to the airport, in order to prove her father's innocence. Benny and Rico try to intervene, but end up getting arrested. It is revealed that Tommy and Charlie set them up, as Tommy departs to Hawaii. Steve goes insane by running around the airport, telling security guards that it is his "best friend." During the credits, Charlie and Laurie get married with her mother and father present, Steve is now placed in a strait-jacket, Fern is also present after being thrown out of a van, Ernie is now a brain surgeon, and Tommy is enjoying his retirement.
