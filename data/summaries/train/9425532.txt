The story follows Arjun , a dutiful police officer and the brother-in-law of acclaimed Jailer Raghuvir Singh . He is in love with Geeta , and they get married, and turn proud parents. Arjun soon gets involved in the bad books of a notorious gangster named Nagraj . Arjun starts working undercover in Nagraj's gang, but soon enough, Nagraj is informed about Arjun's real identity, and Arjun is brutally killed. When Geeta finds out she decides to avenge Arjun's death. When Nagraj finds out that Geeta is out for vengeance, he scoffs at her, refusing to believe that a lone defenseless widow can do him any harm. But Nagraj is in for a surprise when he comes face to face with Geeta and a group of young convicted men on the run who are dedicated to bring him down - even if they die trying.
