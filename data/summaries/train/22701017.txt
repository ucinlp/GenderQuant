The story starts with Jhilmil Rao a ladyand Virag Rao  buying a new house in the suburbs of Mumbai. Jhilmil, a gynacologist, works late just like her husband Virag &mdash; they're hardly there for their little son. The problem comes when he starts making imaginary friends. At least that's what Jhilmil and Virag think. But their son starts getting obessed with his "friends". Jhilmil becomes worried and tries finding out why this is happening. It is revealed that his "imaginary friends" are ghosts who have been telling their son to move out of their house. Will Jhilmil and Virag realise this before it's too late?
