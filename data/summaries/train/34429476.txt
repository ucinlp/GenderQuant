The film center's on a happily married couple, the husband runs into his wealthy high school sweetheart and sparks fly. The protagonist is convinced she is his wealth maker, but he has one huge problem, he needs to sign an agreement, for rupees one crore. For this, he requests his wife to get divorced with him legally, but wishes to live with her as his soul mate. But his emotionally blackmailed, wife will not let him go out of the marriage, and the consequence's, lead to the frustrated wife, who wishes to invite, her family and friends for their divorce.
