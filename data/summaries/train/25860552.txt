After an explosion at an army base that kills several soldiers, President Jonathan Hayes  attends a memorial service and is shot while speaking. It is revealed that a militia rebel group has resurfaced with a vengeance to assassinate President Jonathan Hayes for the death of their brothers. Agent Alex McGregor , now the head of the Secret Service, tries to prevent the attack on the President's life, but when the President is shot and agent McGregor's husband Grant Coleman , is kidnapped, the stakes are raised, and Alex realizes she has become a target as well.
