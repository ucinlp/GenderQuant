Brahmasthram tells the story of a boatman, whose only daughter Sindhu is the victim of ragging and rape by some rich recalcitrant students in medical college. No one supports Sindhu as the culprits are from very rich families. Due to pressure from Sindhu and her father, Superintendent of Police Indrajith is appointed to investigate the case.
