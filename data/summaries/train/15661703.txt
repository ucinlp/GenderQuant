Bhai  is a notrious mafia don in Hyderabad who masks his true nature by doing some good deeds for society. Pandu  is his right hand. Ajay  is Pandu's son. Ajay is a studious guy who wants to become an IAS officer. He clears the civils examination too. Hussain  is a cop whose mission is to eliminate the mafia gangs in Hyderabad. Though son and father share a caring personal relationship, Ajay and Pandu fall out due to the latter's association with Bhai. The rest of the story is all about how the system forces Ajay to tread the wrong path in order to avenge the death of his father, and to eliminate Bhai and his mafia network.
