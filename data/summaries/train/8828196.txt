Rookie lawyer Alec Brno  has just been assigned the case of his career: exposing a billion-dollar oil scam led by a ruthless mafia boss . When he reluctantly falls for the gangster's beautiful, but drug-addicted wife , who is also his key witness, Alec soon realizes that all the legal savvy in the world can not protect him from the dangerous reality of mob violence. In a system where criminals often walk free, sometimes courtroom warriors must take the battle for justice into their own hands.
