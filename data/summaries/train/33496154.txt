The film tells the story of Mia, an up-and-coming architect about to marry Tim, her business partner. At the engagement party for her also newly-engaged Father, Lasse, she meets Frida, daughter of her Lasse's fiancee, Elisabeth. Mia and Frida exchange many glances, portending mutual attraction. Mia and Frida both visit the remote island home Elisabeth and Lasse plan to share, and Frida, who is lesbian, continues to be intrigued by Mia who's feelings are mutual but conflicted by her relationship with Tim. Mia and Frida's emotions for one another blossom, becoming increasingly urgent, until finally, Mia is left to decide whether to suppress her feelings for Frida and go on with her wedding plans or break off her engagement to Tim and pursue a new life with Frida. This beautifully shot and sexy, romantic film, destined to be a new classic in Lesbian Cinema, is inspired by Producer Josefin Tengblad's real life story.
