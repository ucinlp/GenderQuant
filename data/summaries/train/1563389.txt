The documentary tracks Globo's involvement with and support of the military dictatorship, its illegal partnership with the American group Time Warner , Marinho's political maneuverings  and a controversial deal involving shares of NEC Corporation and government contracts. It also features interviews with noted Brazilian personalities, such as singer-songwriter Chico Buarque, politicians Leonel Brizola and Antonio Carlos Magalhães and the ex-president Luiz Inácio Lula da Silva.
