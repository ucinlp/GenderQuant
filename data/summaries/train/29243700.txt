The film portrays the Rudd family of 'Aussie battlers' in late 19th century colonial Australia; Dad and Mother Rudd, and their hardworking but somewhat gormless son Dave. They take up a selection of land and attempt to farm it. The difficulties of the life are portrayed, with its ups and downs, humour and frustrations. Finally tired of local corruption and the harshness of his life, Dad Rudd decides to run for State Parliament.
