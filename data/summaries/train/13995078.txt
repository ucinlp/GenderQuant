After saving a homeless Hungarian violinist named Freddie , Mike , a jazz musician and leader of a quartet The Four Seasons, convinces his band members to let afford her room and board. For their act of kindness, Freddie offers to manage the band. After the Four Seasons are fired from their job, Freddie convinces Keppel , the owner of a Hungarian cafe, to hire the band which she soon joins. After Prince Nicholaus of Aregon  goes to the cafe to see the band, the Four Season gain a following. Problems arise when both the Prince and Mike fall in love with Freddie.
