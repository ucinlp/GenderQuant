Nandagopal  is a respected dance teacher at the Kerala Kala Mandiram . However, his wife  commits suicide, causing the hero to turn into an alcoholic and compulsive rule breaker. He is suspected of having killed his wife and is suspended form his job for drunken misbehaviour. The new secretary of the institute wants him to be sacked but his former reputation gives him a reprieve. He trains the talented student Malavika  to perform in his ambitious composition of the Sita Kalayanam, but her jealous young lover  poisons him.
