The Stooges are census takers trolling through an apartment complex when they come upon a Mrs. Wyckoff . The Stooges learn that Wyckoff is one-half of a husband-and-wife Magician Troupe who perform regularly. Unfortunately, Mr. Wyckoff  is also a jealous man and a precise knife thrower. When Mr. Wyckoff comes home, the Stooges make a failed effort to take cover. After several frightening threats, the trio depart as fast as their feet will carry them.
