College student Wes  who comes from Oklahoma to a University in Minnesota, signs up to participate in a psychological experiment where he meets Susan . The two are instantly attracted to each other. Besides the problem of their differing socio-economic backgrounds, Susan is also engaged. However, Susan's grandfather recognizes her fiance's opportunism and when he sides with Wes, their relationship is given more of a chance, in spite of the concern Susan's mother has about social status. Susan's fiancee is Whitley . As events unfold, her grandfather  places his millions on Wes' side of the table since Whitley's opportunistic streak is as apparent as the white stripe on a skunk. Maybe the lovers have a chance after all, even if Susan's mother  is hung up on social status.
