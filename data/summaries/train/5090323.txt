Mohan and Sohan are the two sons of a police inspector. The kids excel in boxing. Their father gets killed by a goon following orders from Ranjit . Mohan kills the goon and takes refuge in a car boot, and goes missing. Years later Sohan  goes on to become a CID officer called Sohan Kumar, who solves his cases taking up different guises. He takes the identity of Johny a petty thief and gets himself in Jail, befriends Heera  and goes to solve a case wooing Rekha  and finding the criminal.
