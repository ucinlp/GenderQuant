Hariharan Pillai  is running his construction company with uncle Velappan . He constructs a house for the rich money lender Satyapalan , who does not compensate them after completing the house. Satyapalan's daughter Kavya  initially dislikes Hariharan, however she eventually falls for him. Pillai also has to take care of sister Latha, who is in love with Nikhil. How Hariharan Pillai sorts out all the issues, and gets Kavya to form the rest of the story.
