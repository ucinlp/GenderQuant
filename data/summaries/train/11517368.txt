Arrogant and impudent Brice Wayne , a West Point cadet who is a star player on the football team, painfully learns the error of his ways when, after a year, he realizes that he hasn't acquired the proper school spirit. Dismissed from the football team shortly before the Army-Navy match, Brice resigns from the Military Academy, then retracts when his friends Tex McNeil  and Betty Channing  encourage him to stay. Restored to the team, Brice scores the decisive goal in the game against Navy.
