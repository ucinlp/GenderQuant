Bill , a divorced deadbeat dad, is estranged from his teenage daughter Molly . Bill's friend Terry , a high school principal, helps him land a job as the coach for the girls' varsity basketball team. Bill begins to regret his decision when he meets the girls on the team: Abbie , Tamra , Mindy , Wendy , Lisa , and Kathy . The team improves under Bill's coaching and the girls advise him on his relationship with his daughter.
