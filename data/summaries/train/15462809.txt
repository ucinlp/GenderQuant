Ajay  an intelligent ex-student of the S. T. School is appointed in the same school as a teacher. Jaya  who studies in his class harasses him by playing mischievous pranks. Ajay's refusal to enter into a romantic relationship with Jaya upsets her. She challenges him that she will one day become his wife. In a hurry he marries Saraswati , an illiterate orphan girl. Jaya befriends Saraswati by teaching her everything from cooking to reading and writing. One day, some terrorists attack the school and take the children as hostages. Ajay, Jaya and all are trapped. Now, can Ajay succeed in saving school children, by destroying the terrorists? It is a Remake of 1992 Telugu film Sundarakanda starring Venkatesh, which was a remake of Tamil film Sundara Kandam starring K. Bhagyaraj and Bhanupriya.
