"When Strangers Appear" is a psychological thriller including Radha Mitchell , Barry Watson  and Josh Lucas . When Beth  opens her remote roadside diner, she expects another slow day. But almost immediately, Jack , a handsome drifter burst in, bleeding and on the run. Within moments, the three surfers Jack claims are chasing him appear and the stage is set for a deadly game of cat and mouse as Beth must decide whom to trust and whom to fear. The film features the single 'Calling On' from Weta in its soundtrack.
