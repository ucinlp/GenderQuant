The story involves an incredibly strong ship stoker name Bill  and the beautiful woman named Mae  whom he saves from drowning. She was attempting suicide as she had no money, clothes and felt remorse about her life of prostitution. They get "married" the same night they meet  but Bill promises Harry  that he'll have it the next morning. The next morning Bill must go back to sea. Mae is upset about this as he says he was "just having a good time" and wasn't serious. She is later accused of stealing clothes from a store . He decides to return and stay for her and takes the sixty-day punishment.
