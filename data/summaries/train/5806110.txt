Bobby "Gator" McKlusky  is serving time in an Arkansas prison for running moonshine when he learns his younger brother Donny was murdered and that Sheriff J.C Conners  was the one behind it. Gator knows the sheriff is taking money from local moonshiners, so he agrees to go undercover for the Feds and try to expose the sheriff. He gets a job running moonshine with Roy Boone  and starts having an affair with his girlfriend Lou . Eventually, when the sheriff discovers Gator is working for the Feds and sends his enforcer Big Bear , Gator decides to go after the sheriff in an epic car chase finale.
