The plot involves a brother and sister, Devon  and Regina , who survive a brutal attack in the small town in which their mother was raised. As adults they return to the town to sell their mother's property and get on with their lives. They return to the town with three friends: Tom , Samir , and Terra . But in returning home they run into old friend Harley  and old enemy Mac , and returning to that dark place awakens a killing rage in a town where everyone has ties that bind.
