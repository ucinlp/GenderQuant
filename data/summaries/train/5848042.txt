Lakshmi Narayana  is an elder son in a family with three brothers and two sisters. He owns a factory that is worth hundreds of crores. Janardhan , a former employee of Lakshmi Industries who was accused of forgery , plans to take revenge on Lakshmi by creating division in his family. This leads to Lakshmi Narayana revealing to this siblings that he and his sister  were actually adopted by their parents at a young age. All this leads to the splitting of the siblings. It is also revealed that Lakshmi holds the wrath of a don based in Kolkata. How Lakshmi reunites his family, eliminates the don, and win his lady love, Nandini , forms the rest of story.
