Arts professor Jung Ji-woo  is searching for a nude model for a video clip that will be played at her exhibition. When Hee-jin , one of her students, recommends Yoon Ji-woo  for the job, the three woman head to the beach to shoot the video. As they spend time together, Yoon Ji-woo begins to share pieces of her past relationship with Kang Ji-woo .
