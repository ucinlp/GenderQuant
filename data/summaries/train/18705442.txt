Plane , a troubled cop who hits the skids when his girlfriend Carrie  gets killed by a stoic hitman named Angel . One year later he finds himself saddled with new partner Turkey , an immature cop who's in love with up-and-coming idol singer Tinny Chung . There are the usual growing pains as the two partners learn to like and trust one another. Plane meets Tinny's friend Mandy, her looks resembles Carrie and they fall in love for each other. Then things go bad when Angel returns to settle same scores...
