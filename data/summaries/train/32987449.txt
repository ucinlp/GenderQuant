Edith  is married to a dashing naval officer, Lt Coventry , who bears a resemblance to the their Jack Baxter. Edith's father is killed by Baxter and Coventry is framed for this by the evil Standish  who has designs on Edith. Partly convicted on the testimony of his son, Cedric, Coventry is thrown in prison. Edith and Cedric are lured to a sawmill by a forged letter from Standish. Standish places the boy on a saw bench and threatens to cut him unless the girl marries her. But Coventry escapes from prison in time to rescue the boy and the girl. Baxter confesses and Coventry and Edith are reunited.{{cite news}}
