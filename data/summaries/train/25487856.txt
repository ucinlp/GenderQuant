Thyagayya  is a villager composing devotional music to Rama while rejecting the court of Serfoji, Maharajah of Tanjore , the dominant cultural center of the region. Turning down invitations and gifts from the Maharajah, he provokes the jealous wrath of his brother Japesen . The film's climax comes when Japesen destroys Rama's idols, Thyagaraja resurrects them eventually and sacrifices his life to his God.
