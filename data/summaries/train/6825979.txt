Prithvi, Vikram and Dattatreya  are the sons of Ranganath and Srividya living in a village. Ranganath goes against Sarath Saxena in an incident and is killed by him. Srividya who is pregnant with twins is separated from Prithvi. She thinks that he is killed while escaping from the goons and delivers the twins in a temple. The pujari  who is childless adopts one son and srividya is left with vikram who becomes an Assistant Commissioner of Police. Dattatreya is a Dance master. The story revolves on how the brothers unite with one another and also with their mother and take revenge on the villains.
