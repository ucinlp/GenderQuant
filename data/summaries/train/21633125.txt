The central character is Alex , a Danish photographer with a Stockholm-bred girlfriend, Simone . Late one evening Alex suddenly abandons his girlfriend, Simone, to pursue the beautiful Aimee . In his encounter with Aimee time and place dissolve for him and he becomes a stranger to Simone, to whom he cannot return. “It’s all a film. It’s all a construction,” announces the narrator, who is soon revealed to be a noted Swedish author, August , as well as the tale’s apparent inventor.
