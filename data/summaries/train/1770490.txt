Mike McCoy, the lead singer for a band and a part-time race-car driver, is just trying to get by when he is pursued by three different women who seek to marry him. The first is Les, the female drummer of his band . The second is Cynthia Foxhugh , a spoiled heiress and daddy's girl. The third is Diana St. Clair , an author of books on men. McCoy drives a Cobra 427 sports car, which is towed around the country by a 1929 Model J Duesenberg. McCoy is driven off the road into a river by Cynthia. Her father, Howard Carl Betz, becomes determined to hire McCoy to drive his Fox Five car in a car race, but McCoy is just as determined to win the race and the girl in his own way.
