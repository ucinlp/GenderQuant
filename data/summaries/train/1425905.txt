When Claire  learns that she is five months pregnant at the age of seventeen, she decides to keep the baby and not to tell the father . Instead, she quits her current job at a local supermarket and goes to work as an assistant for Madame Mélikian , an embroiderer for haute couture. Mélikian has just lost her only son in a motorcycle accident. Claire shows her willingness in taking her duties, as well as taking over Mélikian's private order when she is treated in the hospital.
