 In one long Friday evening, Takeshi Miyata , a straight-arrow businessman, will encounter a number of people  who have intertwining fates. The plot of the film is presented in succession first from the point of view of Maki Kuwata , a young woman disappointed in love, then from Takeshi's point of view, then of his friend, Yusuke Kanda , a private detective, then of Takeshi's former girlfriend, Ayumi Kurata , then of a wannabe-tough Yakuza, Asai .
