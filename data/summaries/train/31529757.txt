The story is based on the love affair between Tara  and Sasank .Tara Sasankam in Dramatic History of the World, Kolachalam Srinivasa Rao, pp. 224. Tara, the wife of pious teacher Brihaspati successfully seduces his student Sasank. They had a child by their illicit union. This results in dispute between the teacher and his student about the fatherhood of the boy. It goes to the court of the Gods; who express their inability to solve the issue. Tara herself had to decide that the child was born to the student.
