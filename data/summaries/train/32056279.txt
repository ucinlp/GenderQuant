Agra-based collegian, Samar Thakur, lives in a joint family consisting of his dad, mom, brother Amar and his wife; as well as a married sister, Munni, estranged from her husband. His parents force him to marry Prabha, a matriculate, much to his chagrin as this interferes with his future plans. The marriage does take place, and he soon finds that she is not only incompatible with him, but also not well versed in household chores, leading to arguments, abuse and neglect, which may result in the end of this marriage.
