This youth melodrama tells the story of a law student from a small village in Korea, and the irresponsible girl with whom he falls in love. The girl leads a reckless life, working in bars in Itaewon, and in the U.S., while the law student follows her. Their relationship ends in tragedy with the man shooting the woman, and her confessing her true love before dying.<ref namehttp://www.kmdb.or.kr/eng/md_basic.asp?nation04279|title2009-07-19|publisher=Korean Movie Database  }}
