The film is about a nine-year-old boy's  obsession with women's breasts. Tete becomes jealous of his baby brother who is breast fed by their mother. Tete goes on a personal mission to find the perfect pair of lactating breasts to feed on. A beautiful Portuguese dancer  arrives in town, the answer to Tete's prayers. Unfortunately for Tete, Estrellita is the attention of many men's affections including her husband Maurice , an older man working as the other half of her travelling act, and attractive Flamenco-singing teenager, Miguel . With this amount of competition will Tete fulfil his wish?
