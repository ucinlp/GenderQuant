A 13-year-old Palmyra, Nebraska girl, Luli , runs away from her alcoholic parents, bringing along a revolver. She meets Eddie , an erratic drifter and asks him to take her to Las Vegas. After he physically threatens her, she leaves him and is later taken under the wing of a grifter named Glenda . After raping Luli, Eddie ties her up, cuts and dyes her hair. Glenda then shows up and tries to untie Luli, revealing to her that she and Eddie were lovers. Eddie shoots Glenda with Luli's gun, and Luli shoots Eddie. In the end, she heads to Los Angeles to where she is invited by Beau  to live with his sister.
