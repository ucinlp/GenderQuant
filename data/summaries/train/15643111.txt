Ryan and Pete are 27-year old best friends in Miami, born on the same day and each searching for the perfect woman. Ryan  is a rookie stockbroker living with his psychic mother . Pete  is a slick surfer yet to find commitment. Each meets the women of his dreams on the same day. Ryan knocks heads in an elevator with the gorgeous Jessica , passing out before obtaining her number. Pete falls for the insatiable Tiara  whose uncle is the mob boss Vincent Bublione , charged with her protection. This high-energy romantic comedy asks to what extent one can go for true love.
