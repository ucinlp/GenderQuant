After his young son dies from the negligience of medical professionals at a hospital, Harry Fertig  takes matters into his own hands and kills the negligent doctors responsible. Slick lawyer Roy Bleakie , looking only to win a case and not caring of the matters involved, is assigned Fertig's case. Shocked to hear that his client wants to plead guilty, the case causes Bleakie to question his own morals by defending an honorable man.
