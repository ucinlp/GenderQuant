On her 15th birthday Avery Colden  discovers from her father  that she is to be given as a secret bride to the new Prophet  of a religious cult. She escapes with the help of Army Colonel Jude Marks  and a renegade female Sheriff . When they find out that a younger girl will take her place as the secret bride they join forces to expose the truth that lies hidden in a town in Utah. Col. Marks calls in favors from the military and Washington but even that may not be enough to help them fight the deeply secretive cult.
