Seo Joo-Hee is a Horse Jockey who dreams of winning the Grand Prix championship. One day, she suffers an accident during a horse race and her beloved horse is put down and she injures her arm. She falls into depression and quits horse racing and decides to go to Jeju for a vacation. There, she meets Woo-suk, a fellow Horse Jockey who has previously won the championship. They fall in love and he helps and encourages her for comeback to Grand Prix Championship.
