Hong Jeong-hye is sentenced to serve 10 years in prison after killing her abusive husband. Pregant at the time of her arrest, she gives birth to a baby boy behind bars but must give him up for adoption according to the law. One day she sets out to start a choir with the help of Kim Moon-ok, a fellow inmate on a death row for killing her abusive husband like Jeong-hye. The prison chief promises her a special outing with her baby if she succeeds.{{cite web}}
