During the Second World War, Kay Walsh  is a woman who has been assigned to work in an armaments factory in California while her husband Jack , a Leading Seaman, is overseas in naval service. Lonely and vulnerable, Kay falls for the charms of another man, a musician named Lucky , and befriends her embittered neighbor Hazel , a former singer. The three of them enjoy their time together until Kay's husband comes home and realizes what has occurred.
