Victor Ballard  is a poor but happy-go-lucky New York sidewalk photographer who shares a studio apartment with a painter from Poland, Stefan Janowski . When Victor shoots a photo of Alexandra Curtis , he realizes she is desperate and in need of a friend who can guide her through the ways and means of surviving in Manhattan with no money.
