The story is about an Indian family of Ramaiah  and closely resembles Hindu epic Ramayanam. Ramaiah and Gopi  are brothers. Janaki  is wife of Ramaiah and looks after the home very well. Chitti  is their dearly loved sister. They married her into a wealthy family of Kantham  giving big dowry lending money from Rayudu . This leads them to many problems including differences between the family members. Finally their son in law  and sister helps them solving the problem. The entire family is reunited and live happily.
