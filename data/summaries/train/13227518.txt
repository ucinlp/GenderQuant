The plot is built around the story of Sara , who is married to an orphaned alcoholic Huseyn , losing her engagement ring during the family vacation at a country house near Baku. Sara suspects her yard neighbors of stealing the ring, and it does not take long before neighbors start fighting with each other. The story further reveals the ills and corruption of society at the end of Soviet period, disintegration of old social values, relationships between neighbors living in hidden envy of each other.
