Juliette  is a graduate student pursuing her Ph.D in psychology. She is writing her dissertation on human relationships and sexuality, as well as deformation and castration. She utilizes local newspaper classified ads for primary research. When she meets one research subject , she learns the importance of choosing research subjects carefully. The film also focuses on Juliette's love of literature, with sequences picturing Juliette's fantasies of being part of various classic novels, including Pride and Prejudice, Emma, Ulysses and War and Peace.
