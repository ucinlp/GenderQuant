Lefty Farrell  links up with con artists Brandy Kirby  and Vincent Mailer . The three concoct a scheme to rob a rich couple out of ten million dollars by having Farrell pose as the couple's long-lost son. When the husband refuses to change his will, Kirby and Mailer decide to kill them. Farrell confesses the scam to the elderly couple, which prompts Mailer to add him to his list of potential victims.
