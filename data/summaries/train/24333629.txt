Ganesh  is an orphan who always pitches in to help needy people. In one such strange situation, he is forced to act with Divya  that he loves her. But later on, she comes to know that he is loving her to fulfill some other ambition and his love is an act of fabrication. By then, Ganesh genuinely falls in love with her. The rest of the story is all about how he convinces her about his true love.
