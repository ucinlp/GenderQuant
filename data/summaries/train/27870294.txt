Zak O'Brien  is an animal trainer for the popular television series "Caesar & Romulus", which has been selected for a "Patsy" Award to be presented in Burbank, California. Zak, along with Caesar , and Romulus , board his personal plane in Denver for the flight to Burbank, but en route at night over the Utah wastelands they encounter a sudden blizzard. When Zak's radio and engine fail, he guides the craft down to a crash landing. All three passengers survive, but the plane is destroyed and a struggle for survival begins.
