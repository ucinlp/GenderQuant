Nazim Tahiri  finds out that his father has been murdered in Antwerp by members of the rival Gaba clan. Kanun, an Albanian law from the 15th century, forces Nazim to take revenge. He goes to Antwerp, where his godfather, Prenk Shehu , leads the family mafia businesses. Police officer Vincke  is in charge of finding out what is going on in the world of the Albanian mafia clans.
