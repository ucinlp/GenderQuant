Jon Davis is a policeman who consoles 'Foxy' Pattis at his shooting gallery that his criminal father has died. Foxy blames all policeman for his father's death feeling they harassed him all his life and thus were responsible for his death. Both enlist in the United States Army Air Forces where Foxy is the instructor and an aerial gunnery school and tries to drive Jon, now a "Flying Sergeant"http://www.af.mil/news/story.asp?id=31103853 student out of the school. In addition to the hostility of his instructor Foxy, Jon befriends a young Texas boy, Sandy, whose father was an airman killed at the Japanese attack on Hickam Field. Sandy invites Jon and Foxy to his family's ranch where both men fall for Sandy's sister Peggy. After graduation Jon is commissioned as a pilot with many of his classmates now the crew of his A-29 Hudson light bomber including Foxy. During a bombing mission against the Japanese the plane is shot down behind enemy lines with the crew using their talents to get their craft back in the air to continue the fight.
