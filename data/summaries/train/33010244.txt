Paul Craig  is an aviator-inventor who becomes blind due to overwork. He stumbles into a room as a person is being killed by foreign agents - who decide to let Craig live because he cannot identify them. The murder is part of a plot to overthrow the monarchy of fictional Ruvania. Paul regains his sight and marries Angela , whose brother was killed by the gang, and who is the ward of Carlo Gelmini , head of the gang. Angela lost her memory with the shock of her brother's death but manages to recover it and help Paul save Ruvania.{{cite news}}
