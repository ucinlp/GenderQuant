Vinnie is a teenage boy who is an outcast at school, alienating teachers and students alike. He is a local courier for the local Mafia boss. He lives in welfare housing with his mother , a young brother and sister, and his Mum's lover who he can't stand. Vinnie takes out his aggression with the world practicing boxing at the local gym. He is haunted by images of his father  and his father's boxing career. His music teacher  encourages him to get involved as a drummer with the school band, and his girlfriend Gloria  and others influence him to stay away from the Mafia.
