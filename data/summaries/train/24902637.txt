The story revolves around two youngsters Ashwin and Anita, who live in police quarters with parents. The two are always quarreling and don’t see eye to eye.But tragedy strikes the two after an incident that takes place in the riots following the Babri Masjid demolition in 1992, where Ashwin saves Anita. But Anita’s parents have already decided to marry their daughter off to somebody else. But Anita doesn’t approve of this marriage and wants to run away with Ashwin. But Ashwin doesn’t want to hurt Anita’s parents, who are close to his family.Anita finally get married to someone, but Ashwin goes missing.
