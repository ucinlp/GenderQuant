Amal  is the wife of Ahmed  who manages a textile factory. One day, she receives a threatening letter directed at Ahmed. The letter reveals that Ahmed committed a murder; he killed a teller to steal the factory's money. Amal remembers that her husband had come to her telling her that he had lost his money, and weeks later had restored his money. One of the factory workers  meets Amal and confirms what the letter said, claiming that he witnessed the crime.{{cite web}}
