Rajnikanth finds himself to be an orphan. He travels from his village to Madras. There he meets Janakaraj, who gives him a job. If Rajni wants his inheritance, he has to follow certain rules. He can get an inheritance of {{Indian Rupees}}30 billion if he can spend {{Indian Rupees}}300 million in a month. The three conditions are: no contributions to charities, not to own any assets at the end of the month, and, above all, no one else must not know. If Rajni fails to spend the money according to the rules, all the money will go to his father's trust which is managed by V.K.Ramasamy, "Nizhalgal" Ravi, Kitty and Raghuvaran. These four form a team of "villains". They want to spoil Rajni's efforts and get all the money for the trust and then to loot the money. Rambha is Visu's daughter and she is appointed as the accountant for Rajni for 30 days.
