Nagraj sends John to steal rare Diamond worth crores from museum. But John betrays Nagraj and run away with Diamond, but gets killed by Ronnie, who leaves the Diamond with a taxi triver Yadav Das. So Yadav is jailed in John's murder case. His daughter Madhuri disguises herself as a boy to run the Taxi for taking care of her family. Now Manik and Chand turn up at Madhuri's house and claims to be her long lost uncles from Africa. Madhuri is in love with Rahul, a Nagraj's henchman. As the Diamond is the prime attraction, everyone wants its possession. In the Climax, Manik and Chand rescues Rahul, Madhuri and Madhabi from Nagraj and Police arrests Nagraj.
