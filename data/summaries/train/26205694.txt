Madhavan ([[Dileep  is a clever thief who does robbery for a living. He is following the principles of his mentor Mullani Pappan . Meesa Madhavan got his name by the popular saying that if Madhavan rolls his Mustache  looking at someone, he will rob his house that night. His enemy was a local money lender Bhageerathan Pillai  who refused to give back his father's property. Madhavan falls in love in Bhageerathan Pillai's daughter Rukmini . The sub inspector in the village Eappen Pappachi  has an eye on Rukmini. He steals the idol from the local Temple with the intention of selling it and puts the blame on Madhavan. It becomes Madhavan's responsibility to find the culprits and he does that with his mentor's help and thus uniting with his girl friend.
