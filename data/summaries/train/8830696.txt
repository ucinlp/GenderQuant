Hatley High is about an 18-year-old boy named Tommy  and his father  who after the death of Tommy's mother move to her hometown. Unknown to them is that Tommy's mother was a chess superstar back home. The entire town is obsessed with the game and the school's 'jocks' are the chess team. Through romance with a chess cheerleader  and rivalry with the team captain , Tommy uses his natural and apparently inherited talents for chess to help the school win the chess match against their rivals, the Russians.
