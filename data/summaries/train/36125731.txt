Jack Lane joins the Australian Navy and trains on the Tingira. His brother Stanley discovers a German spy ring amongst the social set in Sydney. They capture him and take him to a secret island output. Germans are about to destroy an Allied radio station but it manages to alert the HMAS Sydney beforehand and the ship attacks German sailors. A half-caste island,er Kana , leads the Sydney to the spies' island. A landing party attacks and kills the spies but Kana is killed.
