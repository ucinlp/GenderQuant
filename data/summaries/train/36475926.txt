Five different women walk, ride and work through the day and each performs one of the five Muslim prayers Fajr , Zohar , Asr , Maghrib  and Isha . The film begins with 'Isha' and ends the next day at 'Maghrib' with each prayer being performed in real time amidst stunning settings. This structure also offers a glimpse at the beauty, stress and contradictions of people interacting throughout the day.Say a beautiful prayer by Faisal M. Naim, The Hindu January 31st, 2011
