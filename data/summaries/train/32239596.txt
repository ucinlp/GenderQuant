The film begins on a summer's day in Northern Canada  with a herd of Edmontosaurus and an Edmontonia feeding on the lush vegetation that grows all around them. Scar, a young male Edmontosaurus, enjoy his life in the arctic forests with his extended family, he comes across a young immature male Troodon named Patch, who has been feasting on baby Edmontosaurus all summer, but now they're too big for him and he's having a tough time catching prey. And the plentiful food for Scar is ending as well, as the dark, cold Arctic winter is approaching.
