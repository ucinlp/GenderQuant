A college student, a single middle aged mother and an older widow meet in a steam room. Elizabeth  is a college freshman, beginning to understand her sexual identity in the shadow of her strict Catholic parents. Laurie  is just beginning a relationship with her son's young coach while her ex-husband's tries to use their son against her. An older widow, Doris , after years of loneliness, has met a new man. The film weaves their stories together, exploring the similarities that connect three very different women.
