The film follows a day in the life of a divorced Zagreb journalist Marko Požgaj , an average modern intellectual who goes about his daily business. Mundane scenes of Požgaj's day are shown interspersed with flashbacks and fantastic imagery reflecting his inner life. These include his recollections of childhood, his feelings about the present and past, including memories of his first marriage, his current girlfriend Rajka  and his father killed in World War II , as well as his fantasies and hopes about the future.
