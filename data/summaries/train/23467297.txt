{{Peacock}} Mr. Bhalla , a hyper-vibrant Punjabi lives in US along with his wife, daughter and nephew Aman . Aman is in love with Neha , daughter of Mr. Patel , a conventional Patel. Although the two are in love, their families have huge differences due to drastically different lifestyles. In the end, it's up to Aman and Neha to not only bring their families together but to live up to their expectations.
