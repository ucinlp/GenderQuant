Dorothy Gale  returns to Kansas to find it devastated. Dorothy then finds a new way to get back to the Land of Oz, only to discover that her old friends the Scarecrow , Tin Man , Cowardly Lion  and the entire Land of Oz, are all in grave danger. On Dorothy's new journey through Oz, she meets new friends like a marshmallow man named Marshal Mallow , a china doll princess , a tugboat named Tugg , and a large owl named Wiser . With the help of her new friends, they band together against a wicked Jester  who wants to control Oz by turning important people into china dolls.
