A young woman and two other people are killed in a Kindle County local bar. Experienced detective sergeant Larry Starczek  begins investigation on the murders. Soon everything points to the small time thief Squirell. Larry arrests him and makes the thief confess. After a short trial Squirrel goes to the prison where he'll be executed. The story now moves 7 years later, as new evidence surfaces. Nobody is so sure anymore that it was Squirell who actually killed those three people years ago. Furthermore, it seems that the judge from his trial wasn't completely clean.
