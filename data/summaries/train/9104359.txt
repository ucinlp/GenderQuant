While a mad bomber is terrorizing the city, Porky Pig  is searching for money in order to buy an ice cream soda. He earns a few cents retrieving lost items for people  and stumbles upon the bomber's lost bomb. The bomber tries to get away, but ends up getting defeated by Porky's relentless pursuit in trying to give the bomb back. Porky is given a cash reward and treats himself to an ice cream soda feast.
