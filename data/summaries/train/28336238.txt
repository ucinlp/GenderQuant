Hridoy  is the son of a head constable . Hridoy is a guy with carefree attitude. He is beaten by a rival gang at night and was rescued by a beautiful girl Priya. She pays his hospital bills and donates her blood. She is gone from the hospital by the time Hridoy became conscious. When Hridoy's friends tell him who rescued him, he starts loving her immedietley for her good-heartedness though he did not see her. Priya later turns out to be the sister of the city police commissioner Misha Sawdagar.
