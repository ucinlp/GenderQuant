{{Expand section}} In the film, Warbucks , 13 year old Annie , her friend Hannah , an eccentric scientist  and an orphan named Molly  travel to England, where Warbucks is to be knighted by the King. However, the kids get mixed up in the scheme of an evil noblewoman known as Lady Edwina Hogbottom  to blow up Buckingham Palace while all the heirs to the throne are present for Warbucks' knighting, thus making her queen.
