The plot involves a witch who accompanies two children named Small and Tender with their babysitter  to a Halloween party and transforms them into a werewolf, Frankenstein's monster, and a ghost . The witch takes them to the Halloween party-in-progress at her house. Other citizens of the town get offended. They try to catch these supernatural beings as a mob. The children and Bazooey spend the night as real eerie creatures but decide they need to become human beings again. A disco song entitled "Witch Magic" was sung in this film.
