Audrey finds a pair of sunglasses at the beach and that night she dreams about a girl who was murdered on the beach. After seeking help from Mrs Bud Lung, Audrey learns that the spirit wants to tell her about her fate. Mrs Bud urges Audrey to return the sunglasses back to where she found them. She becomes unconscious and witnesses the entire murder in her subconscious state. While having dinner later, Audrey recognises the killer as a rich man called Lee Ka-hing and she allows the victim's spirit to possess her and force Lee to confess the murder.
