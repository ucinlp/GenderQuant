When a smuggler kills his sister, a mentally impaired man  vows to deliver justice and joins a martial arts school. He trains hard there to become a master fighter, honing his fighting skills. Taking on a new identity, Paayum Puli , he sets off for revenge, but a beautiful woman  soon puts a hitch in his plans. Manorama, Silk Smitha and Jaishankar also star in this action-packed movie.
