The silent drama tells the story of a man who names himself Count Wladislaw Sergius Karamzin  in order to seduce rich women and extort money from them. He has set up shop in Monte Carlo and his partners in crime  are his cousins: faux-Princess Vera Petchnikoff  and faux-Her Highness Olga Petchnikoff . Count Karamzin begins his latest scam on the unworldly wife of an American envoy, Helen Hughes , even though her husband is nearby.
