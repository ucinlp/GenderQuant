Number One Shakib Khan is a Bengali social action drama film directed by Badiul Alam Khokon. The film focuses on a young man who struggles to stand on his own feet. Thrilled by his ideology and honesty, the heroine Apu Biswas makes up her mind to stand beside him with her love to make his dream come true. Popular film actor Shakib Khan is playing in the name role. The cast also includes Nutan, Ali Raj and Sadek Bachhu, among others.movie review
