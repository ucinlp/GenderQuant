Lara Tyler  is one of the most famous movie stars around, but all she wants to do is marry her fiance, writer James Arber . Besieged by paparazzi, especially Marco Ballani , who is obsessed with Lara, they escape to the tiny Scottish island of Hegg to try to wed in peace. However, when the paparazzi track them down, and with the locals smelling a payday, Lara becomes upset and runs away. In desperation her management team, led by Steve Korbitz , decide to stage a fake wedding, hoping the paparazzi will fall for the scam and leave the island. Local girl Katie , who is nursing a broken heart, is recruited to pretend to be Lara. But is James really sure Lara is his true love?
