Meneghel plays a singer/TV star, who starts a campaign all over Brazil for children to color old, ugly walls, in an effort to make the world happier. But her plans are ruined by Baixo Astral, an evil demon that lives under the city of Rio de Janeiro and uses bad children as slaves. Baixo Astral kidnaps Xuxa's dog, Xuxo, expecting she'll go after him. She travels the magic world of Alto Astral searching for her dog, helped by the lizard Xixa.
