An army aircraft crashes in dense forest. Captain Roy Jacob (Mohanlal, starts his mission to rescue his colleagues from the dense forest, fighting a terrorist group and recovering confidential documents. The film resembled Sylvester Stallone's Rambo series. It was dubbed into Tamil as Captain Dhevaram and was well received. The film was remade into Telugu as Adavilo Abhimanyudu, starring Jagapathi Babu.
