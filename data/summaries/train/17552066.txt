Tarzan is called to Brazil by an old friend, The Professor  to help stop the Jaguar Cult, led by Barcuma , from running off Dr. Ann Philips  who is there to give much-needed inoculations to native villagers along the Amazon River. Tarzan is assisted by Baron  and Cheeta , both of whom he brought from Africa, as well as Captain Sam Bishop , a riverboat pilot, and Bishop's young ward, Pepe .
