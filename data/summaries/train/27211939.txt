Small town Cop Michael Savion  gets stone-walled by big city detectives led by Hardwick  when he asks them to investigate his younger brother's death. His brother David , a gangster, was murdered by members of his own gang for having wanted to quit the gang lifestyle in order to marry Gloria , and for being suspected of 'ratting' out his gangs activities... Savion takes off his badge and goes vigilante to seek justice for the death of his brother, and crosses paths with Arturo , the leader of an international ring involved in human trafficking.
