A fantasy, it tells the story of seven-year-old Albert Franklin , the son of a Las Vegas magician's widowed assistant . His mother dies suddenly in a car accident and Albert, who is now an orphan, is sent to New Jersey to live with his mother's foster sister, Harriet . The plot revolves around Albert, and his imaginary friend named Bogus , a French magician, who helps the boy cope with his transition. Gradually Harriet, who can also see Bogus, comes to terms with her new situation as well.
