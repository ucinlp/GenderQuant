Detective Vincent Hanna is on the trail of a gang of ruthless professional criminals, led by the methodical Patrick McLaren. But Hanna is soon surprised when he discovers that he and McLaren have quite a lot in common. While McLaren and his gang plan another heist, Hanna and his colleagues keep surveillance. But McLaren also faces a personal problem when he finds himself falling in love, which he personally condemns due to the commitment required to his profession. Things then take a turn for the very worst, when the details of McLaren's planned heist are betrayed to the police. When they  arrive on the scene unexpectedly, McLaren and his crew engage them in a mid-street shootout, in which most of McLaren's crew are killed. After making an escape, McLaren is torn between a new life with his girlfriend and seeking revenge on those that betrayed him.
