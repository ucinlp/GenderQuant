Bobo, a young man,  kills two criminals in self-defense. Acting on his lawyer's advice, he turns himself in, but ends up in a mental institution. He then escapes and meets up with his girlfriend , but she gets killed in a police shootout. With Bobo on the run, the police start a manhunt which also attracts a great deal of media attention, including an ambitious journalist  who follows the chase. Bobo then barricades himself in an abandoned house and takes a woman for a hostage.
