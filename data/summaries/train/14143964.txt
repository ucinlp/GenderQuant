Hop Harrigan  and his pal "Tank" Tinker operate a small airport and flying service, and are hired by J. Westly Arnold  to fly scientist, Dr. Tabor , to his secret laboratory where he has a revolutionary new power unit. But an unknown character known as The Chief Pilot is interested in the invention and uses a destructive ray to cripple Hop's airplane and kidnap Tabor. Hop and "Tank", aided by Gail Nolan (Jennifer Holt and her kid brother, Jackie , finally overcome the villains only find a bigger threat to them all within their group...
