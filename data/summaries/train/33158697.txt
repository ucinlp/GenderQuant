When teenagers Josh  and Ling  join their respective parents, Chris  and Dr. Li  on an archaeological dig in China they encounter something trapped beneath a temple beyond their wildest imagination. A real live Chinese dragon. With the Dragon Pearl buried on the excavation site, Josh and Ling implore their parents for help, but met with disbelief the children realize they must ﬁnd the pearl on their own. However thereʼs one who does believe them: archaeologist Philip Dukas , who wants to seize the pearlʼs awesome power for his own sinister ambitions. The only way to stop him is for Josh and Ling to get to it ﬁrst and return it to its rightful owner.
