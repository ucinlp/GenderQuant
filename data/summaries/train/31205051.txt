Danny Stetson  is a clean cut all-American college student and gymnast with dreams of Olympic gold when he's lured into "Homeland", a youth-oriented religious "commune" by a compellingly beautiful girl, Rebecca . Here he is programmed by the charismatic leader, Neil Kirklander  to believe that his new life now has the true meaning that it previously lacked. Anguished by their son's disappearance, Danny's parents  hire modern-day bounty hunter, Charles Pratt  to abduct Danny and exorcise his brainwashed mind, but the psychological change could be traumatizing.
