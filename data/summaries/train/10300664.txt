The plot centers around single nurse Karaitas , her son, Guðumund , who only plays with schizophrenic family friend, Marino , and criminal father Garðar  and his conflicts which lead to the near death beating of twin brother Georg. Karaitas lives in a housing project in Fell , Reykjavík and is struggling to take care of her children with her ex husband trying to take custody of her kids. Garðar , who is struggling to wanting to change from his criminal life lives in Lower-Breiðholt and decides it's time to try and connect with his son. But, when he tries to change he realizes it can be harder than he thinks.
