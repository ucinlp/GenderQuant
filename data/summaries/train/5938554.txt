A romantic thriller, Crossing follows the story of macho Danarama , who makes a deathbed promise to his father to turn their family from petty gangsters to upright brokerage owners. The only thing standing in Daniel's way is his newly discovered desire to dress up like a girl. Embroiling himself in an intense romance with a blackmailing prostitute , Daniel must find a way through a maze of disgrace and pleasure to discover where his real loyalties lie.
