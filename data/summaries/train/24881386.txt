The Fall of a Nation is an attack on the pacifism of William Jennings Bryan and Henry Ford<ref nameRothstein |firsthttp://movies.nytimes.com/movie/90923/Fall-of-a-Nation/overview |titleMovies.nytimes.com |date2009-10-30}} and a plea for American preparedness for war.{{cite news}} America is unprepared for an attack by the "European Confederated Army", a European army headed by Germany. However, America is saved by a pro-war Congressman who raises an army to defeat the invaders with the support of a suffragette. According to IMDb the film is split into three sections: "A nation falls", "The heel of the conqueror" and "The uprising two years later".
