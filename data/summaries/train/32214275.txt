The titular character is Isabel, a woman with a goddess-like allure and beauty who was washed ashore to a sea side town. She was rescued by couple without a child. All women envy and want to be her. All men worship and desire her, but beneath her beautiful facade is a rotten soul. She is a sociopath. A person who takes joy in the destruction of others. She is worse than a tempest. Because of her, friends became bitter rivals, families are torn apart, women kill themselves, men kill each other and even the insane became more insane. She single-handedly destroyed the whole town. But what will be the price? Even she can't stop her explosive end once fate intervenes.
