Xiao Yu , a barista and cake maker in a teahouse-cafe by Westlake in Hangzhou. Since a car crash long ago, she had been leading a peaceful life with her kind-hearted best friend and fellow car crash survivor Tong  who is unabashed in admitting that his feelings for Xiao Yu have developed into love. One day A Qin  celebrates his birthday alone in her cafe. Xiao Yu's curiosity sparks off their dialogues and both discover they have many parallels. But A Qin is a player who is escaping to Hangzhou from the pressures of his relationships in Beijing and Xiao Yu is pursued by Tong ... What will be their choices?
