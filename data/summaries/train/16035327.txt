Set in the modern day mid-west, the film focuses on a young couple trying to establish a new life. They become stranded in a very isolated vineyard. They are forced to face secrets from their own pasts as well as the malicious intentions of the vineyard's inhabitants who are not what they seem. It turns out that the inhabitants are demons who are punishing them for killing their mentally challenged child who wouldn't be capable of living a normal life. Prior to driving to the house they actually die in a car crash and the farmhouse is simply their embodiment of hell as they still believe they are alive. When all is revealed they are asked if they could redo everything they have done, would they? They say no.
