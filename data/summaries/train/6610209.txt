As a kid, Charlie Banks  both admired and feared the charismatic and violent local tough Mick Leary ; in high school, Charlie witnesses Mick beat up two jocks at a party, leaving the two victims near dead. Despite Mick being the buddy of his best friend, Danny , Charlie reports Mick to the police. Three years later, Charlie  and Danny  are college freshmen who are in for a surprise when Mick  shows up for a visit. Though claiming to be visiting for the weekend, Mick moves into the two friend's dorm and begins borrowing their clothing, attending their classes, reading their books, and flirting with a woman  that Charlie has a crush on. Charlie begins to wonder if his friend has changed or if he is plotting a fiendish revenge against Charlie for turning him in to the police.
