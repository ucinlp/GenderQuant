When she was still young, Dalida, then Dalila for her stage's name, she left her native country, Egypte to Paris, in order to have her chance. She met Lucien Morisse who permitted her to start a beautiful career as a singer. Dalida and Lucien got married finally but divorced soon. Some others lovers would leave her with a brutal and dramatic manner that would push her to a little decline. But her wish to have a child would never come true that finish her decline so that she decided to kill herself.
