A private detective goes undercover in an asylum in search of a judge who is hiding out from the police. The detective was hired by a pretty reporter who is sure the judge is hiding out in the private sanitarium. The reporter and P.I. begin to fall in love as well as falling more and more into danger from abusive attendants and other guests of the asylum. Other inmates include an arsonist patient and 'The Champ', a man who attacks anyone put into a room with him.
