The story sets in the Amsterdam of the thirties, from teacher Bruis' point of view. It centers around Cis Vrijmoeth, an oaf who never sees father because he is a sailor and who is neglected by his mother. Time after time he is expelled from school. Only teacher Bruis can really handle and restrain the boy. In an fit of rage and fear, Ciske kills his mother with a knife and he is sent to a youth detention center. In the end, his father returns from the sea and decides to take care of him, together with his wife Aunt Jans.
