Gandharva Kumar's arrival disrupts the festival mood of Durga Puja at Ratanlal Babu's house. He captivates the grandchildren of Ratanlal Babu, Habul, and Tinni, with his magical tricks. Now Gandharva Kumar reveals a long-forgotten secret wherein he claims to be heir to the family property to Ratanlal Babu. This creates shock in the entire family and casts a gloom, following dramatic incidents. It reveals that Gandharva Kumar is fake. Whether the magic trick works or not for Gandharva Kumar forms the climax.
