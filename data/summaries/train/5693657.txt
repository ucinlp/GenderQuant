The protagonist, Eddy  is a secondary level student repeating his Leaving Certificate after failing it the previous year. By chance he meets an enigmatic "Jock", Shane , who invites him to join a gang called the Jock Club, a secret organisation  who attack, bully and torture those who they consider to be nerds. As Ed is being introduced to the other members of the Jock Club, he learns that they have recently kidnapped Eugene Nerdlinger, a nerd from their class and are in the process or torturing him. Over the course of the afternoon, Ed becomes a member of the Jock Club and joins the club in its violent activities, until he is ordered by Shane to murder Nerdlinger as part of his initiation ritual.
