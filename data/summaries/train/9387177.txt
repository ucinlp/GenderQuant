The setting of the story is San Telmo, one of the oldest barrios of Buenos Aires. The film tells of the characters who live in a building on 672 Chile Street, they include: *An afflicted driver Nelson Infanti  who finds calmness in Macarena  a young girl he takes to school each morning; *An actress Malena Marlene  who used to be famous and seeks to have a strong comeback in her profession; *A devout and orphan young girl Silvia Locatti  who listens to moans that come from the next apartment; *A liberal Italian Simona Innocenti  who has won the enmity of her neighbors who are collecting signatures so that she can be evicted from the building.
