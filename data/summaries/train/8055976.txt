Gwyn Marcus  has always wanted a marriage like her parents. She has just accepted the proposal of her boyfriend Matt , but she has some misgivings about their future together. Her fear of commitment grows as she learns of the various affairs that her family is having. With her sister Leslie  getting married and her brother Jordan  already married, her mother  is growing concerned about Gwyn's being the last single person in the family. But the more she thinks about marriage, the more she must search for the balance between career, marriage, and family
