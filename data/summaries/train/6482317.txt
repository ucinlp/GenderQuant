Damnation tells the story of Karrer , a depressed man in love with a married torch singer  from a local bar, the Titanik. The singer broke off their affair, because she dreams of becoming famous. Karrer is offered smuggling work by Willarsky , the bartender at the Titanik. Karrer offers the job to the singer's husband, Sebastyen . This gets him out of the way, but things don't go as Karrer plans. Betrayals follow. Karrer despairs.
