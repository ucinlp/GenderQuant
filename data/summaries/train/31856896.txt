Ann Barton , a girl from a once-wealthy family, must make a living by clerking in a cigar store. There she meets and falls in love with James McDonald , a cigar salesman. She is then adopted by Margaret Wyndham , her rich and aristocratic aunt, who disapproves of James due to his crude manners. Wishing to break up the two, Aunt Margaret sends Ann away to finishing school. In response, Ann acts out publicly and embarrasses her aunt. In the meantime, learns how to be a proper gentleman and wins her back through having learned good manners and a more diginified bearing.
