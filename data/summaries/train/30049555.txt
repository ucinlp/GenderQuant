Kudirithe Cup Coffee is a simple story. The hero, Venu's  friend commits suicide because of unrequited love and the former develops aversion and no sympathy for such folks who give up life for women whom they hardly know or have met, cause pain to family friends by taking such an impetuous decision. When a woman  takes a liking towards him and gives a peck on his cheek, he begins distancing her because he has a set image of love and lovers, and when the distance widens he realizes what it means to stay without her.
