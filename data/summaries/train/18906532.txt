Arumugam  is a good Samaritan who makes a living selling Idli’s on roadside platform shop along with his father , sister  and sidekick . Arumugam is in love with his childhood sweetheart Yamini  His best friend is the rich kid Karthik  whose sister is an arrogant tycoon Malini . She and her uncle  hates Karthik’s deep bonding with Arumugam, who she finds to be below her stature. Malini, through her machinations is able to separate the friends and bring Arumugam to the streets. Our hero after his mother’s  grave is bulldozed and his sister humiliated, vows to bring Malini to the streets. And like you have seen in hundreds of earlier films the hero due to his hard work and smart moves becomes the richest man in the town and takes his revenge.
