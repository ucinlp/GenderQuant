Eileen Shannon  falls in love with Dr Burton . However he is a Protestant and her strongly Catholic father John  refuses to give his consent to marriage between them. Eileen pleads to her brother Darcy , a Catholic priest, for his help, but he does not support mixed marriages either. John is murdered and Dr Burton is arrested for the crime and sentenced to death. The real murderer confesses to Darcy in the confessional. Unable to break the confidence of the confessional, Darcy admits to the murder and Dr Burton is freed. The murderer eventually confesses and Eileen marries Dr Burton.
