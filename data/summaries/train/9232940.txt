The film takes place in a port in Alexandria and focuses on the lives of some sailors. Hamidah  works in a ship and plans to marry her cousin, Ragab , but Ragab is forced to leave for a long period. While he was away, Hamidah falls in love with Mamdouh, a wealthy man. Ragab returns three years later to find her in a relationship with someone else. He fights for her and wins her back again and marries her.
