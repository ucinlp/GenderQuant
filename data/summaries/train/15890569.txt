In New York, millionaire Travers Gladwin  learns of a plot to steal his collection of paintings by some art criminals led by Alfred Wilson . Gladwin pretends to leave to go on holiday, but actually remains in the area disguised as a policeman. Wilson and his gang arrive, with Wilson posing as Gladwin, and a young lady as his fiancee. Gladwin tries to stop the robbery but eventually lets Wilson go in order to save the honour of the young last.
