Rica  has been very protective of her sister Cecil  even giving up her one true love Bong  for her sisters future and after a freak accident 8 years ago at their little party store for fireworks, but as they grow up under the care of their grandmother  their parents come back . As they come back the girls are all grown up and Rica fears that her younger sister, Cecil, will no longer need her to look after her. Cecil later on meets and later falls in love with a con-man Edzel .
