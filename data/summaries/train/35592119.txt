Gopal is a village boy with a melodious voice who comes to the city in search of a job. Mohana a young and vibrant girl helps him to get a break in a good music company. In this process Mohana falls in love with him and forces him to get married to her. On the other hand Gopal returns to his village and gets married to his childhood love Nilambari.
