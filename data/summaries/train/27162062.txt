Grace Goodright , is the wife Warren Goodright , an American playwright living in Paris. Grace is living beyond her means and owes dressmaker Renee Malot  money. Malot suggests that Grace contact American army officer Captain Rex Strong  who might be able to assist her financially. Rex offers Grace a loan, but only if she grants him sexual favors. Grace refuses, and Malot, angered at losing an opportunity for money, attempts unsuccessfully to blackmail Grace.
