The story centers around suburban housewife Beverly Boyer  and her husband, a successful obstetrician and devoted family man, Gerald . Beverly is offered the opportunity to star in a television commercial advertising soap. After a shaky start, she gets a contract for $80,000 per year to appear in weekly TV commercials. Soon the TV studio places greater and greater demands on the unlikely TV star. Gerald becomes jealous of the fact that she is now out-earning him, and their relationship slowly deteriorates until she decides to return as a housewife.
