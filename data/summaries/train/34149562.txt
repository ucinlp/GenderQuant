Captain Robert Kelly  while attached to the American Embassy in Peking at the time of the Boxer Rebellion befriends Lady Patricia Rutledge  and rescues her from the priests of a Chinese temple that she has gone to visit. He then asks a friend to escort her to safety and battles the Chinese in order to give them time to escape. Eventually he brings news or the approach of the allies to the barricade.Motion Picture News, 28 Apr 1928, pg.1348
