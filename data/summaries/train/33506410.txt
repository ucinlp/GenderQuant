Ryan  has been plagued with nightmares since the night his sister Amy  went missing 8 years earlier after playing the game "Bloody Mary" Ryan, now a senior at college suffers from stress and guilt from his sister's disappearance. His girlfriend Rachel  calls for help of Father O'Neal , a former professor of Ryan. O'Neal, a priest and a archaeologist tries to help Ryan by attempting to uncover the circumstances on Amy's death.
