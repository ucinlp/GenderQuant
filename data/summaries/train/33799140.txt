Stephen Louis  is a quadriplegic by birth who is immensely rich, but he believes in celebrating each moment of life. He always loves to see beautiful things and speaks about celebrating life even though he can't move any part of his body other than his neck. He is always assisted by his Manager Kamalu  and his caretaker/driver Karunan  in a palatial house in Fort Kochi. As most of his relatives look to take advantage of his position, Louis has kept all of them at bay, barring his cousin brother Alex  with whom he shares a good relationship. John , a musician at a hotel, enters his life. John is completely opposite from Stephen's nature. He is always worried about the future. Though poles apart in their attitudes, both enjoy a good friendship and try to make each other happy. But with the arrival of a beautiful home nurse named Anjali , the attitudes of both friends towards their lives turn around. The movie takes unexpected twists and turns from here, with a thrilling suspense.
