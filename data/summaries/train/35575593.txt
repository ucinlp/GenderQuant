Siblings Addison  and Liza  are on the run from a casino heist gone wrong. When a car accident leaves their wheel man and a state trooper dead, they split up and make a run for the Canadian border in the worst of circumstances – a near whiteout blizzard. While Addison heads cross-country, creating mayhem in his wake, Liza is picked up by ex-boxer Jay , en-route for a Thanksgiving homecoming with his parents, June  and retired sheriff Chet . It’s there the siblings are reunited in a tense and thrilling showdown that pushes the bonds of family to the limit.
