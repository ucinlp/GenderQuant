Multi-millionaire industrialist Dev Mallya  takes a personal interest an attractive interior designer Saanjh Batra  and wants to marry her. He starts by hiring her to re-do the interior of his palatial home. He makes it a point to be pleasant to her and her mom, Mrs. Kiran Batra. One day Dev finds out that Saanjh has some feelings for his friend, Rohan Potdar , and this knowledge makes him insecure. Shortly after, Rohan is arrested for murder, is tried, and sentenced to life imprisonment. This news breaks Saanjh's heart, and she eventually consents to marry Dev. All is perfect in Dev's world, until ACP Rajvir Scindia  announces the re-opening of the case against Rohan Potdar, as he is convinced that Rohan is the victim of a conspiracy.
