Dharmayya Naidu  a underworld kingpin who lost his wife because he is uneducated. Hence he wants to felicitate education in his seema area by constructing schools. He realizes that the quarry he owns has good quality granite deposits. Sambashiva Naidu  is the son of Dharmayya Naidu. Prakash  is the son-in-law of Dharmayya Naidu. Pasupathy plays the game in such a way that Dharmayya Naidu is forced to give granite quarry as a dowry to Prakash's family, due to which Dharmayya Naidu's daughter commits suicide. When Samba comes to know the reason behind his sister's suicide, he occupies the quarry and kills the brothers of Prakash. Prakash kills everybody in the house of Samba in retaliation. Samba and his nemesis Prakash take shelter in Kanchi and Guwhati respectively as police ban them from entering AP for a year. Samba settles as a saree manufacturer and trader in Kanchi . The rest of the story is all about how he returns to Seema and takes vengeance against Prakash and then starts serving in educational field.
