{{Expand section}} The film opens in San Francisco, where John Triton  is "The Mental Wizard", a nightclub fortune teller. During a show one evening, Triton suddenly urges an audience member to rush home, cautioning that her son is in danger. As the story unfolds, Triton struggles with his newfound psychic ability, as all of his relentlessly bleak predictions prove accurate. Jerome Cowan (of [[The Maltese Falcon  plays Whitney Courtland, Triton's best friend, who becomes wealthy using tips from the now-psychic Triton.
