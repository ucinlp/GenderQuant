The pretty and quirky interior designer Michelle Kelly  has convinced her struggling photographer boyfriend, August Loder  to finally move in with her. On the same morning they're moving August out, the girl moving in mistakenly arrives to move in... she's a hot looking free-spirit named Hunter  and August falls in love with her at first sight.August and Hunter decide to help each other move in and out. Throughout the day, their two very outrageous groups of friends tangle through conflicts and sex while August tries to decide which girl he wants to be with before it's too late.
