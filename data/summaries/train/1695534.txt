Talented pre-teen children, including Andrew "Ender" Wiggin, discovered by humanity's military International Fleet , are trained for battle against the alien insectoid Formics  invading Earth. Ordained by Graff, an IF commander, as their next great hope, Ender is promoted to Command School.
