Pyaasa is a love triangle with revenge as its base. Suraj  aspires to be a millionaire some day, but all hopes of making it big are squashed by his father,  and uncle , who don't approve of selling their ancestorial land. Sheetal , a tycoon, who hires Suraj to work for her business empire. Prem  is Suraj's cousin and an accomplished businessman, who Suraj detests. What Suraj does not know is that Sheetal is just using him as a pawn to extract revenge from Prem and his father, Anang Desai. How Suraj faces up to Sheetal and thwarts her motives forms the rest of the story.
