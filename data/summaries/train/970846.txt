The film follows the fate of four Formula One drivers through a fictionalised version of the 1966 Formula One season: * Jean-Pierre Sarti  – a Frenchman, previously twice world champion, nearing the end of his racing career. * Pete Aron  – an American, who is on the comeback trail. * Scott Stoddard  – an Englishman, recuperating from a bad crash during a race. * Nino Barlini  – an Italian, a promising rookie also a former world motorcycle champion. Sub-plots in the film revolve around the women who try to live with or love the racers with dangerous lifestyles.
