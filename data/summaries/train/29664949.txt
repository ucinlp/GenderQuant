Heeralal Pannalal is a Comedy film, where Mithun and Johnny stars in Parallal roles. It is the story of two sets of twins and both has been named as Heeralal and Pannalal, set out in search of love and adventure, but they are quite unaware of the presence of the other set of Heeralal and Pannalal. The film resembled Amithabh-Govinda starrer Bade Miyan Chote Miyan.
