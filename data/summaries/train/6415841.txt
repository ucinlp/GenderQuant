Police officer Kit  leaves his job after his family is killed by a terrorist named The Doctor. Kit becomes a bodyguard for movie star Frankie  who is reported to perform his own stunts. The report is untrue; Frankie is a drunken womanizer incapable of performing the stunts, and Kit performs them for him. Frankie attends a gala event in a high-rise hotel where The Terrorist shows up and takes everyone hostage. Kit has an opportunity to face his family's killer and save Frankie and the rest of the hostages.{{cite web}}
