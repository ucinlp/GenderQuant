Sakalakala Vallavan has the young agriculturist Kamal Hassan goes to extremes to attain revenge against his devious landlord and his family. Our hero is an upright young man, but when his family gets cheated out of money, he decides it's time to take a stand. Donning various disguises, he—with help from his sister—sets out seeking justice against the landlord and his confrontational daughter Ambika and son Raveendran.
