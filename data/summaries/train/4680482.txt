Three air hostesses, based in New York City, are working for the fictional airline Polar Atlantic Airways. The three serve on a Boeing 707 making regular flights between New York and Paris or Vienna. Along the way, one girl , meets an impoverished Austrian baron  who turns out to be a diamond smuggler. Another girl, a "Southern belle" , develops a crush on the first officer of the airplane , who himself is having an affair with a married woman . The third air hostesses  gets noticed by a multi-millionaire widower from Texas .
