Aham is the story of Sidharthan , who grew up in a disciplined and strict environment and tried to lead his family life in similar lines. But his wife ([[Urvasi  was not able to adjust with his crazy lifestyle. She wanted to become self-dependent but this created suspicion in Siddharthan's mind.
