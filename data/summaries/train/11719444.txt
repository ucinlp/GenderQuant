The film is composed of four episodes, each following a different couple, each focusing on a particular stage in the story of a typical love relationship: *Innamoramento : Giulia and Tommaso  meet and fall in love; *Crisi : Barbara e Marco  are a couple going through a crisis, close to divorce; *Tradimento : A traffic police woman  is cheated on by her husband and takes revenge; *Abbandono : Goffredo  is a rich doctor abandoned by his wife. The characters in the movie are all related in one way of another, and each transition brings background characters from a previous episode to be the protagonists of the next episode. The film is set in Rome.
