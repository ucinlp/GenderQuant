Lt. Amor "Moe" Santander  is a tough chick who's used to going after crooks. But there's also one thing she's running away from ever since her mother died: falling in love. Lt. Kevin Robles  is daunted by most things, but most especially coming clean with the girl he's been loving from afar for a time now. Meanwhile, Caloy  is a pirated DVD vendor whom Moe captures, along with his heart. Things get complicated when Caloy enlists Kevin's help to win Moe, just when Moe and Kevin's friendship gets deeper. Who does Moe really love? Will she even surrender to her feelings in the first place? Suddenly her life as one of the best performers in the service suffers.
