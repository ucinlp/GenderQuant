Tim, a boy with autism, has supposedly witnessed his parents' double murder. Jake, a former child psychiatrist turned therapist, is called on to probe the child's mind in order to solve the case. The psychological drama is provided by the fact that not even Jake can entice Tim to communicate what he has or has not seen regarding the crime. Tim's sister, Sylvie, is protective of him. She eventually warms to Jake's efforts, but discovers that he was implicated in the suicide of a young child who was under his care.
