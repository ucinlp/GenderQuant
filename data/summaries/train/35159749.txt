On a class field trip to a mental hospital, psychology major Abby Jones  has a disturbing meeting with a dangerous patient, Harmon . Later, studies with her two roommates unaware of the dark impact she had in him. As the weather turns stormy, Harmon works his way towards Abby. Abby and her roommate Lynn  receive a visit from Carter Thomas , the neighbor who is a police officer, who warns them about a local voyeur preying on locals. He assures them that he is close by and as long as that they should keep their curtains closed and doors locked. Meanwhile, Harmon works his way towards them slowly and methodically for a showdown between them.
