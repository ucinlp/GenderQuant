Hariyali Aur Rasta is a triangular love story among Shankar , Shobna  and Rita . Marriage of Rita and Sankar has been fixed in their childhood, though when they grow up, Shankar falls in love with Sobhna, though eventually ends up marrying Rita as per their family's desires. But they both cannot reconcile to the ill fitting marriage, though situation deteriorate further when ex-flame Shobhna comes into their lives once again! Story Synopsis
