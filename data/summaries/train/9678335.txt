Vijay comes to the city with an ambition to star in the movies. He is totally mad about cinema. He lives as a tenant in Swathi's house. He tries acting out film scenes and speaking film dialogues in a loud fashion thus disturbing her studies.So both of them get off on the wrong foot. But finally they fall in love with each other. Mansur Ali Khan is her maternal uncle who also wants to marry Swathi. So the villain starts disturb the lovers. But the hero fight for his love and finally wins his lady love's hand.
